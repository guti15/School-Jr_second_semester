 Wutan we gebiddan God ealmihtine  heofena heah cyning  and Sancta Marian  and ealle Godes halgan &t;&ae;t we moton Godes &ae;lmihtiges willan gewyrcan &t;a hwil &t;e we on &t;yssan l&ae;nan life wunian &t;&ae;t hy us gehealdan and gescyldan wi&d; ealra feonda costnunga  gesenlicra and ungesenelicra  Pater noster. 
 Wutan we gebiddan for urne papan on Rome  and for urne cyning  and for urne arcebiscop and for urne ealdorman  and for ealle &t;a &t;e us gehealda&d; fri&d; and freondscype on feower healfe into &t;ysse halgan stowe  and for ealle &t;a &d;e us fore gebidda&d; binnan Angelcynne and butan Angelcynne  Pater noster. 
 Wutan we gebiddan for ure godsybbas and for ure cum&ae;&d;ran  and for ure gildan and gildsweostra  and ealles &t;&ae;s folces gebed &t;e &t;as halgan stowe mid &ae;lmesan sece&d;  mid lihte and mid tige&d;inge  and for ealle &d;a &t;e we &ae;fre heora &ae;lmessan befonde w&ae;ron  &ae;r life and &ae;fter life  Pater noster. 
 Bidde we. 
 For &t;orfer&t;es saule bidde we Pater noster  and for M&ae;lm&ae;re saule  and for ealle &t;a saula &t;e fulluht underfengan and on Crist gelyfdan fram Adames d&ae;ge to &t;isum d&ae;ge  Pater noster.

 De nocturnali caelabratione.. 
 On uhtan we sculon God herian ealswa Dauid cw&ae;&d;  Media nocte surgebam ad confitendum tibi super iudicia iustitie tue  &d;&ae;t is  To middre nihte ic aras  and andette drihtenes doma rihtwisnesse. 
 Crist sylf bead &t;&ae;t we georne wacian sceoldan. 
 He cw&ae;&d;  Vigilate ergo quia nescitis quando ueniet dominus  &d;&ae;t bi&d;  Wacia&d; georne for&d;am &t;e ge nyton hw&ae;nne eower drihten cym&d;. 
 And eft he cw&ae;&d;  Beati serui illi quos cum uenerit dominus  &t;&ae;t is  Eadige beo&d; &t;a men &t;e se hlaford wacigende gemet &t;onne he tocym&d;  &t;&ae;t is  ure drihten &t;onne he to dome cym&d; &t;onne he wile witan hwa wacigende beo on godum d&ae;dum. 
 Us is mycel &d;earf &t;&ae;t we geornlice wacian and w&ae;re beon  for&d;am nele dea&d; na cy&d;an hw&ae;nne he cuman wyle &t;e ma &t;e &t;eof  ac he cym&d; &t;onne man l&ae;st wen&d;. 
 &T;onne bi&d; se swy&d;e ges&ae;lig se &d;e bi&d; &t;onne wacigende. 
 Se bi&d; wacigende se &d;e a smea&d; ymbe Godes willan and ymbe his agene &t;earfe and on &d;am geenda&d;  and se bi&d; sleac and sl&ae;pende se &d;e fullg&ae;&d; eallum his ly&d;rum lustum &d;urh deofles lare and on &d;am geenda&d;. 
 And &d;onne age we mycle &t;earfe &t;&ae;t we geornlice wacian and a w&ae;re beon wi&d; deofles costnunga  and &t;&ae;t we georne to Gode clypian and to him geearnian &t;&ae;t he us gefylste &d;urh his mildheortnysse &t;&ae;t we on urum endetiman swa wacigende beon on godum d&ae;dum &t;&ae;t we sy&d;&d;an a us gerestan magan and motan on ecere reste. 
 Leofan men  nu ic h&ae;bbe be suman d&ae;le ahrepod be &d;am d&ae;ghwamlican tidan&t;enungan &d;e man to nydrihte don sceall. 
 &T;onne is mycel &t;earf &t;&ae;t man understande &t;&ae;t man toeacan &t;am oft and unseldan sceall God herian and to Gode clypian for manegum neodan eallswa se apostol cw&ae;&d;  Sine intermissione orate  &t;&ae;t is Beo&d; a symble eow gebiddende  to Gode georne. 
 And eft se apostol cw&ae;&d;  Siue enim manducatis siue bibitis siue aliud quid facitis  omnia in gloriam dei facite  &t;&ae;t is  Gyf ge etan  o&d;&d;on drincan  o&d;&d;on elleshw&ae;t wyrcean  don &t;&ae;t ge don  do&d; eall Gode &t;anciende and herigende. 
 Beo &t;&ae;t &d;inga &t;&ae;t hit beo &t;&ae;t se man to note wyrcean wylle  bidde he God fultumes  aa him spew&d; &t;e bet  eallswa Dauid cw&ae;&d;  Adiutor meus esto domine  &t;&ae;t is  Min drihten beo min fultum  and ne forl&ae;t &d;u me. 
 And eft he cw&ae;&d;  Adiutorium nostrum in nomine domini qui fecit celum et terram  &t;&ae;t is  Ure fultum is God &t;e gesceop and geworhte heofonas and eor&d;an and ealle gesceafta.

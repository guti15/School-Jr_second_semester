 Godes &ae; us forbyt  and godes witegan eac swa  &t;&ae;t nan man ne sylle nan feoh to gafole  for &d;an &d;e se swicola mann &d;e beswic&d; &t;a unwaran  and se re&d;a reafere &t;e bereafa&d; &t;a cristenan  and se &t;e his feoh syl&d; to gafole ongean godes bebod beo&d; ealle gelice on heora arleasnysse. 
 Gif &d;u &ae;nigum menn gehylpst &t;e behofa&d; &t;ines feos butan &ae;lcum gafole  god &d;e syl&d; &t;&ae;s edlean. 
 Manslyht is eac swy&d;e manfullic d&ae;d  ealra synna m&ae;st  buton &t;&ae;t man wi&d;sace gode: for &d;an &d;e se mann is godes anlicnyss.

 &T;is is se quide &t;e Alfric biquath er he ofer se ferde. 
 &T;at is erst Lodne into seynt Eadmunde be wude and by felde and be fenne so ful &amp; so forth so ic it formist ahte. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Birthe into seynt A&t;eldrithe so ful &amp; so forth so ic it bigeten hauede ey&t;er on wude o&t;er on felde and into Holm to sancte Benedicte Berton al so ful &amp; so forth so ic it ahte. 
 And ic an into Rameseye six marc silures and &t;at schal Godric mine brother lesten. 
 &amp; ic an &T;urwineholm into Lodne and Fuglholm into Berhe. 
 And &t;a schep delen men on to  half into Lodne &amp; half into Beorthe. 
 And ic biquethe to min heregete ane marc goldes and &t;at schal Godric mine brother lesten. 
 And Alfric biscop I biquethe mine teld &amp; min bedreaf &t;at ic best hauede vt on mi fare mid me. 
 &amp; be Alfric biscop &amp; Tofi Prude &amp; &t;runni &t;ese quides mundes hure&t;inge &t;at it no man awende. 
 &amp; gif it wa wille awenden God almithen awende is ansene fram hym on domesday.

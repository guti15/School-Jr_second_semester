 Edward king gret Grimketel biscop and Alfger eerl &amp; Toly and alle mine theines on Suthfolk frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ihu &t;at ic wille &t;at &t;e half nigende hundred sokne &t;e in to &t;inghowe lige in to Seint &ae;dmunde mid sake and mid sokne so ful and so forth so it mine moder on hande istod. 
 &amp; ic nelle thafien that hem oni man abrede ani &t;ere &t;inge &t;at ic her uthe.

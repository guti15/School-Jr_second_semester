 Mortun  Beornege &amp; Byrhstane. 
 In usses Drihtnes noman H&ae;lendes Cristes. 
 Ic Oswald ercebisceop cy&t;u &t;&ae;t seo heor&ae;dden &ae;t Wiogurnacestre ge ealdde ge iunge me &t;afedan &t;&ae;t ic moste gebocian twa hida landes on Mortune on &t;reora monna d&ae;g minum twam getreowum mannum Beorhn&ae;ge &amp; Byrhstane tw&ae;m gebro&t;rum. 
 &amp; se eldra h&ae;bbe &t;a &t;reo &ae;ceras &amp; se iungra &t;one feor&d;an ge innor ge utter swa to &t;am lande gebyrige &t;&ae;t mon nemne&d; o&d;re naman Upp&t;rop. 
 &amp; we ealle halsiga&d; on Godes naman &t;&ae;t hio n&ae;nig mann &t;&ae;s ne bereafige &t;a hwile &t;e hieo lifien. 
 &amp; ofer heora d&ae;g cerre to &t;&ae;s honda &t;e heo unnen. 
 &amp; hit seo &t;onne &t;&ae;m agen &ae;ghw&ae;s to brucenne to freon twegra manna d&ae;g butan &t;&ae;m circsceatte. 
 &amp; ic cy&d;e &t;&ae;t &d;a gebro&t;ra twegen me gesealdon IIII pund licwyr&d;es feos wi&d; fullan unnan.

 &D;es synd &t;a landgemaera to Myceldefer. 
 Arest on Myceldefer  andlang mearc weges on &t;one sand pytte  &t;onan anlang &t;ara ealdena dala  &t;onan oster &t;one beorh &t;&ae;t li&d; betweol &t;an twan langan beorgan. 
 &T;onan to Wor&d;igsaetena mearc  andlang Wordihaema mearc on &t;one hlinc bufan Fri&t;elinga dic  of &t;an hlinc on &t;at su&d;myste gat  andlang dic on &t;at ealden fyrd gat. 
 Of &t;am fyrdgat on &d;a hocedan dic  of &t;aere hocedan dic on Dennerdes treow  of &t;am treow on weg gelaetan  nor&d;rihtis to Waddanyge swa innan &t;one broc to Miceldefer.

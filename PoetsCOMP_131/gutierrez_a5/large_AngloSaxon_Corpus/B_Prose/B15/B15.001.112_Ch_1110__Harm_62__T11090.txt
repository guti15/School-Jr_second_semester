 Eadward cing gret wel Wulfwi biscop &amp; Tosti eorl &amp; Nor&d;man scirrefe &amp; ealle his witen &amp; ealle his holden inne Hamtunescire hadede &amp; leawede freondlice. 
 &amp; ic cy&d;e eow &t;&ae;t &AE;lfwine abbod of Ramesege &amp; Leofric abbod of Burh habben me gecyd of &t;&ae;t hw&ae;rf &amp; of &t;a forewarde &t;e heo habben gespeken &amp; gedon heom betweonan. 
 &amp; ic wylle &t;&ae;t ge understanden &t;&ae;t &AE;lfwine abbod of Ramesege on &t;as wise haf&d; gehw&ae;rfd &ae;t Leofrice abbod of Burh nigen gerde landes &ae;t Ludintune of Sanctus Petrus socnelande of Burh. 
 Huntinges hide be name &amp; Godrices twa gerde &t;e Densce  &amp; Brandes gerde  &amp; Leofgares gerde &amp; &AE;lfwines gerde &t;e Blace scer &amp; sacleas to fulle hw&ae;rfe wi&d; &ae;fric man &ae;r d&ae;ige &amp; &ae;fter d&ae;ige. 
 &amp; haf&d; gifen him &t;&ae;r fore &t;&ae;t land &ae;t M&ae;rham al &d;&ae;t Sanctus Bendictus &t;&ae;r ahte  scer  &amp; sacleas wi&d; &ae;fric man to fulle hw&ae;rfe. 
 &amp; to&ae;ken &t;is &t;e abbod &amp; &t;a gebro&d;ra of Ramesege sculen gifan &ae;lce gere feower &t;usend &ae;l inne lenten to carite&d; &t;on abbode &amp; &t;am gebro&d;re in to Burh. 
 To &t;&ae;re forwarde &t;&ae;t &t;e abbod &amp; &t;a gebro&d;ra of Ramesege sculen habben of Sanctus Petrus landare wercstan &ae;t Bernace  &amp; walstan &ae;t Burh  alswa mycel swa heom behofe&d; to fulle forewarde scer &amp; sacleas wid toll &amp; wid ealle &t;ing be w&ae;tere &amp; be lande in to Ramesege &ae;fre mare. 
 &amp; heo habben me gecyd &t;&ae;t &t;is forewarde w&ae;s imaked on Leofsies abbodes iwitnesse of Eli  &amp; Wulfgeates abbodes of Crulande  &amp; on &t;&ae;re manne &t;e heom mide w&ae;ren. 
 Nu cy&d;e ic eow &t;&ae;t &AE;lfwine abbod haf&d; swa wi&d; me gespeken &amp; of his me igefen &t;&ae;t ic habbe &t;is ilce forewarde igeated. 
 &amp; ic wille &t;&ae;t hit stande al swa heo hit gespeken habbe&d; Gode to lofe  &amp; Sancta Maria  &amp; Sancte Benedihte &ae;fre mare wi&d; borene &amp; wi&d; unborene. 
 &amp; ic hate &amp; beode &t;&ae;t nan man ne wur&d;e swa deorf ne swa dyrsti &t;&ae;t &t;is ilce hw&ae;rf &amp; &t;is ilce forwarde breke  haded ne leawed. 
 &amp; ic forbeode be fulle wite &t;&ae;t nan man ne wur&d;e swa dyrsti  &t;&ae;t Sanctus Benedihtus men ne heore &t;ing nahw&ae;r ne derie  ac Godes gri&d; &amp; min habben heo &amp; heore &t;ing be w&ae;tere &amp; be lande. 
 &amp; ic h&ae;te &amp; beode mid &t;is ilce writ  &t;&ae;t &t;&ae;t ilce m&ae;rke &amp; m&ae;re &ae;fter Cnutes delfe kinges stande alswa &AE;lfwine abbod of Ramesege hit betalde wi&d; Siwar&d; abbod of &T;ornege. 
 &AE;l beo &t;e Gangestyde be east halfe &t;am d&ae;lue  &amp; be west halfe be Hyndelake swa anan to Wendelesmere &amp; healf Ragereholt in to Ramesege. 
 On Leofsies abbodes iwitnesse of Eli  &amp; on Leofrices abbod of Burh  &amp; Wulfgeates abbod of Crulande  &amp; &t;are manne &t;a heom mide w&ae;ren. 
 &amp; gif &ae;niman &t;is ilce forewarde mid &ae;nige &t;inge abreke  &amp; awansige  si he gesyndred fram heofenerices myrh&d;e butan he hit ibete &ae;r he heonan gewite.

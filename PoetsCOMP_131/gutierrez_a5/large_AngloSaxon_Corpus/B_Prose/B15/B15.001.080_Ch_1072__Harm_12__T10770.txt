 Eadward king gret mine bisceopes &amp; mine earles &amp; alle mine &t;eignes on &t;am schiran &t;er &t;e lond inne ligge&d; freondlike. 
 &amp; ic kithe eow &t;at ic wille &t;at Leofstan abbot &amp; &t;a brothere &t;at he ben here sake &amp; here socne wrthe ofer &t;a land &t;e man in to &t;er halgen minstre beque&t;eth so ful &amp; so forth so he hauede &t;e hig her ahte. 
 &amp; ic nille &t;afien &t;at men hem ani unricht bede.

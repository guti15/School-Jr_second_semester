 &amp; ic nelle &t;&ae;t &ae;ni man &ae;ni &t;ing &t;&ae;r on teo butan hy &amp; heora wicneras &t;e hi hit bet&ae;can wylla&t; for &t;an &t;ingan &t;e ic habbe &t;as gerihta forgiuen minre sawle to ecere alysednesse swa Cnut cyng &ae;r dyde.

 Of cleuan hithe on yone mide mestan thorn bi thyythe to landscharleighe on Wormester ist andlang ifre on Wormesleighe welle &t;anen on ya vor saide ake on humberwe stede. 
 Of gere ak on &t;a tyo sirsas &t;anen on thar eor&d;briste and &t;anen on ruwanleighe on &t;an ealde heierewe &t;anen on &t;an schiren mor midward. 
 And soa west after streme bitwixe bradan mede and driganhurste soa forth bi suthene herthine on tettanstaples &t;anen on clewan hithe on &t;an midde mestan thorn.

 Hyer swutele&d; on &t;esen ywrithe hu Wolgi&t; yan hire &t;ing efter hire forthsi&t;e &t;e hire se almiyti God yu&t;e on lyue to brukene. 
 &T;et is &t;anne herest mine louerde his riyte heriet. 
 And ic yan &t;et land at Stistede a Godes ywithnesse and mine vrenden into Xristes cheriche &t;a muneken to uostre on &t;an hyrede &t;et Elfkitel and Kytel mine bearn bruke &t;as londes hye dey and se&t;&t;en gange &t;et land into Xristes cheriche buten ecchere agentale vor mine saule and vor Elfwines mines louerdes and for alre mine berne and by hialve &t;e men vrye efter here daye. 
 Ad ic yan into &t;are cheriche at Stistede to &t;an &t;e ic on lyue yu&t;e Eldemes land and &t;ertohycken &t;et &t;er sy alles vifty ekeres on wude and on velde efter mine forthsi&t;e. 
 And ic yan Wulkitele and Kytele mine sunes &t;et land at Walsingham and at Karltune and Herlingham and ic yan minen twam doytren Gode and Bote Sexlingham and Sumerledetune and into &t;are cheriche at Sumerledetune sixtene eker londes and enne eker med. 
 And ic yan Ealgi&t;e mine doyter &t;et land at Cheartekere and at Essetesford and &t;ane wde &t;e ic leyde &t;erto. 
 And ic Godwine eorle and Harold erle Fri&t;etune. 
 And ic yan into Xristes cheriche to Xristes weuede ane litlene geldene rode and ane setrayel. 
 And ic yan seinte Eadmunde tueyn yboned hornes. 
 And ic yan Seynte E&t;eldrithe ane wellene kertel. 
 And ic yan seynte Osithe half pund fees and &t;e yeue mine barnes. 
 And ic yan seynt Austine an regrayel. 
 And se &t;e mine quyde beryaui &t;e ic nu bique&t;en habbe a Godes ywithnesse  beriaued he wor&t;e &t;ises erthliche mery&t;es and ashireyi hine se almiyti Driyten &t;e alle shep&t;e shop and ywroyte uram alre haleyene ymennesse on domesday and sy he bytayt Satane &t;ane deule and alle his awaryede yueren into helle Grunde and &t;er aquelmi and Godes withsaken bute ysweke and mine irfinume neuer ne aswenche. 
 &T;isses is to ywithnesse Eadward king and manie o&t;re.

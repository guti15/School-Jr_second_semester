 Eadward kyng gret Aylmer bisscop &amp; Alfger earl and alle mine &t;eynes into Norfolke frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ihu &t;at ic am to withnesse &t;at Alfric Modercop hauede unnen after his day &t;at land at Lodne into &t;at halegan minstre at Eadmundes biri and alle &t;e &t;inge &t;at &t;er to bire&d; mid sake and mid sokene so ful and so forth so it him seluen on hande stod.

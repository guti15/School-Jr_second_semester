 Her kythet on &t;is write ihu ic Thurketel an min ahte after min day. 
 &T;at is erst &t;at ic an &t;at lond at Castre &amp; at Thorpe mid medwe and mid merisce and ingong and vtgong mine soule to alisednesse God and sancte Benedicte and seynt Eadmunde to Biry and to Holm. 
 And mine wyues del euere unbesaken to gyfen and to habben &t;er hire leuest be. 
 And mine lafard rithte heregete. 
 And Alfwen mine douter habbe &t;at lond at Ormisby to &t;an forwarde &t;at he it ne may forwirken and after hire day go &t;at lond into Holme for mine soule and for hire buten &t;at lond &t;at Omund ahte  &t;at habbe Ketel mine nefe. 
 And &t;at lond at Scrouteby habben mine nefe kild Swegner sunen &amp; Alemundes. 
 And an pund habbe se Abbot on Holm an other se Abbot on Byri. 
 And mine men fre &t;o it ihernen wellen. 
 And wo so &t;is quides bereuen wille  bereue hym God heueriche buten he it her bete. 
 &T;ise write sinden &t;re  and is on Holme and o&t;er on Byri  &t;ridde mid Thurkitel seluen.

 &T;is is &t;e landmere at batecombe. 
 At austien on &t;an put. 
 Of &t;an putte on &t;an olden wei of &t;an weie. 
 On &t;e harenapuldre &t;anen endelang weie rigthwei &t;at it comit on combisberghewei endlang &t;is weies to &t;is kingesmere. 
 &T;ane endlang &t;is weies &t;at it comit on &t;an Wodestou pin &t;anne a doun on mere wei &t;at it comigt on &t;ere hevedstock &t;anen on &t;a herenwirnen. 
 On &t;ene pulle. 
 &T;anen endlang stremes &t;at it comigt on alum. 
 &T;anne get it over alum on merebrok &t;anen endlang stremes op atte &t;a water sweliend &t;anen up on &t;a hille of &t;are hulle on folanbrok. 
 Of &t;an broke on &t;a ealde dich endlang dich &t;at it comigt to &t;an combe over &t;an combe on wliate on wolfgate on alum awwilman of alum awwilman on bradeston. 
 Of &t;an stane out atte &t;ere folanmede of &t;ere mede over &t;ane feld to &t;a wode &t;anen endlang rigtes to fileleighe on dikemor of dikemour eft on &t;ane put.

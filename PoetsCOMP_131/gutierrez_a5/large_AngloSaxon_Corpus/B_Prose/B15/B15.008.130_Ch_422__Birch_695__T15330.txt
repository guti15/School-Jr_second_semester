 &AE;rest on easthealfe  of eastan wylles heafde  andlang lace to mylenburnan  thonne andlang streames oth gifle. 
 Thonne eft andlang streames oth &ae;nnan pol thanon andlang lace oththa ealdan dic  thonne andlang dic oth hloscumbes heafud. 
 Thanon on ecge  andlang ecge oththa dic thanon andlang dic  oth ecgulfes treow  thanon east to &ae;ttandene middeweardre andlang dene eft to &ae;ttan wylle.

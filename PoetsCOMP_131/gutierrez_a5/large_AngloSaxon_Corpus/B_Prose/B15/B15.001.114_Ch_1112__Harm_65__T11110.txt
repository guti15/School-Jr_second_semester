 Edward king gret Harold erl &amp; Aylnod abbot &amp; Godwine &amp; ealle mine &t;eynes on Sumerseten frendliche. 
 Ich que&t;e eou &t;&ae;t ich wille &t;&ae;t Gyse bissop beo &t;isses bissopriches wrthe heer inne mid eou and alch &t;are &t;inge &t;as &t;e &t;ar mid richte to gebyra&d; binnan porte &amp; butan mid saca &amp; mid socna swo uol &amp; swo vor&d; swo hit eni bissop him touoren formest hauede on ealle &t;ing. 
 And ich bidde eou alle &t;at ge him beon on fultome Cristendom to spekene loc whar hit &t;arf sy &amp; eower fultumes be&t;urfe eal swo ich getrowwen to eow habben &t;at ge him on fultume beon willen. 
 And gif what sy mid unlage out of &t;an bissopriche geydon sy hit on londe o&t;er an o&d;&d;er &t;inge &t;at fulstan him vor minan luuen &t;&ae;t hit in ongeyn cume swo swo ge for Gode witen &t;at hit richt sy. 
 God eu ealle gehealde.

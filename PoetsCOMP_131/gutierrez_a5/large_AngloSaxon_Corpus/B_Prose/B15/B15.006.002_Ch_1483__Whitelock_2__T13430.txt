 &T;is is Alfgares quide. 
 &T;at is erst &t;at ic an mine louerd tueye suerde fetelsade and tueye bege  ayther of fifti mancusas goldes  and &t;re stedes and &t;re scheldes and &t;re speren. 
 And me kidde &t;eodred bisccop and Edric Alderman &t;a ic selde mine louerd &t;at suerd &t;at Eadmund king me selde on hundtuelftian mancusas goldes and four pund silueres on &t;am fetelse &t;at ic moste ben mine quides wirde. 
 And ic nefre forwrouht ne habbe on Godes witnesse wi&d; mine louerd buten ic so mote. 
 And ic an Athelflede mine douhter &t;e lond at Cokefel&d; and at Dittone and &t;at at Lauenham ouer min day on &t;e red &t;at heo be &t;e bet for mine soule and hire moder soule &amp; for hire brother soule &amp; for hire seluen. 
 And &t;anne ouer vre aldreday ic an &t;at lond at Cokefeld into Beodricheswrthe to seynt Eadmundes stowe. 
 And ic wille &t;at Athelfled vnne ouer hire day &t;o londes at Dittone into suilke halegen stowe suilk hire redlikest &t;inge for vre aldre soule. 
 And ouer vre aldreday ic an &t;at lond at Lauenham mine douhter childe gif &t;at God wille &t;at heo ani haue&d; buten Atelfled her wille him his vnnen. 
 And gif heo non ne habbe gange it into Stoke for vre aldre soule. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Babing&t;irne Atelflede mine douhter and after hire day min other douhter hire day and ouer here bothre day mine douhter berne gif he bern habbe. 
 And gif he bern ne habbe &t;anne go it into sancte Marie Stowe at Berkynge for vre aldre soule. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Illeye mine ginger douhter hire day and ouer hire day Berthno&d;e his day gif he leng libbe &t;anne heo. 
 Gif he bern habben &t;anne an ic hem. 
 Gif he non ne habbe&t; &t;anne an ic it Athelfle&d; mine douhter ouer here day and after hire day into Cristes kirke at Caunterbiri &t;en hirde de brite. 
 And &t;e lond at Colne and at Tigan ic an min gingere douhter and ouer day gif heo bern habbe hire bern. 
 And gif heo bern ne habbe beque&d;e it Bernothe his day and ouer his day into Stoke for vre aldre soule. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Piltendone and at Mereseye into Stoke. 
 And ic an &t;at Athelfled bruke &t;e lond &t;er wile &t;e hire lef beth one ra&d;a heo it on riht helde and on &t;e red &t;at heo do &t;an hirde so wel so heo best may into Stoke for mine soule and for ure aldre. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Grenstede into Stoke for mine soule &amp; for Athelwardes &amp; for Wiswi&d;e. 
 And ic Athelfled &t;ere brice wille hire lif beth on &t;e red &t;at heo do for &t;a saule so wel so heo best may. 
 Nu his me God u&t;e &amp; min lauerd. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Tidwoldingtone Alfwold ouer mine day &t;e he formige ilke ihere &t;en hird at Paulesbiri for vre aldre soule. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Totham Berchno&d;e and mine gingere douhter here day. 
 And after here day wende lond into Mereseye Athelfled mine douhter. 
 And ic an &t;at wudelond at Aisfeld into Stoke also Eakild self it her bouhte. 
 And ic mine moder &t;at lond at Ryssebroc gif heo leng liui&d; &t;an hic. 
 &T;anne after vnker bother day ic an it Winelme gif heo Athelfled on richte hird. 
 And ic wille bidden suilc louerd so &t;anne beth  for Godes luuen and for alle hise halegen  werken min bern &t;at he werken &t;at he nefre ne mugen forwerken mine quide &t;e ic for mine soule cueden habbe. 
 And gif it wo awende habbe him wi&d; God gemaene and wi&d; &t;e holi sanctas &t;e ic it to becue&t;en habbe &t;at he it nefre ne bete buten on helle wite se &t;is quide awende boten it me seluen wende er min ende. 
 And ic Athelgar an an hide lond &t;es &t;e Aeulf hauede be hundtuelti acren  ateo so he wille.

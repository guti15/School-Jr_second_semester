 Hec sunt termini terre. 
 Arest on &t;ane reanden weg &d;at on &d;one forde to teofunte &t;anen. 
 On brochenenberge &d;at on hoddes clif. 
 &T;anen on abbenbeorg &d;at on stortencumb on &d;es linkes havede suthward. 
 And northward &t;anen on leon berg &t;at on &d;a ealden dich &t;anne. 
 Onne &t;at furch &d;e is a&d;e riuen to nodre &t;annen on funtnesford &t;annen on herepa&t; on &t;e reden wege on &t;a two meades o&t;er be nodre o&t;er be wilig &d;e &d;arto hera&d;.

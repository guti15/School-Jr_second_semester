 In nomine Domini. Ego Berchtwulf cyning sile For&d;rede minum &d;egne nigen higida lond in Wudotune in ece erfe him to hiobbanne  &amp; to siollanne &d;aem &d;e hit wille mi&d; ea&d;modre hernisse him to geeornigan ofer his daeg  Cisse&d;ebeorg  Feowertreowehyl  &amp; Eanburgemere  Tihhanhyl  &amp; ut bi Geht tu higida lond in erfe ece. 
 &amp; he salde to londceape XXX mancessan &amp; nigen hund scilling wi&d; &d;aem londe him in ece erfe. 
 Ic Berhtwulf rex &d;as mine gesaldnisse trymme &amp; faestna in Cristes rodetacne &amp; in his &d;aere haligran a &amp; in his wotona gewitnisse. 
 &amp; we aec alle bibeoda&d; &d;e aet &d;isse gewitnisse werun  on Cristes noman &amp; on his &d;aere haligran  gif aenig monn &d;as ure gewitnisse incerre on owihte  &d;aet he aebbe &d;aes aelmaehtgan Godes unhlisse &amp; his &d;aere haligran up in heofnum &d;aes we him gebeodan maege.

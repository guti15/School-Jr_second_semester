 Her switele&t; on &t;ise write wam Wlsi an his aihte. 
 &T;at is erst for his soule &t;at lond at Wiken into seynt Eadmundes biri &t;a tweye deles &amp; Alfric Biscop &t;e &t;ridde del buten ane girde and XII swine mesten &t;at schal habben Wlwine hire day and after hire day into seynt Eadmundes biri. 
 &amp; alle &t;o men fre for vnker bother soule. 
 And ic an mine kynelouerd II hors and helm and brinie &amp; an swerd and a goldwreken spere. 
 And ic an mine lauedy half marc goldes an mine nifte ann ore wichte goldes. 
 And habbe Stanhand alle &t;inge &t;e ic him bicue&d;en habbe and mine brother bern here owen lond &amp; II hors mid sadelgarun and I brinie and on hakele. 
 And se &t;e mine cui&d;e awende  God almithtin awende his asyne from him on domesday buten he it her &t;e rathere bete.

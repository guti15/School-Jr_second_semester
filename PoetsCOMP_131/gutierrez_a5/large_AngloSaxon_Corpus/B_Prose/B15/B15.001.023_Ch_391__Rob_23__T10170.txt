 In Godes name ich A&t;elstan God gyuing kyng welding eal Brytone mid alle mine wytene &amp; alle biscope of &t;an kinedome of Engelonde gelad by &t;e pricingge of &d;e Haly Goste grantye and confirmye by &d;isse minre chartre for me and for &t;e kingges of Engelonde &d;&ae;t come&t; &ae;fter me ene and euere ich tille Gode and sainta Marian and sainte Michaele  sainte Sampsone and sainte Branwaladre XXVI hyde londes &ae;t Muleburne mid &d;an &t;&ae;t &d;ereto li&t;. 
 And fif &ae;t Wonlonde and &t;reo atte Fromemou&t;e atte yle &d;an ye  to on see and on on londe  &t;&ae;t is to seggende &ae;t Ore. 
 And &t;reo at Clyve mid &d;are mede &t;&ae;t &t;ereto li&t; and &t;reo and on half at Liscombe and on at Burdalueston and on at Litele Pudele and fiue at Cattesstoke and VI at Comptone and to at Widecome and V at Osmyntone &amp; VI at Holewert&t;e  &t;&ae;t is alles seuene and sixty hyden into Middeltone. 
 And anne were on Auene at Twynham and al &t;&ae;t water binne sta&t;e of Waimou&t;e and half strym on &d;an Waymou&t;e out on see and twelf acres to &t;an were &amp; &d;an werhurde. 
 And &t;reo &d;egne on Su&t;sexan and salterne by were and XXX hyden on Sidemyntone to fosterland and to at Chelmyntone and six at Hylfelde &amp; X hyde at Ercecombe to tymberlonde. 
 And ich wolle &t;&ae;t al &d;is myn almeslonde mid al &d;an &t;&ae;t &d;ereto li&t; and freo beo in alle &d;inge and freo custumes. 
 &T;&ae;t is for mine saule helpe and for &d;e helpe of here saulen &t;&ae;t tofore me were and after me comen schulle kynges of Engelonde &d;an minster to fore gesed of Middeltone in rigte clene almesse wulle &amp; grantye &t;&ae;t hit beon al so freo in alle &d;inge mid &d;an &t;&ae;t &d;erto li&t; in eche stede in Englonde in myne cynedome alswa myn ogen ore. 
 And ich stedeuastliche hote and bebeode in Gode almigties hege name  Fader and Son and Holy Gost  &t;&ae;t &d;is min wille and gifte and of &d;is writ fastnynge ungewemmed beo and ungewered and ungewendelich &d;e hwile &t;&ae;t Christendom dure&t; in &d;is gelonde Englisckan. 
 Oure Lourd God almigtig and alle his halgen  al &d;e ylc ho so hit beo &t;&ae;t &d;is my dede in o&d;ere wise hit byturne o&t;er gewanye o&t;er ho &t;&ae;t euere beo be hey Judan feyre Christes traytour on helle wytte pynende &amp; on echenysse. 
 And &t;&ae;t &d;is sond beo and stedeuast euere boute ende ich &d;e foresedene kyng Adelstan &d;is gewritene bocleof habbe gemerked mid Cristes holy rodetokne and min ogen honde mid &d;isse gewitnesse of alle mine gewytene &t;&ae;t herafter gewriten beo gefunden &amp; mid mine biscopes.

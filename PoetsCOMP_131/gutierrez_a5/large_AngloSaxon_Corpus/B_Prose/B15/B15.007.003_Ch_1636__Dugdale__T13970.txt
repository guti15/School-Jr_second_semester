 For mine saule alisendnesse gean cristes chirican inne Ghantwareberi landas on mine gerihte  &t;&ae;t is to wittenne Sandwich &amp; &AE;stre. 
 To &d;ere moncken bigleue in &d;e silue ministre gode seruiende  from alle worldliche &d;eudome &amp; kinges geuewi&t;ute briggewurke &amp; castelwurke. 
 And gif &ae;nig is &d;&ae;t gewilnat o&t;&t;e to brekenne  o&t;&t;e to geslitende mid to&t;undene modignesse &d;as ure gefestnunge  wite he hine silfe amansumod fram gode  &amp; fram eallen his halgen mid &d;a foreworpenen inne domes deie a christes swinste hand geloged underfang &d;one dome of fordemdnesse mid &d;am deofle and his &ae;nglea. 
 Btan he toforan &d;am dea&t;e mid wur&t;e bereusinge gebete &t;&ae;t he unrihtwisliche forgilde.

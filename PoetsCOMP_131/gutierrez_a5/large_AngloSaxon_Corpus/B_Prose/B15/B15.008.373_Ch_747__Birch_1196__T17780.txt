 &T;is bet &t;e landmere. 
 Erest on stonhan and on eston Merkepol on hidebourne and &t;anen west on slade edichs southward &t;anen on benanberwe &t;anen on trdmere endlang ridde north biweste hoppinge ouer &t;ane Mersh on &t;e rigt on ruanmere. 
 And &t;anne est bi wimbedounyngemerke on &t;er hop bi north bradenforde on hidebourne and &t;anne south endelang bournin bi michamingemerke &t;ar eft on merke pol.

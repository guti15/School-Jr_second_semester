 Eadward kyng gret Stigand bisscop &amp; Harald erl and alle mine &t;eynes on Estangle frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ihu &t;at ic wille &t;at alle &t;inge &t;e mid rithte bire&d; into Seynt Eadmundes are mine meyes &amp; gode men &t;ider inne uthen lige &t;ider in unbesaken. 
 And ic nelle &t;afien &t;at ani man uttige ani &t;ing &t;at hem mid rithte to bire&d; neither ne on londe ne o&t;ere &t;ingan.

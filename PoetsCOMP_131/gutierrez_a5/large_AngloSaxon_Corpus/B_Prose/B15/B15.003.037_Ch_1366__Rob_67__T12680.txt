 On T&ae;tlinctune &amp; &ae;t Neoweboldan  Eadrice. 
 In usses drihtnes noman H&ae;lendes Cristes  ic Oswald arcebiscop mid ge&t;afunge &amp; leafe &t;&ae;s arwyr&d;an hiredes on Wiogernaceastre ge iunges ge aldes gebocie sumne d&ae;l landes minum holdan &amp; getriowan &t;egne &t;&ae;m is Eadric noma on twam stowum &t;riora hida landes on twera monna d&ae;g twa on T&ae;tlintune &amp; ane &ae;t Nioweboldan &amp; &t;&ae;t inlond &t;&ae;rto &t;e Leofinc h&ae;dde for his eadmodre hersumnesse. 
 &T;&ae;t is &t;&ae;t he h&ae;bbe &amp; wel bruce his d&ae;g &amp; &ae;fter his d&ae;g anum erfewarde &t;&ae;m &t;e him leofest se &amp; him to gearnian wille. 
 &amp; he hit h&ae;bbe to frion &ae;lces &t;ingces butan geweorce &amp; brycggeworce &amp; ferdsocne. 
 &T;is w&ae;s gedon &t;u geare &t;e agan w&ae;s fron Cristes gebyrdtide nigon hund wintra &amp; an &amp; hundnigonti wintra on &t;ara bro&d;ra gewitnesse &t;e hiora naman her beneo&d;an awritene standa&d;. 
 Ic Oswald ercebiscop mid Cristes rode tacne &t;as sylene gefestnade.

 Arest of horspoles heauede anlang dich on ludenham. 
 Of ludenhame alang streames on eldenham. 
 &T;annen for&d; to &t;ere ealden hege &t;annen forder be hegen one stoc wey. 
 Of stok wei anlang hecgham to filed hamme. 
 Of filed hamme ut &t;urth &t;ere groue on irichte to &t;ere eald dich. 
 Of &t;ere eald idich. 
 On irichte to here pa&d;. 
 Of here pa&t;e anlang richtes to lortenwille on &d;a elden dich of &t;ere ealden dic  on duccenhulle. 
 Of duccenhulle  on cawel andlang dich. 
 On wincawel  &t;annen anlang streames. 
 On hors poles heaued.

 Her swutela&d; on &d;issum gewrite &t;&ae;t Leofric eorl &amp; his gebedda habba&d; geunnen twa land for Godes lufan &amp; for hyra sawle into &t;&ae;re halgan stowe sancta Marian mynstre &ae;t Wihgraceastre &t;am gebro&d;ran to bryce inn to heora beodderne &t;e &t;&ae;r d&ae;ighwamlice Gode &t;eowia&d; to &t;am foreweardan &t;&ae;t &t;a gebro&d;ra beon &t;ingiende for uncrum saulum. 
 &T;&ae;t synd V hida landes &ae;t Wulfweardiglea &amp; twa &ae;t Blaca wyllan &amp; &ae;nne hagan on porte  &t;&ae;t hit sig &ae;fre into heora beodderne freols &amp; hit nan man ne geutige. 
 &amp; gyf &ae;nig sy &t;e hit mid &ae;nigan &t;ingan geecean wylle o&d;&d;e godian God &ae;lmihtig him geunne gesundfull lif &amp; ece mede. 
 &amp; gyf &ae;nig &t;onne sy up ahafen &amp; swa swi&d;e gredig &t;issere worulde &t;&ae;t &t;as ure sylena geutian wylle h&ae;bbe he her on &d;isse life Godes curs &amp; sancta Marian &amp; sancte Oswaldes &amp; ealra gehadedra manna &amp; sy he amansumod on domes d&ae;ige toforan Drihtnes cneowan fram Gode &amp; fram eallan his halgan &amp; mid Iudan &amp; his geferan &ae;fre on ece wite cwylmed butan he geswice &amp; to rihte gecyrre. 
 &amp; wit synd &t;isra landa hald &amp; mund into &t;am halgan mynstre &t;a hwile &t;e uncker lif bi&d;.

 Eadward kyng gret Aylmer bisscop &amp; Aelfger erl &amp; alle mine &t;eynes on Norfolke frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ihu &t;at ic wille &t;at &t;at lond at Kirkeby &amp; alle &t;inge &t;at &t;er to mid rithte bire&d; lige into &t;an halege minstre at Seynt Eadmund mid sake &amp; mid socne so fol and so forth so it ani man firmest hauede. 
 &amp; mine moder it &t;ider inne byquath. 
 And ic bidde ihu alle &t;at ge deme me swilc dom of Semere &t;at haue&d; &t;ider inne faren mid unlage swilk ge for Gode witen &t;at me mid rithte to bire&d;.

 Limites of driganhurste andlang pillis &t;anen on &t;e olde Gore an soan Roanleighe on &t;e olde hege rewe so up endlang humbers bitwixe douningleighe. 
 &T;anen on crichhulle and soa bi linen bitwixe abbingleie and soa on Doultingstrem up endlanges at Winterwelle of &t;an Welle on lincombesleighe on &t;e rizte on the stanleie Wal. 
 &T;anen on croppanhulle and soa endlang dich on trindleaie mideward. 
 &T;anen on rizt on midden merkesburi. 
 &T;anen endlang weies on Ramer &t;anen estrizt endlang pathes on &t;an olden fosse andlang fosse south on pulle a doune bi pulle on lutletone south op on pennard and soa bi Wyrt taman West to Weie endelang weies north est on pil.

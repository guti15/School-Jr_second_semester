 Willelm kyng gret mine biscopes &amp; mine eorles &amp; mine scyrgerefan &amp; ealle mine thegnas on Hamtescyram thar Walkelin biscop hafeth land inne &amp; men frendlice. 
 And ic kythe eow thaet ic an &amp; wille thaet he beo his biscopesrices werthe &amp; aelc thara thinga the thider in gebyrige on lande &amp; on strande &amp; on woude &amp; on felde on laese &amp; sace &amp; socne &amp; toll &amp; team &amp; infangennre theof binnan porte &amp; butan nu swa full &amp; swa forth swa aenig his foregenga fyrmest heold &amp; weold in eallen thingan. 
 And ic nelle gethafian thaet aenig man beo swa dorf thaet him fram hande drage heononforth aenig thera thinga thaes the he rihtlice age &amp; in to his bisceoprice gebyrge be minan fullan frieondscype &amp; gif aenig man haefth aenige aehta ut of his landan gedon ic beode thaet hi bynnan thrym nythan ongean cuman &amp; nan other ne sy be eallen than aehtan them age.

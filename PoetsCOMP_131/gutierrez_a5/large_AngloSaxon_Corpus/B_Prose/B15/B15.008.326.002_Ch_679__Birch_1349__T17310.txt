 &D;is sint &d;a landgemara to scroppen &t;orpe &amp; &t;uresby. 
 Of langanforda dic andlang dices &tbar; on &t;a miclan dic andlang dices on blidan et celfa seyeade andlang blidan on beo lese su&d;ewearde &tbar; up &d;a miclan strat andlang strat ut &t;urh beolease nor&d; ut &t;one feld &tbar; adun on blidan up be blidan on iddil. 
 Up be iddel on &t;one fulan broc andlang broces eft on langan ford. 
 &D;is sint &d;a landgemara to suttune of fulan forda andlang stret to &t;am grafe &t;on geriht mid &t;one graf on &t;one broc andlang broces on langan ford on gerihte of scir wuda midde weardne on iddel at brodan fleote. 
 &tbar; up a be iddel. 
 Eft on fulan ford. 
 &D;onne is sisotef land binnan &t;issum gemara.

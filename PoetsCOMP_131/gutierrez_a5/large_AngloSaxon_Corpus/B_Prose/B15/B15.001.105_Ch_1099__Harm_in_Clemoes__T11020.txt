 Edward ge kynge gret myne Busshopes &amp; myne Erles &amp; alle myne theygnes in ye shire ger ger Leouwyn Abbot in Couentr' habbet lond inne freondliche. 
 &amp; ich chuthe ou yat ich wille yat he boe his sace &amp; ys socene wurth tolles &amp; temes ouer hys lond &amp; ouer hys meyn bynnen borough &amp; bouten so foul &amp; so forth so so Leofric Erl firmest hauede. 
 &amp; ich nulle suffren yat mon hym in any thyng mysbede.

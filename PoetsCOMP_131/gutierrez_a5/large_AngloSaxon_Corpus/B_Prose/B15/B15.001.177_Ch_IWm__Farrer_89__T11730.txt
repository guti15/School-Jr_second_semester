 Willelm cyngc gret ealle mine &t;egenas on Eoferwicscire Frencisce and Englisce freondlice. 
 And ic cy&d;e eow &t;&ae;t ic h&ae;bbe gegyfen Sancte Johanne &ae;t Beuerlic sac and socna ofer eallum &t;am landum &t;e w&ae;ron gyfene on &AE;dw&ae;rdes d&ae;g cynges innto Sancte Johannes mynstre and eac ofer &t;&ae;m landum &d;e Ealdred &ae;rcebiscop h&ae;f&d; si&d;&t;an begitan on minan dagan &t;ider inn. 
 On witword o&d;&d;e on caupland beo hit all freo wi&d; me and wi&d; &ae;ghwilcum men butan &d;am biscope and &d;am mynster preosten. 
 And ne beo nan man swa deort &t;e hit undo &t;&ae;t ic hebbe gecy&d;et Criste and Sancte Johanne. 
 And ic wylle &t;&ae;t &d;&ae;r beo &ae;fre mynsterlif and canonica samnung &d;a hwile &t;e &ae;nig man leofa&d;. 
 Godes bletsunge beo mid eallum cristenum mannum &d;e filsta&d; to &t;es halgan weordscipe.

 Eadward cyng gret mine biscopas &amp; mine eorlas and ealle mine &t;egenes on &t;an sciran &t;ar mine preostas on Paules mynstre habba&d; land inne freondliche. 
 And ic cy&d;e eow &t;&ae;t ic wille &t;&ae;t hig beon heora saca and heora socne wur&d;e &ae;g&t;er ge binnan burh ge buton &amp; swa godera lagana wur&d;e nu swa full and swa for&d; swa hig best w&ae;ron on &ae;niges cynges d&ae;ge o&d;&d;e on &ae;niges biscopes on eallan &t;ingan. 
 &amp; ic nelle &t;&ae;t hig underfon ani ma preostas into heora mynstre &t;onne heora landare aberan mage &amp; hig sylfe willan. 
 &amp; ic nelle ge&t;afian &t;&ae;t heom &ae;nig man &ae;t &ae;nigan &t;ingan misbeode.

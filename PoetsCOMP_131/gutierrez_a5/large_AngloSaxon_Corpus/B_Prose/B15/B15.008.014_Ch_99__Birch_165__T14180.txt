 &T;is synd &t;a land gem&ae;ru &ae;t Eastune &t;e &ae;&t;elbald cyning Myrcna gebocade Utele bisceope into sancte Marian. 
 &AE;rest of Turcanwyllas heafde andlang str&ae;te on Cynelmesstan on Mylenweg. 
 &T;onne andlang hrycges on Heortford. 
 &T;anen andlang streames on Buruhford on foron &t;a spelstowe. 
 &T;onan on Turcandene on Seofenwyllas middeweardan of &t;am wyllan to Balesbeorge su&d;an &t;onne on Cealcweallas. 
 &T;onan eft on Turcandene andlang eft on Turcanwyllas heafod. 
 &T;is w&ae;s gedon &t;y geare &t;e w&ae;s ag&ae;n from Cristes fl&ae;scnesse DCCXLIII on &t;am cynehame &t;e is gecyged Bearuwe.

 Uulfwold abbod gret Gisa bisceop &amp; &AE;gelno&d; abbot &amp; Tofig sciregerefan &amp; ealle &t;a &t;egenas on Sumers&ae;ton freondlice. 
 And ic cy&d;e eow &t;&ae;t Eadweard cingc min hlaford geaf me &t;&ae;t land &ae;t Eofestige &t;&ae;t min f&ae;der ahte and &t;a feowwer wor&d;ias &ae;t &AE;scwican and &t;a &ae;keras m&ae;de &t;e &t;arto gebyria&d; and on wuda &amp; on felda swa micel swa &t;&ae;t ic heafde l&ae;s minan orfe &amp; minra manna orfe and eallswa freols on eallan &t;ingan eallswa &t;&ae;s cinges agen innland to gyfanne &amp; to syllanne &ae;r d&ae;ge &amp; &ae;fter d&ae;ge loc hwam me leofast is. 
 Nu ky&d;e ic eow eallan &t;&ae;t ic habbe gifen &t;&ae;t land in to Sanctus Petres mynstre in to Ba&d;an &t;am munecan to scrudfultume &amp; to fodan eallswa full &amp; swa for&d; swa &t;e cingc hit me geu&d;e on eallan &t;ingan. 
 And se &d;e mine gyfe geece &t;e ic habbe gyfen in to Sanctus Petres mynstre geece God &ae;lmihtig his lif her on worulde and &t;onne he heonan fare gyfe him Crist heofona rice to medes. 
 And se &t;e hit &t;ence to litlianne gelitlige hine God elmihtig her on worulde &amp; &t;onne he heonan faran sceal si his wunung on helle grunde buton he hit &ae;r his ende &t;e sti&d;licor gebete.

 In nomine domini nostri Ihesu Christi. 
 Ic Eadno&d; bisceop cy&d;e on &t;isson gewriton &t;&ae;t ic onborgede XXX mancsa goldes be leadgewihte to minre landhreddinge &ae;t beorhno&d;e. 
 &amp; ic gesealde hym ane gyrde landes to underwedde be Cridian to &t;am forewerdon &t;&ae;t he h&ae;bbe his d&ae;g &amp; ofer his d&ae;g becwe&d;e &t;one sceat &t;am &t;e him leofost beo &t;e on &t;am lande stent. 
 &D;is sind &t;a landgem&ae;ro &t;&ae;re gyrde be Cridian. 
 &AE;rest on Sceocabroces ford  &t;onne east on herpa&d; on &t;one lytlan garan easteweardne  su&d; on &t;a deadan lace on Cridian  up ongean stream on &t;one &ae;nlypan &ae;cer  &t;onne east on herpa&d; eft on Sceocabroces ford. 
 &D;isses ys to gewitnisse  Cnut cyning &amp; Wulstan arcebisceop &amp; Lifing arcebisceop &amp; Birhtwold bisceop &amp; Eadno&d; bisceop &amp; Burewold bisceop &amp; &AE;&d;elwine bisceop &amp; Birihtwine bisceop &amp; &AE;&d;elwerd ealdorman &amp; A&d;elwold abbud &amp; eall se hired on Exancestre &amp; se hired on Cridian tune. 
 &amp; &t;is cydde se bisceop &t;am burhwiton on Exanceastre &amp; to Tottanesse &amp; to Hlidaforda &amp; to Beardastapole. 
 Pax sit hoc seruantibus &amp; infernus sit hoc frangentibus.

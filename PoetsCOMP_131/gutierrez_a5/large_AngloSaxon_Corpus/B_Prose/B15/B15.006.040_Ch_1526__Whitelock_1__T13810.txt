 Ic &t;eodred Ludeneware Biscop wille biquethen mine quiden mines erfes &t;e ic begeten habbe &amp; get bigete  Godes &t;ankes and his halegen  for mine soule &amp; for min louerde &t;at ic vnder bigeat and for mine eldrene and for alle &t;e mannessoule &t;e ic fore&t;ingiae and ic almesne vnderfongen habbe and me sie rithlike for to bidden. 
 &T;at is &t;an erst &t;at he an his louerd his heregete &t;at is &t;anne tua hund marcas arede goldes and tua cuppes siluerene and four hors so ic best habbe and to suerde so ic best habbe &amp; foure schelda and foure spere and &t;at lond &t;at ic habbe at Dukeswrthe and &t;at lond &t;at ic habbe at Illyntone and &t;at lond &t;at ic habbe at Earnningtone. 
 &amp; ic an Eadgiue fifti marcas redes goldes. 
 And into sancte Paules kirke mine to beste messehaclen &t;e hic habbe mid alle &t;e &t;inge &t;e &t;ereto biri&d; mid calice and on cuppe. 
 And mine beste masseboc and alle mine reliquias &t;e ic best habbe into Paules kirke. 
 And ic an &t;at lond aet Tit into seynte Paules kirke &t;en hewen to bedlonde mid al &t;at &t;e &t;eron stant buten &t;e men &t;e &t;er aren fre men alle for mine soule. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Suthereye mid alle &t;e fisco&d;e &t;e &t;erto bire&d; &t;en hewen into sancte Paules kirke and frie men &t;o men for &t;e Bisscopes soule. 
 And &t;eodred bisscop an &t;at lond at Tillingham into sancte Paules kirke &t;o hewen to hare and fre men &t;o men for mine soule. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Dunemowe ouer mine day into sancte Paules kirke &t;en hewen. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Mendham Osgote mine sustres sune ouer mine day buten ic wille &t;at se minstre and hide londes at Myndham to &t;ere kirke. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Scotfor&d; and Mydicaham into Myndham kirke &t;o Godes hewen. 
 And ic an Osgot &t;at lond at Silham &amp; at Isestede &amp; at Chikeringe &amp; at Aysfeld and at Wrtinham and alle &t;e smale londe &t;at &t;ereto bere&d;. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Horham and at Elyngtone into Hoxne into sancte Aethelbrichtes kirke &t;er Godes hewen. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Luthinglond Offe mine sustres sune and his bro&t;er and fre men &t;o men halue and at Mindham also for &t;e Bisscopes soule. 
 And ic an Osgote mine mey Eadulfes sune &t;at lond at Bertune and at Rucham and at Pakenham. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Newetune and at Horninggesh&ae;the and at Ikewrthe and at Wepstede into sancte Eadmundes kirke &t;en godes hewen to are for &t;eodred bisscopes soule. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Waldringfeld Osgote mine Sustres sune and min hage &t;at ic binnin Gypeswich bouhte. 
 And ic Wlstan &t;at lond at Wrtham so it stant. 
 And ic an into eueri bisscopes stole fif pund to delen for mine soule. 
 And ic an &t;en Archebiscope fif Markes goldes. 
 And ic an &t;at men dele at mine biscopriche binnen Lundene and buten Lundene X pund for mine soule. 
 And ic an at Hoxne at mine biscopriche &t;at men dele X pund for mine soule. 
 &amp; Ic wille &t;at men nieme &t;at erfe &t;at at Hoxne stand &t;at ic &t;erto bigeten habbe and dele it man on to  half into &t;e minstre &amp; dele for min soule. 
 And lete men stonden so mikel so ic &t;eron fond and fre man &t;o men alle for mine soule. 
 And ic wille &t;at men lete stonden at Lundenebyri so mikel so ic &t;eron fond &amp; nime &t;at ic &t;erto bigat and dele on to  half into &t;e minstre &amp; half for mine soule  and fre men alle &t;o men. 
 &amp; do men &t;at ilke at Wunemannedune &amp; on Sceon. 
 &amp; lete men stonden at Fullenham so it nu stant buten hwe mine manne fre wille. 
 &amp; on Denesige let stonden so mikel so ic &t;eron fond and dele it man on to  half into &t;e minstre &amp; half for mine soule. 
 And ic an into Glastingbiri V pund for mine soule. 
 And ic an &t;eodred min wite massehakele &t;e ic on Pauie bouhte and al &t;at &t;erto bire&d; &amp; simbelcalice &amp; &t;ere messeboc &t;e Gosebricht me biqua&t;. 
 And ic an Odgar &t;ere gewele massehakele &t;e ic on Pauie bouhte &amp; &t;at &t;erto bire&d;. 
 And ic an Gundwine &t;er o&t;er gewele massehakele &t;at is ungerenad &amp; &t;at &t;e &t;erto bire&d;. 
 And ic spracacke &t;e rede messehakele &amp; al &t;at &t;e &t;erto bired. 
 And wo so mine cuyde ofte God him ofte heuene Riches buten he it er his ende it bete.

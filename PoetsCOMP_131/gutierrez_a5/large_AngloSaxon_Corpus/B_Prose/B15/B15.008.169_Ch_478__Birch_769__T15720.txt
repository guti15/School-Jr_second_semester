 &T;is sand &t;es landes i mare at Stoke up anlang ninge burne o&d; &d;at hrisc lad. 
 Anlang &t;ese richtledes on &t;ane ealde treo stede. 
 &T;anan anlang weies on stanforde. 
 &T;anen an langstreame on botenwelle. 
 And of boten welle on prestes Setel. 
 &T;anen anlang weges on Wiuelesford. 
 &T;anen up anlang mercdene onne stokebroc. 
 &T;anen anlang stocbroc on hringheburne &t;ar iuarest on feng.

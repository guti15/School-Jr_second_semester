 &D;is sant &t;e landimare to Archet of eldmannes wrthe on irichte anlang hina imares to &t;an scamelen. 
 From &t;anne scamelen anlang hine imares o&d; wdebrige. 
 Of wudebricge  a dune anlang streames o&d; land scorfor&d;. 
 Of &t;ane forde  anlang leouen imare o&d; kinghes imare. 
 And gemare  o&d; funtemel for&d; of &t;anne for&d;. 
 Adune mid stream o&t;es bissopes imare ut sceo&t;a&t;. 
 &T;anen on irichte to &t;an lipgete. 
 From &t;ane gete on irichte to anne wonalre. 
 Of &t;ane alre  on irichte to sucgimade hauede. 
 Of &t;ane hauede on irichte &t;urch &d;ene holte to Wlgares imaren. 
 Of &t;an imaren eft on eldemanneswyrde.

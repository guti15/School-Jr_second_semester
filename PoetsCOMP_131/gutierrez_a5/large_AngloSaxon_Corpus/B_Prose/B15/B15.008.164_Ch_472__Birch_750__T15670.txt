 Erest of elde dich above stibbe &t;anne endlang weies on strete so bi strete on holedene endlang denes. 
 To springwellen &t;anen to olde Gore so on cleyate on &t;e elde stibbe. 
 &T;anen on olde burgh suthward of &t;are berwe in on &t;e foulle lake on thone bithe of &t;an bithe in on thone welle bi este foxcotone. 
 Of &t;an welle west on &t;one mer aker of &t;an acre endlang weies eft to &t;e olde dich.

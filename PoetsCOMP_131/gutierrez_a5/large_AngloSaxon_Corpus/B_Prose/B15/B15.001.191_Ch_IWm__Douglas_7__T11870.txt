 Willelm Engla Kyncg &amp; eorl ofer Normandie &amp; ofer &t;a Mans. 
 Ic sende gretincge arcebiscopan &amp; leodbiscopan abbodan &amp; eorlan &amp; eallum geleaffullan mannum. 
 We gelyfa&d; &amp; geara witan &t;at we &t;urh Godes mildheortnysse synd uponhafan to &t;isses Kynerices heahnysse. 
 For &t;i gedauena&d; us &t;at we geornlice sin&ae;gen ymb &t;a gesundfullan sta&t;elunge ealles uran under&d;eoddan folces &amp; swy&d;ost &t;ara &t;e d&ae;ges &amp; nihtes swinca&d; on Cristes &d;eowdome.

 And anbida&d; &t;ara eadigan &ae;rista d&ae;g mid unformolsnodan lichamon. 
 &T;a for &t;issere spr&ae;ce w&ae;s lange hwile micel geflit betwux Arfast biscope &amp; Baldwine &t;ara forseadan cyrican abbode. 
 Us &t;uhte &t;a riht &t;at hi begen comon to urum hyrede &amp; &t;at heora &ae;g&t;er tala come beforan us &amp; beforan uran witan &amp; si&d;&d;an &ae;fter heora beigra tala w&ae;re gescyled &amp; ged&ae;med &t;at rihteste mid ercebiscopa r&ae;de &amp; leodbiscopa &amp; abboda &amp; eorla &amp; o&d;ra wisra manna. 
 Hi comon &t;a begen beforan us to gesetton d&ae;ge &amp; se biscop tealde ful ger&ae;delice his tale gif hit so&d; w&ae;re ac heo w&ae;s eallum mannum ge&t;uht idel &amp; unnyt for&t;i &t;e he n&ae;fde na&t;or ne gewritu ne gewitnesse.

 Si&d;&d;an he &t;is eall geteald h&ae;fde. 
 &T;a brohte he for&d; Cnutes Kynges gewrit &amp; Eadwardes &t;&ae;s wulderfullan Kynges.

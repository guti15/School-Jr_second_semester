 Toeake &t;am  ic heom geu&d;e ealle &t;a cyrican and teo&d;inge and land and hus binnan Lunden o&d;&d;e buten &t;e heom Cristes holde geofan habba&d;  o&d;&d;e her&ae;fter geofen wur&t;e&d;.

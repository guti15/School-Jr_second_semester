 On eallweldandes drihtnes namen &t;e ealle &t;ing geworhte and him seluum to anwelde hyld  and seld &t;am &t;e his willa is. 
 Ic edward of his agenum &t;e he for his godnesse butan minre geearnunge me wealden led. 
 Ic sille and scher cwe&d;e widuten ende for minre saule alysednysse ealle &t;a kirken tunes. 
 Landes  fredomes  ealswa hy hyranuppen on leden gewretene stande&d; in to holme ministere  &t;am abbete et &t;am brodre  &t;e in &t;a stede gode simple &t;ewie&d; to &t;erfe on ecnesse aa buten eande.

 &T;is beth &t;e landmere to stoure &t;are thritti hide. 
 Erest op of stoure on trildoune vppe tril on landscher ford ouer &t;ane ford bi merehale on Conibrok of &t;an brok bi merehawe on wadeleighe northward on stanwey. 
 Of stanweie bi merehawe on deuelisch. 
 Of defelich on stut &t;are on vppe stut on &t;ar dich southward be dich ouer sanddoune on filithleighe &t;anen on Holambrok of &t;an brok on hedemchil &t;anen on Lideman bi lidenan on stoure on &t;an olde wested vp on stoure on &t;er dich be dich on litegesheued. 
 Of lutegesheued bi groue on minnanlinche &t;anen north bi heuede &t;anen est to hedeswelle. 
 &T;anen bi sixe acres north an heued on &t;are &t;orn rewe &t;anen on sand mor of &t;an more on smeryate by seuergate eft on stoure.

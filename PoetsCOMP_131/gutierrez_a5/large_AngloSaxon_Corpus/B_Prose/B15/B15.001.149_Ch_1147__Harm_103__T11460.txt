 Eadward king gret Wulwi biscop &amp; Guyr&d; eorl &amp; alle mine &t;eignes on Oxnefordscire. 
 &amp; ic ki&t;e eow &t;at ic habbe gegifen Criste &amp; Sancte Petre into Westmunstre Gihtslepe &amp; ane healfe hide &ae;t Mersce swa full &amp; swa for&d; swa hit me silfan on hande stod mid saca &amp; mid socna &amp; on eallan &t;ingan. 
 God eow alle gehealde.

 In &d;ane halgene namen ures helendes Cristes. 
 Ich Eaduuard king ofer Engle&d;eode cu&d;e Stigand archebissop  and Harold eorl  and alle mine &t;eines &d;at ich mid &d;usen write gelde and geue Criste and seinte Petre and Wluuold abbode and &d;en bro&d;ren on Cherteseye &d;o X hyden lond on Waltham  and &d;e cherche of &d;an seluen tune  and &d;ane wode of Halewik  and &d;ane of Lidlegewik  mid alle o&d;ere &t;inge &d;e &d;areto belempe&d;  and XX &ae;ceres of &d;are made at Cocham into Weltham. 
 And &d;is ich hem habbe geunne for &d;an hit har into &d;an munstre laig sua ich hit habbe mid mine &t;eines sodsage onlierned of &d;are syrren  and &d;arefore ich wille &d;at hie habben and helden alsua hiere rigte &d;ane tun mid alsua muchele munde alsua on meseluen stant  on alle &t;inge  in wode and in felde. 
 Se man &d;e mine geue her gelitla&d; o&d;er on o&d;ere wisen to auuenden &d;ene hit her beforen awriten is  ben his lif her ilitlade  and his wonienge in helle grunde buten he hit her &d;e sti&d;licor gebete.

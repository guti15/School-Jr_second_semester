 &D;is synd &t;a landgem&ae;ro &ae;t Wenbeorgan stigandes bisceopes. 
 Of &t;an stapole stent innan scocera wege. 
 Swa innan &t;a wogan dice. 
 Of &t;&ae;re wogan dic. 
 Innan beh hilde sloh. 
 Of beh hilde sloh uppan beordune. 
 Of beorhdune to wullafes stane. 
 Of wullafes stan andlang cumbes innan hlydan &ae;wylmas. 
 Swa andlang hlydan innan &t;o ham dic. 
 Of &t;&ae;re ham dic innan &t;a heafod stoccas. 
 Of &t;am heafod stoccan. 
 Innan dorcan swa andlang streames innan &t;a ealdanig dic. 
 Swa andlang dic innan smitan stream. 
 Swa andlang streames on easteweard lilles ham up in &t;ane blacan pyt. 
 Of &t;an blacan pytte on midde wyr&d; &t;ane m&ae;re on &t;&ae;ne stan. 
 Of &t;am stane innan &t;a holan wannan. 
 Of &t;&ae;re holan wannan up on icen hilde weg on &t;&ae;ne h&ae;&d;enan byriels. 
 Of icen hilde wege innan &t;a cwealm stowe. 
 Of &d;ere cwealm stowe innan swyrd &ae;ceras heafod on &t;onam hwitan pyt. 
 Of &t;am hwitan pytte innan &t;a twegen stanes on mortan cumb&ae;. 
 Of mortan cumbe andlang &t;es wogan hlinces. 
 Of &t;am wogan hlince innan &t;a catt hola. 
 Of &t;am catt holan innon &t;one crundel. 
 Of &t;am crundele innan folces dic. 
 And swa eft in on &t;one stapol.

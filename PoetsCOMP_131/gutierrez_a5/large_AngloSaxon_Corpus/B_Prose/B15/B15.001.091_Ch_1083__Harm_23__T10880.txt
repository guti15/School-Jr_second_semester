 Eadward kyng gret Aylmer biscop &amp; Girth earl &amp; Toly and alle mine &t;eynes on Estangle frendlike. 
 &amp; ic kithe ihu &t;at ic habbe unnen Baldewine abbot &t;e abbotriche into Seint Eadmundes biri &amp; alkere &t;inge &t;e &t;er into bireth mid sake and mid sokne so ful &amp; so forth so Lefston abbot o&t;er ani abbot it toforen him firmest haude. 
 Nu bidde ic alle &t;e manne &t;e ben Godes frend and min &t;at him filste to lage &amp; to rithte loc wer him ned beth &amp; he ihure fultumes be&t;urfe for mine loue. 
 And ic nelle nane men &t;afien &t;at him fro hande teo ani &t;ing &t;at ic him gifen habbe o&t;er get &t;inke to done no swither &t;an he min brother were.

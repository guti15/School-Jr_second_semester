 And ich cy&d;e eow &t;at ich habbe geunnan Gisan minan presteo &t;es bissopriche her mid eow and alre &t;are &t;inge &t;as &t;e &t;&ae;r mid richte to gebyra&d; on wode and on felde  mid saca &amp; mid sokna  binnan porte &amp; butan swo ful &amp; swo forth swo Duduc bissop o&t;er any bissop hit formest him toforen hauede on &ae;llen &t;ingan.

 And ich bidde eou allen &t;at ge him fulstan to driuan Godes gerichte lock huer hyt neod sy and heo eowwer fultumes bi&t;urfe. 
 And ich nelle nanne man ge&t;efien &t;at him vram honde teo anige &t;are &t;inge &t;as &t;e ich him unnen habben.

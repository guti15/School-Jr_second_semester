 Willelm king gret mine biscopes and mina eorles and ealle mine &t;egnas  Frencisce and Englisce  on &t;an scyran &t;er sanctus Augustinus hef&d; land inne  freonlice. 
 And ic cy&d;e eow &t;at ic habbe geunnan Gode and sancte Augustine and &t;am hirede &t;e &t;erto hyre&d;  &t;at hieo bien heore sace weor&d;e and heora socna  and gri&d;bryces  and hamsocna  and forstalles  and infangenes&t;eofes  and flemene ferm&d;e ofer heora agene men  binnan burgh and butan  tolles and teames  on strande and on streame  and ofer swa fele &t;egna swa ic heom togeleten habbe. 
 And ic nelle &t;at anig man anig &t;ing &t;eor onteo  butan heom and here wicneres &t;e hyo hit beotecen willan. 
 For &t;am &t;e ic habbe forgifen Gode and sancte Augustine &t;as gerihte minne sawle to alysednesse  ealswa Eadword king  min meg  &ae;r efde. 
 And ic nelle ge&t;afian &t;&ae;t &ae;nig man &t;is abrecan be minan frendscipe. 
 God eow gehel&d;. 
 Amen.

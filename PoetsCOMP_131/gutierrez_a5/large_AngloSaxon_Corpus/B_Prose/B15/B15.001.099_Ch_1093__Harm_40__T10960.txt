 Eduuard king gret Stigand archebiscop and Harold eorl and alle mine &t;egenas on Su&t;&t;ereie frondliche. 
 And ich ku&t;e geu &t;at ich habbe geunnen Criste and Seinte Petre in to Cherteseye &t;ane selue tun and Egeham and &t;orpe and Chabbeham freo wi&d; eche gescot and werc and ware and saca and socha and tol and taem and infangene&t;ef and grithbruche and forestel homsocne and flemnes fremthe and morthslechte inne freols and ut of freols and wi&t; alle &t;e &t;inge &t;e to me beolimpe&t;. 
 And be Godes bletsunge &t;is nan man ne awende for &t;an &t;e ich hit beo minre witene rade for muchelere neode Gode geu&t;e mine saule to helpene.

 Eadward kyng gret Aylmer bisscop &amp; Aelfger erl &amp; alle mine &t;eynes on Suffolke and Norfolke frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ou &t;at ic wille &t;at it is min fulle unna &t;at Alfrich Modercope mot bugan to &t;o tueyen abboten at Seynt Eadmunde &amp; at Sancte Aetheldrede be Godes leue and be mine.

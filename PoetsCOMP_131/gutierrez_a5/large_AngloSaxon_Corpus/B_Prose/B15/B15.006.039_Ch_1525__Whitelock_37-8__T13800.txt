 Her svitele&t; ihu Sifled an hire aihte ouer hire day. 
 &T;at is erst into seynt Eadmunde Mar&t;ingfor&d; for hire leue soule al buten tuenti acres and tueye waine gong wudes and &t;ere wude nor&t;ouer. 
 &amp; min kirke be fre and Wlmer prest singe &t;erat and his bearntem so longe so he &t;en to &t;en hode and fre leswe into &t;ere kirke and mine men fre. 
 And be seynt Eadmund mund &t;er ouer &t;ene freschot. 
 Se &t;e &t;ise cuide wille awenden  be he amansid from God almichtin &amp; from alle hise halegen &amp; fram sancte Eadmunde. 
 Her switele&t; on &t;is write ihu Sifled vthe hire aihte &t;o sche ouer se ferde. 
 &T;at is erst into &t;e tunkirke on Mardingfor&d; V acres &amp; ane toft &amp; II acres medwe and to wayne gong to wude and ic mine landsethlen here toftes to owen aihte &amp; alle mine men fre. 
 And ic an ei&t;er mine brother ane wayn gong to wude. 
 And ic an into Northwich to Cristes kirke IIII retheren and to into sancte Marian and on into mine duzme. 
 And ic an into seynt Eadmunde al &t;at &t;ere to lafe gesceotte  &t;at beth on Mardingfor&d;a hus and hom &amp; wude and feld &amp; on medwe and on yrue. 
 And gif ic hom cume &t;anne wille ic sitten on &t;at londe mine day and after mine day stonde &t;at cuide. 
 And wo so &t;is awende  God awende his ansene from him on domesday buten he it here bete.

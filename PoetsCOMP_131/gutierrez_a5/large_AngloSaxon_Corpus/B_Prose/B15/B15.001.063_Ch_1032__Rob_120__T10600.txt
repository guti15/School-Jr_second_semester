 Regnante in perpetuum domino nostro Iesu Christo. Rixiendum urum Drihtne h&ae;lendum Criste on ecnysse &d;am heahstan &amp; &t;an untosprecendlican ealra &t;inga &amp; ealra tida scyppend  se &t;e manegum &t;ingum his mihtum sette&d; &amp; wealde&t;  eac swylce &t;am upahafenlican &amp; &t;am unasecgendlican rice &t;&ae;t &d;e he &t;ises lifes eadignysse &amp; ges&ae;lignesse n&ae;nigum &t;ingum ne forl&ae;te. 
 For&t;an ic Eadwerd Englalandes cyngc mid Godes gyfe &amp; mid minra witena ge&t;eahte &amp; r&ae;de  ic forgyfe &t;isne freols into &t;&ae;re haligan stowe &ae;t Hortune Gode to lofe &amp; Sancte Marian to wur&d;mynte &d;e seo stowe ys fore gehalgod. 
 &amp; me on gemende ys mid &t;isum eor&d;licum &t;ingum &t;a ecanlican gestreon to begytende sicut Salomon dixit  redemptio anim&e; propri&e; diuitie  swa swa Salomon cw&ae;&d; &t;&ae;t we sceoldon mid urum spedum urum sawlum &t;a ecan ges&ae;lignysse begytan. 
 For&t;an ic cu&d;lice mid ge&t;eahte ge&t;afunge &amp; leafe ealra minra biscope &amp; eorla &amp; butan &d;an ealra minra selostra witena. 
 Ic forgef &t;isne freols for mine agene sawle &amp; for mire leofostra frenda  &t;&ae;t eall &t;&ae;t land &t;e li&d; into &t;an haligan mynstre &ae;t Hortune &t;&ae;t hyt sy f&ae;stlice &amp; unawendedlice a ecelice gefreod ealra cynelicra &amp; ealdordomlicra &t;eowdoma ge &t;eoffengces ge &ae;ghwylcere unea&d;nesse ealles woroldlices broces buton fyrdsocne &amp; burhgeweorce &amp; bricggeweorce. 
 Gif hwa &t;anne si &t;&ae;t he hine for Godes lufon to &t;am geeadmedan wylle &t;&ae;t he &t;as mine gyfe geycan wylle o&d;&d;e gemenigfyldan geyce him &ae;lmihtig God eall god her on worolde &amp; his dagas gesundfullie. 
 Gif &d;onne hwylc mann to &t;am ge&t;ristl&ae;ce o&d;&d;e mid deofles searwum to &t;am beswicen sy &t;&ae;t he &t;is on &ae;negum &t;ingum lytlum o&d;&d;e mycelum &t;ence to abrecende o&d;&d;e to awendenda wite he &t;onne &t;&ae;t he &t;&ae;s riht agyldende sy beforan Cristes &t;rymsettle &t;onne ealle heofonwara &amp; eor&d;wara on his andwerdnysse beo&d; onstyred &amp; onhrerede buton he hyt &ae;r her on worolde mid rihte gebete. 
 &D;is w&ae;s gewriten on &t;am geare &t;e w&ae;s agan fram Cristes acennednysse an &t;usend geara &amp; an &amp; sixtig geara &amp; an &t;am tacncircule &t;&ae;t seofanteo&t;e gear. 
 Ic Eadwerd cyngc mid &t;&ae;re halgan Cristes rodetacne &t;is het swi&t;e geornlice getrymman &amp; gef&ae;stnian &t;isne freodom on &ae;lmihtiges Godes naman &amp; on ealra his halgena. 
 Ic f&ae;stlice bebeode &t;&ae;t hine n&ae;nig minra &ae;fterfyligendra eft ne onwende ne on n&ae;nigum d&ae;lum l&ae;ssum ne on marum hyne ne onwyrdon. 
 Ac &t;es freols a ecelice for&d; &t;urhwunige swa lange swa God wylle &t;&ae;t cristen geleafa mid Angelcynne untosceacan wur&t;e ealra &t;&ae;ra woroldcundra hefignyssa &t;e her beforan genemnede syndon &amp; se h&ae;bbe.

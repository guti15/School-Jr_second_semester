 Erest on dicheforde on alum endlang stremes on mereforthesthorn &t;anen suth on wirte on an wei endlang weies on an stapel on hennegraue. 
 &T;anen on ramesleighe suthward on londscher dich endlang diches to bru endlang stremes on alum semido up on alum on bolamtre. 
 &T;anen on &t;e olde alum on ane dich endlang diches on strete yate endlang diches &t;are est to dichforde. 
 Lotthesham: 
 &D;anen is &t;is &t;are fif hide londmere. 
 Erest on nymede of wideres we&t;es forde &t;anen on hagen on whateleighe &t;anen est on hazen on winwares wik &t;anen on landchere&t;orn &t;anen on anne stapel &t;anen on lippe stapel. 
 &T;anen on longar thorne &t;anen est on nimede and &t;er limpatz to twelf mede acres on scheobanwerzthe twentin bi suthan bru an twen bi northan.

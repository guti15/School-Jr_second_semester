 Leofgiue gret hyre leuedi Godes gretinge. 
 And ic kithe &t;e mine quide wat ic Crist an and his halegan mine louerdes soule to alisednesse and mine into &t;e holy stowe &t;er ic self resten wille  &t;at is at seynt Eadmundes byri  &t;at is &t;at lond at Hintlesham and &t;at lond at Gristlyngthorp buten &t;at lond &t;at Ailsi hauede &t;at he at his lauerd ernede and at me. 
 And ilc &t;ridde aker on &t;an wude at Hintlesham habbe Ailri and ingong and vtgong be feld and be fenne. 
 And ic wille &t;at Ailric prest and Ailfric prest and Ailri diacon habben &t;at minstre at Colne so here lofard it hem vthe. 
 And ic wille &t;at Aylfric prest ben on &t;at ilke loh &t;e Aigno&d; was. 
 And be se mund ofer &t;an minstre &t;e is ofer alle o&t;ere. 
 And ic an &t;an kinge to marc goldes  for min eruenumen to &amp; gealaeste &t;at gold  and mine lauedi &t;at lond at Belhcham buten &t;at lond &t;at Godric mine stiward haue&d;  &t;at habbe he  and Alfward bisscop &t;at lond at Benetleye buten half hide &t;e Osemund hauede. 
 &T;at ic an Alfwy min stiward and &t;at he on sit &amp; Alfno&d; min stiward &t;at lond &t;at Berric hauede vnder hande and Alfgar mine mei &t;at lond at Borham and Alfric mine mey Withgares sone &t;at lond at Bromforde and Stigand &t;at lond at Willauesham and Ailric mine brotheres sone &t;at lond at Stonham and at Waldingfeld and at Lithtletic and Alflet mine douhter &t;at lond at Hagele and Godwine min a&d;um &amp; mire meygan &t;a &t;re hide at Werle and Lefkyld &t;a to hide onfast his owen. 
 And Aylmer habbe &t;at lond at Stonham &t;e ic hym er to hande let to reflande. 
 And ic an Godric mine reue at Walddingfeld &t;a &t;ritti acre &t;e ic him er to hande let and Ailric min hirdprest &amp; Alric mine cnihtes &t;at lond at Lalleford &t;e me best heren willen. 
 &amp; Ailric prest habbe an hide at Forendale. 
 And ic wille &t;at alle mine men ben fre on hirde and on tune for me and for &t;o &t;e me bigeten. 
 And ic bidde mine leuedien for Godes louen &t;at &t;u &t;olie &t;at ani man mine quide awende. 
 And se &t;e it awende  God almichin awende his ansene from him on domesday buten ic it self do. 
 God &t;e healde. 
 Nu sinden &t;ise write &t;re  on is mid &t;ise kinges halidome and o&t;er at seynt Eadmunde &amp; &t;ridde mid Leofgiue seluen.

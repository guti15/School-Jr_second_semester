 &T;ys synden wa lande gemer atte Kyngesbury. 
 Erest on Pedridan op alang stremes on Lambrokes gemoure. 
 Op alang Lambrokes to Schepton gemer to we &t;rires dyke. 
 Adoun &t;er to &t;ere lake lancke &t;ere dyke adoun to Foulenbroke. 
 Alang Foulenbroke out on a Moure to &t;an Northlangpulle. 
 For&t; by Lanpulle by Northen Pendanmede wy&t; Myddelnyes Lanpulle. 
 Of &t;an Lanpulle to &t;ornylake fornegen Thorny Myddewarde on Southhal. 
 O&t; on an lake fornegen Esthele yn an Horsyes Northlandpulle. 
 For&t; out on an Moure est on Pedrenstreme. 
 Thys beth Kyngesbery landboundes.

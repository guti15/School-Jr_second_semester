 &D;is sint &tbar; landgemaera to niwebotle. 
 Aerest sudweard yes de semaere to seurtre bouan yest wid anes &t;ornes &t;onan nord to &t;an broce &amp; ilc oder acra be fastan hode. 
 Of &t;an broce &tbar; est to &t;an dice andlang dices to Elfredes bricge &t;onne nord to ysemere &amp; &t;onan to saxferdes lawe of saxferdes lawa nord on &t;a dic &amp;lang dices on &t;ene sic &amp;lang sices be &t;ere acera anheafda on &t;a strete of &t;ere strete east onan up one yalde be nordan faryisdal. 
 On &t;a ealdan dic andlang dices to brusun lawa of rusun lawa andlang &t;es Greneweges &t;aet hit cyn&d; to fif lawan &amp; &t;onne sud &amp;lang &t;e sor Deoppendala. 
 Sud andlang dene up to &t;an wege andlang weges to &t;ins housum &t;on swa ford andlang weges &tbar; eft to saer irtre.

 Erest of buckansticke west on buckanmere to &t;an ruancroundele &t;aron middeward burnestowe to &t;an stone. 
 &T;anen west endelang smaleweies on &t;are croundel bi este &t;a ertheburgh and so north on rigt to hordenestone &t;anen to elden berwe. 
 And soa endelang diches north to &t;an white stone &t;anen to &t;an Stanberwe yate &t;anen to &t;e litel berwe &t;anen endelang meres to middel dich &t;anen north to rigt weie to &t;an ston on mid rigt weies. 
 And soa to loppancombe ouerward &t;ar forth endlang furch on rigte to merewelle endlang stremes to folanruwers ouer &t;an ridde to &t;an stone whit oute &t;ar irwelonde &t;ar forth to &t;an beche. 
 &T;anen of piwannere on lippanstibbe &t;ar on kingesdich endland dich to melanbroke of milanbrok on lortanbroke &t;anen on lortanberwe so vp endlang furtg to mereberwe. 
 &T;anen out to &t;an widen yate of &t;an gate to &t;an horeston ford to rammesburi yate of rammesburi so forth endelang weies to buckansticke.

 Hich egger bischob bebude alle mine afthercomende to cridintones ministre  &t;at &t;e geuenisse &t;at hich behet afther &t;at &t;at hich forsoc sainte marie ministre for mire pride and to rome hy wende. 
 And &t;ar hich hontromede sove yer and more an thar me bifore hylomp &t;e reue quene on heuene marie  and nedede me to scrifte go to &t;an holiapopa leon and be his lore do. 
 And he me radde anbidia ones similissamuis on rome  and behithe to giuenisse to bote mire mere leuedie and to mire cherche. 
 And &t;ar hich begeth of souentine archebischobes to &t;ousent dages to giuenesse of hure sinna alle &t;ane ministre Criditones aginneras an godieras of bute hende heuereche day to comde  and the worke bitrende and for Ad&t;elston synge. 
 And of o&t;er bischopes on estende romes burg and on westende of &t;as mountes mougeus feour &t;ousent dages and sonne dages. 
 And of ho&t;er archebischopes and bissopes on &t;isser side &t;as mountes on wale londes &t;e ich alle hi sogte ho&t;er bi wise sende  so mi scrift was  &t;ru &t;ousend dage and souene. 
 Of &t;ru archebischopes on bruthude and of hyre onderbischopes on &t;ousent dages and sixsti dages. 
 Of feour archebischopes of hirlonde and of hire ondersetele bischopes nunhenne honderd dages and tin dages. 
 And &t;o hich com hom to &t;an gete on &t;an cherchay  hich silf sinfol hi astahelede &t;ar on hondred dage in helf &t;an gete euemore wo so bith for wrecche edger. 
 And &t;o hich halgede &t;ane cherichay at hechere hurne at hondreid dage. 
 And eft hich bus&t;ide to Rome for mire lacthere to bote and se holia popa Lion &t;ay geuenisse iuasnede and morede hit mid on &t;ousend dage  and awirgede alle &t;aye &t;e hit as&t;ide and &t;ane minister of Cridiantone wi&t;sette.

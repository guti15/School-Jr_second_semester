 In nomine Domini. Ealhburh hafa&t; geset myd hyre freonda &t;eahtunga &t;&ae;t man &ae;lce gere agyfe &t;am hywum to Sanct&ae; Agustine of &t;am lande &ae;t Bradanburnan XL ambura mealtes &amp; eald hry&d;er &amp; IIII we&t;eras &amp; XL &amp; CC hlafes &amp; ane w&ae;ge spices &amp; cyses &amp; IIII fo&t;ro wudes &amp; XX henfugla. 
 Swylc man se &t;&ae;t land h&ae;bbe  &t;as &d;ingc agyfe for Ealdredes saule &amp; for Ealhburge. 
 &amp; &t;a hiwan asingan &ae;lce d&ae;ge &ae;fter hyra ferse &t;&ae;ne sealm for hia  Exaudiat te Dominus. 
 Sw&ae; hwylc man swa &t;is abrece  si he asceaden fram Gode &amp; fram eallum hallgum &amp; fram &t;an halgan were on &t;ysum life &amp; on ecnesse. 
 &T;onne synt her &ae;fter &t;ara manna naman to gewitnesse &t;isse gesetednesse  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne. 
 Gif hit &t;onne swa geg&ae;&t;  swa we na ne wysca&d;  &t;&ae;t hwylc broc on becume &t;urh h&ae;&t;en folc o&t;&t;e hwylce o&d;re earfo&t;nesse  &t;&ae;t hit man ne m&ae;ge &t;&ae;s geres gel&ae;stan  agife on o&t;rum geare be tweofealdum. 
 Gif &t;onne git ne m&ae;ge  sylle on &d;riddum geare be &d;ryfealdum. 
 Gyf he &t;onne git ne m&ae;ge ne nelle  agife land &amp; bec &t;am hiwum to Sanct&ae; Agustine.

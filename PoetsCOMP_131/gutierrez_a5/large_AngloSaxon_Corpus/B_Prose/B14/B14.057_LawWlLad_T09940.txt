 Willelmes cyninges asetnysse. 
 Wilhelm cyng gret ealle &t;a &t;e &d;ys gewrit to cym&d; ofer eall Englaland freondlice &amp; beot &amp; eac cy&d; eallum mannum ofer eall Angelcynn to healdenne  &t;&ae;t is. 
 Gif Englisc man beclypa&d; &ae;nigne Fr&ae;nciscne mann to orneste for &t;eofte o&d;&d;e for manslihte o&d;&d;e for &ae;nigan &t;ingan  &t;e gebyrige ornest fore to beonne o&d;&d;e dom betweox twam mannum  habbe he fulle leafe swa to donne. 
 &amp; gif se Englisca fors&ae;c&d; &t;&ae;t ornest  &t;e Frencisca  &t;e se Englisca beclypa&d;  ladige hine mid a&t;e ongean hine mid his gewitnesse &ae;fter Nor&d;mandiscere lage. 
 Eft: Gif Frencisc man beclypa&d; Engliscne man to orneste for &d;am ylcan &t;ingan  se Englisca be fulre leafe hine werige mid orneste o&d;&d;e mid irene  gif him &t;&ae;t gecwemre by&d;. 
 &amp; gif he untrum by&d; &amp; nelle &t;&ae;t ornest o&d;&d;e ne mage  begyte him lahlicne spalan. 
 &amp; gif se Fr&ae;ncisca by&d; ofercuman  he gyfe &t;am cynge III pund. 
 &amp; gif se Englisca nele hine werian mid orneste o&d;&d;e mid gewitnesse  he ladige hine mid irene. 
 &AE;t eallan utlaga &t;ingan se cyng gesette  &t;&ae;t se Englisca ladige hine mid irene. 
 &amp; gif se Englisca beclypa&d; Frencisne mid utlagan &t;ingan &amp; wille hit &t;onne on him geso&d;ian  se Fr&ae;ncisca bewerie hine mid orneste. 
 &amp; gif se Englisca  ne durre hine to orneste beclypian  werige hine se Fr&ae;ncisca mid unforedan a&d;e.

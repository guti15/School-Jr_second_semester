 &T;is syndon &t;a domas &d;e Hlo&t;h&ae;re &amp; Eadric  Cantwara cyningas  asetton.

 Gif mannes esne eorlcundne mannan ofsl&ae;h&d;  &t;ane &d;e sio &t;reom hundum scillinga gylde  se agend &t;one banan agefe &amp; do &t;&ae;r &t;rio manwyr&d; to.

 Gif mannes esne frigne mannan ofsl&ae;h&d;  &t;ane &t;e sie hund scillinga gelde  se agend &t;one banan agefe &amp; o&t;er manwyr&d; &t;&ae;r to.

 Gif he &d;onne byrigan forw&ae;rne  XII scillingas agylde &t;am cyninge  &amp; sio se sacy swa open swa hio &ae;r wes.

 Gif he &t;onne &t;&ae;t nille  gelde &t;onne C buton a&d;e  si&t;&t;an ane neaht ofer &t;&ae;t gesem hie. 
 Gif man mannan an o&t;res flette manswara hate&t; o&d;&d;e hine mid bism&ae;rwordum scandlice grete  scilling agelde &t;am &t;e &t;&ae;t flet age  &amp; VI scillinga &t;am &t;e he &t;&ae;t word togecw&ae;de  &amp; cyninge XII scillinga forgelde.

 Gif man w&ae;pn abregde &t;&ae;r m&ae;n drincen &amp; &d;&ae;r man nan yfel ne de&t;  scilling &t;an &t;e &t;&ae;t flet age  &amp; cyninge XII scillinga. 
 Gif &t;&ae;t flet geblodgad wyr&t;e  forgylde &t;em m&ae;n his mundbyrd &amp; cyninge L scillinga. 
 Gif man cuman feorm&ae;&t; III niht an his agenum hame cepeman o&t;&t;e o&d;erne &t;e sio ofer mearce cuman &amp; hine &t;onne his mete fede  &amp; he &t;onne &ae;nigum m&ae;n yfel gedo  se man &t;ane o&d;erne &ae;t rihte gebrenge o&t;&t;e riht fore wyrce.

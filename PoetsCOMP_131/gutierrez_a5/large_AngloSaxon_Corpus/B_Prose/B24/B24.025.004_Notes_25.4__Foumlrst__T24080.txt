 Prima etas mundi  ab Adam usque ad Noe  fuerunt anni  III DCLXXVI Her ongin&d; ymbe &t;yses middangeardes ylde  hw&ae;t w&ae;s fram Adame o&d; Noe  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne &t;reo &t;usend wintra and syx hund and sixandhundseofantig wintra. 
 Secunda  a Noe usque ad Abraham  fuerunt anni CCC XCVII &t;onne w&ae;s seo o&d;er yld fram Noe o&d; Abraham  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne &t;reo hund wintra and seofanandhundnygantig wintra. 
 Tertia  ab Abraham usque ad Moysen  fuerunt anni DCCCCLXX &t;onne w&ae;s seo &t;ridde yld fram Abrahame o&d; Moyses  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne nygan hund wintra and hundseofantig wintra. 
 Quarta etas  a Moyse usque ad Dauid  fuerunt anni CCCCXCIII usque in transmigrationem Babylonis &t;onne w&ae;s seo feor&d;e yld fram Moyse o&d; Dauid  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne feower hund wintra and &t;reoandhundnygantig wintra &t;&ae;re for&d;gewitenesse &t;&ae;re micclan burge Babiloni&e;. 
 Quinta etas mundi fuit a Dauid usque ad Christum  fuerunt anni DLXXXVIIII &t;onne w&ae;s seo fifte yld fram Dauide o&d; Crist  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne fif hund wintra and nyganandhundeahtatig wintra. 
 Sexta aetas a natiuatate domini usque in preteritum mundi I wintra &amp; XXXI &t;onne is seo syxte yld fram Cristes acennednesse agan an &t;usend wintra and anand&t;rittig wintra. 
 Ab initio mundi usque in natiuitate Christi fuerunt anni VI C XXV &amp; menses VIIII &t;onne fram middangeardes fruman o&d; Cristes acennednesse synd syx &t;usend wintra and fif and twentig and an hund wintra and nygan mon&d;as. 
 Ab origine mundi usque in passionem domini fuerunt anni VI CL VIII &t;onne fram &t;isse worulde frym&d;e o&d; Cristes &t;rowungtide w&ae;ran syx &t;usend wintra and eahta and fiftig and hund wintra. 
 Aetas domini nostri Iesu Christi  quam secundum humanitatem in hoc seculo habebat  hoc est anni XXX &amp; menses tres  horum quippe annorum sunt XI milia &amp; DCCCXX dies &t;is is seo yld  &t;e ure drihten h&ae;lend Crist &ae;fter menniscnesse her on worulde gebad  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne &t;rittig wintra and &t;ry mon&d;as and feowertig daga and mon&d;a  syndon endlufan &t;usend daga and eahta hund daga and twentig daga. 
 Ex quo autem die baptizatus est dominus  usque in diem passionis sue  sunt dies iuxta numerum mensium DCCCXX Of &t;am d&ae;ge  &t;e drihten gefullod wer&d;  o&d; his &t;rowungd&ae;ge syndon eahta hund daga and twentig daga &ae;fter &t;ara mon&d;a getele. 
 Sancta Maria w&ae;s on &t;reoandsixtigan wintra  &t;a heo of middangearde ferde  and heo w&ae;s feowertyne geara eald  &t;a heo Crist acende  and heo w&ae;s mid him XXXIII geara on middangearde  and heo w&ae;s syxtyne gear &ae;fter him on worulde. 
 Amen.

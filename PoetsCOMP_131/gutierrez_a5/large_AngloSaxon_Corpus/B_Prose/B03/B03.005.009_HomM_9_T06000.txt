 De Inclusis. 
 Sum deofelgast s&ae;de sumen ancre eall helle geryn &amp; hire tintrege. 
 He s&ae;de &t;&ae;t &t;es middaneard n&ae;re na mare dryges landes togeanes &t;an garsegen &t;onne man precode &ae;nne prece on ane wexbrede  for his mycelnesse eall &t;as eor&d;e abuten li&d;  &amp; lytel d&ae;l is under hefene dryges landes &t;&ae;t hi ne sy eall mid garsecge oferurnen. 
 Eft cw&ae;&d; se deofel to &t;am ancre: Gif &ae;nig man beo&d; ane niht on helle &amp; beo&d; eft &t;onen of al&ae;d  &amp; me betyne &t;one garseg eall abuten mid isene  &amp; &t;one eall afylle mid fyre up of hefene hrof  &amp; ymbesette hine eall abuten mid smi&d;es belian  &amp; to &ae;cen belie &ae;nne man &amp; se habbe Samsones str&ae;nch&t;e  &amp; wi&d;innen &t;a garsecge mid mannen afylle &amp; &ae;lc man h&ae;bbe hamer on hande  &amp; &t;a belies blawen &amp; &t;a hameres beatan &amp; se leg arise  for ealle &t;isen broche ne arist seo werige sawle for &t;&ae;re pine &t;e hi on helle ane niht &t;olede. 
 Georne cunnie &ae;lc man to befleonne hellewite  for &t;on hit is &t;&ae;rinne sarlic to wunigenne. 
 Se awergede gast s&ae;de &t;an ancre &t;&ae;t he ne mehte all asecgen &t;a pine &t;e &t;a earme sawle &t;olia&d; on helle. 
 &T;urh deofles swicdom &amp; Adames gemeleaste we w&ae;ren &ae;t frum&t;e ut of Paradis ascofene &amp; on &t;as wrecce world as&ae;nd &t;e we nu onwunia&d;. 
 &T;&ae;rfore nabbe we her nane f&ae;stlice stede ne langsume &ae;&t;ele  ac we gelomlice gehera&d; &amp; d&ae;ghwamlice geseo&d; to hwam se eor&d;lice d&ae;l &amp; se m&ae;nnissce gecynde gewur&d;an sceal. 
 Be&t;&ae;nce se man hine &t;e him selfes rech&d;  for &t;on se man &t;e hefene rice secen wile  he hit sceal secan mid swince &amp; naht mid e&d;nesse ne mid idelnesse.

 In Uigilia Ascensionis Men &t;a leofestan  &t;is syndon halige dagas and gastlice &t;enunge mid mannum; for&t;i &t;e &t;as dagas n&ae;ron for gytsunge geworhte ne for nanum rence. 
 ac hi w&ae;ron geworhte for micelre neode eallum folce. 
 Hit w&ae;s on &t;am dagum  men &t;a leofestan  &t;e &t;as dagas w&ae;ron geworhte  &t;&ae;t eall folc w&ae;s swa wi&d;erweard wi&d; God and swa swi&d;e gymeleas  &t;&ae;t hi ne rohtan ne ne gymdon na&d;er ne God ne Godes beboda. 
 ne hi m&ae;ssan ne gymdon ne hi cirican ne sohtan. 
 &T;a w&ae;ron manega prophetan and witegan. 
 &T;a w&ae;s &t;&ae;r an propheta Helias gehatan. 
 &T;a clypode he mid hluttre heortan to Criste and b&ae;d Crist &t;&ae;t he sumne ege to heom asende &t;&ae;t &t;&ae;t folc wurde gecn&ae;we &t;&ae;t he w&ae;re God on heofonum and n&ae;re nan o&d;er god buton him anum ofer heofonum  wealdend ne sceppend  buton he ana. 
 &D;a cydde God his mycelen mihte and sende heom &t;a tacna to eor&d;an &t;&ae;t hi mihton ongyton &t;&ae;t he w&ae;s so&d; God on heofonum. 
 s&ae;nde heom &t;a to tacne &t;&ae;t &t;rim wintrum and six mon&t;um ne com nan ren of heofonum on eor&d;an ne deaw up ne sloh ne mist up ne aras ongean &t;&ae;re heofonlicum druh&t;an. 
 &D;a w&ae;s mycel ege mid mannum swa &t;&ae;t eal mancyn wear&d; acweald swi&d;e gehende and ealle weastmes forwurdon on &t;yssere eor&d;an. 
 &D;a ofhreaw &t;am witegan Helian eft and him of&d;uhte &t;a seo bene &t;e he &ae;r &ae;t Gode geb&ae;d. 
 &T;a gesamnode he ealle &t;a witegan &t;e on &t;am lande w&ae;ron and r&ae;ddon heom betweonan hu hi sceoldan Criste geeadmedan &t;&ae;t he heom asende &t;a gewideru &t;&ae;t heora w&ae;stmes mosten weaxan and hi &t;&ae;rmid mosten Gode gecweman on &d;yssere eor&d;an. 
 &D;a geworhton hi &t;&ae;s &t;ri dagas for &t;&ae;re micclan neode &t;e heom w&ae;s &t;&ae;s w&ae;teres forwyrned  and budon ofer ealle eor&d;an &t;&ae;t eal folc sceolde f&ae;sten &d;ri dagas o&d; &d;e &t;&ae;t heo on eafen steorran geseagan. 
 And swa eac &t;&ae;t cild &t;e w&ae;re geboron on niht swa hit mon genam fram &t;am modrum breostum &t;&ae;t hit nan &t;ing sucan ne mosta &ae;t &t;&ae;re moder &ae;r steorran arisan of heofonum  ne nanes cynnes &t;ingc ne men ne nytena. 
 &D;a mihtan gehyron  &t;e on &t;an dagum lif&ae;s w&ae;ron  mycele wanunge and granunga and geomerunga  &ae;g&d;er ge men ge nytena. 
 &D;a b&ae;r man of &ae;lcum halgum temple ealla &t;a halgan reliquias ut &t;e &t;&ae;r on innan w&ae;ron. 
 &T;a com Cristes stefn of hefenum to eor&d;an and let dynian ofer ealc &t;&ae;ra manna &t;e &t;as &t;ry dagas his f&ae;sten abr&ae;c &ae;r &t;a halgan reliquias eft into &t;am temple comon  and on &ae;lc &t;&ae;ra manna &t;e an fodspor gesceod eode mid &t;am halgan reliquium o&d;&d;e mid linenum hr&ae;gle o&d;&d;e mid w&ae;pne o&d;&d;e on horse geride o&d;&d;e huntian ongunne binnan &t;ysum &t;reom dagum o&d;&d;e fram m&ae;sse gange &ae;r he h&ae;fde hlam genuman &ae;t &t;&ae;s prestes handum. 
 And se man &t;e nele nu &t;as &t;ry dagas mid Godes halgan reliquian b&ae;rfot gangan  &t;urh &t;a nigon helle he sceal &ae;r domesd&ae;ge ealswa feola si&d;an swa he her fotspora gesceod eode ofer &t;&ae;s prestes bebod. 
 And &ae;fre he sceal steppan on byrnendum n&ae;ddran and &ae;fre he gemet deofol &ae;fter o&d;rum and &t;eostru &ae;fter &t;eostrum. 
 For&t;i &t;e &t;&ae;r syndon IX hus innan &t;&ae;re helle and &ae;lc &t;&ae;ra husa is anra mile deop and o&d;re wid  and nis nan man on bocum swa cr&ae;ftig &t;&ae;t mage asecgan hw&ae;t innan &t;am nigo husum sy godes o&d;&d;e winsumes  swa micel swa an fugel m&ae;g mid his l&ae;stan fi&d;ere windes aswingan. 
 Ac eac hit is mid wyrmum afylled and mid wean and mid wr&ae;csi&d;an  &t;&ae;r n&ae;fre leoht ne leoma&d;. 
 ac &t;&ae;r bi&d; &t;eostru be&t;rycced and hungor and &t;urst and heto and yldo and unh&ae;lo and wanung and granung and to&d;a gristbitung. 
 &D;a eardungstowe sceal &ae;lc wif gesecan &t;e o&d;erne wer lufa&d; ofer hire rihte &ae;we  and &t;&ae;r hi sceolan mid sawle and mid lichamen beon beseanced innan &t;am deopan &t;eostra &t;&ae;r heom n&ae;fre d&ae;g ne gedaga&d;  ac heo &t;&ae;r sceolon &ae;fre wunian mid wyrman and mid deoflum buton heo hit &ae;r on &t;isum life gebeton. 
 &D;onne is &t;&ae;r o&d;er atelic stow &t;&ae;r sceal &t;&ae;t cild &ae;fre wunian &t;e her on heor&d;an his f&ae;der and his moder wyrgia&d;. 
 &D;onne is &t;&ae;r &t;ridde grimlic stow &t;&ae;r sceal &ae;lc m&ae;den inne standan o&d; hire sweoran &t;e heo forlige&d; &ae;r hire bryd&t;ing gewur&d;e. 
 &D;onne is &t;&ae;r feor&d;a atelic stow &t;&ae;r sceal &ae;lc prest standan o&d; &d;a lippan &t;e his folc nele mid rihte wissian hu hi sceolon Gode gehyran. &T;onne is &t;&ae;r seo fifte stow mid wyrmum afylled &t;&ae;r sceolon inn&ae; besincon &t;a mansworan and &t;a mor&d;deadan and &t;a hlafordswicon; &t;&ae;r heo sculan &ae;fre wunian a worulda woruld; &t;&ae;r hi sceolan &t;a wyrmes toslittan and deofla potian. 
 &D;onne is &t;&ae;r seo sixto stow seo is mid hellehundum afylled; &t;&ae;r sceal &ae;lc &t;&ae;ra wifa inne besincan &t;e her hire cild acwella&d; o&d;&d;e hire were forr&ae;de&d;; &t;&ae;r hio sceal &ae;fre wunian and heon sceolan &t;a hellehundes toslitten a woruld in ecnysse. 
 &T;onne is &t;&ae;r seo seofo&d;e stow seo is mid fyre on&ae;le&d;; &t;&ae;r sceal &ae;lc &t;ara manna &t;e o&d;res mannas cotan b&ae;rne&d; for &ae;nigre frace and &t;e o&d;erne berepa&d; rihtes gestreones  and &ae;lc &t;&ae;ra manna &t;e gerefa by&d; and o&d;re men repe&d; and reafad; &ae;lc by&d; deofles &t;egen. 
 On &t;am fyre hi sceolan besincan upp o&d; &d;a eagan. 
 &D;onne is &t;&ae;r seo eahcto&d;e stow atelic seo is eall mid fulum st&ae;nce afylled and mid blacum deoflum; &t;&ae;r ealc &t;&ae;ra manna sceal beon inne bes&ae;nced o&d; &d;e his cin innan &t;am fulum st&ae;nce &t;e unnitt spr&ae;ca&d; and idel innan cirican. 
 He sceal ceowan his agene tungan and hi eft aspiwan; &t;&ae;t sceal beon &ae;fre his weorc o&d; &d;e woruldes ende. 
 &D;onne is &t;&ae;r seo nigo&d;e atelic stow seo is eall mid ni&d;um and mid n&ae;ddrum afylled; &t;&ae;r sceal &ae;lc &t;&ae;re manne inne beon beseanct mid sawle and mid lichamen &t;e ni&d; bere&d; wi&d; his bro&d;er o&d;&d;e wi&d; nyttan freond. 
 Nu  men &t;a leofestan  uto we becyrran &t;a fulen eardungstowe and geearnian us &t;&ae;t leoflice liht  &t;e us heofona Hlaford h&ae;f&d; gegearwod fram frym&d;e &t;issere worulde gif we his lare gehlestan willa&d;. 
 Hu m&ae;g ic eow secgan hu we &t;a f&ae;geran eardungstowe begyton magon? 
 L&ae;tan we fram urum heortan ealle yfele d&ae;de and yfele ge&t;ohtes and mana&d;es and untid&ae;tas and ofermycele drynces and ouerrence and ydelne gylp and leasunga and stala  and innian &t;a gode &t;earfan and &t;a nacodan scrydan and fedan and beon wi&d; earme wuhta eadmoda and mildheort&ae;. 
 &T;onne milsa&d; us God on heofonum. 
 Men &t;a leofestan  &t;&ae;r syndon heofonas us to blisse togeanes &t;am hellebrogan; nu syndon ealle &t;a mid m&ae;r&d;an afylled. 
 &D;eah &t;e w&ae;re twa and hundseofontig manna and h&ae;fde &ae;lc &t;ara manna twa and hundseofontig heafodo and &ae;lc &t;&ae;ra h&ae;fda twa and hundseofonti tungena and &ae;lc &t;&ae;re tungena h&ae;fde twa and hundseofontig gereorda and hig swa lange spr&ae;can &t;&ae;t hi ealle w&ae;ron werige  ne mihten hi &t;one teo&d;an d&ae;l asecgan hu feola beorhtra beama is on &t;an seofan heofonum ne hu fela hwitra blosmena. 
 &AE;al hit is gefylled mid hwitum englum.  
 Wel &t;am &t;e &t;&ae;r mot &t;one eard gestigan  &d;&ae;r n&ae;fre niht ne &t;eostra&d; ne d&ae;g ne abblinna&d;  ac &ae;fre &t;&ae;r is leoht buto &t;eostrum and &ae;fre &t;&ae;r is geogo&d; buton yldum and &ae;fre &t;&ae;r is h&ae;l buton yfele and &ae;fre &t;&ae;r is englene sang and haligra gasta dream.

 &T;&ae;r nis na&d;er ne hungor ne &t;urst ne cele ne oferh&ae;tu ne cwealm ne sar ne sorh ne yfel ne unh&ae;lo; ac &t;&ae;r is blis and m&ae;r&d;e and &ae;fre &t;&ae;r is d&ae;g &ae;fter d&ae;ge and dream &ae;fter dreama and &t;&ae;r is ealre cyninge Cyning Crist on heofonum.

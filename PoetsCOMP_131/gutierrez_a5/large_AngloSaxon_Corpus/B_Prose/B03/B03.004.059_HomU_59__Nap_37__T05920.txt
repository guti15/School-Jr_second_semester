 Nu  leofan men  nu we willa&d; l&ae;ran godes &t;eowas &ae;rest  &t;&ae;t hy hy sylfe georne be&t;encan and &t;urh godes fultum cl&ae;nnesse lufian and gode &ae;lmihtigum eadmodlice &t;eowian and ealle heora wisan regollice fadian and bocum and gebedum geornlice filian and bodian and bysnian godes riht georne and for eal cristen folc &t;ingian gelome.

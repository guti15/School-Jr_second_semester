 MEN &d;a leofestan  weor&d;ian we on &d;issum andweardan d&ae;ge Sancte Petres Cristes apostola ealdormannes &t;rowungtide  &amp;.

 &amp; &t;a &t;a hwile &t;e he &t;&ae;r stod  he wear&t; f&ae;ringa geong cniht  &amp; sona eft eald man.

 &amp; he &t;a wear&d;.

 &T;a cw&ae;&t; Neron to Petre  For hwon w&ae;ron gyt swa treowlease  o&t;&t;e incer cynn?

 Sermo Lupi. 
 Leofan men  do&d;  swa ic l&ae;re  gehyra&d;  hw&ae;t ic wylle secgan to &t;earfe  gyme se  &d;e cunne  his agenre neode. 
 Eallum cristenum mannum is mycel &t;earf  &t;&ae;t hy godes lage fylgean and godcundre lare geornlice gyman; and huru gehadodum is ealra m&ae;st &t;earf  for&d;am &t;e hy scylan &ae;g&d;er ge bodian ge bysnian godes riht georne o&d;rum mannum. 
 Nu wylle we l&ae;ran godes &t;eowas georne  &t;&ae;t hy hy sylfe w&ae;rlice be&d;encan and &t;urh godes fultum cl&ae;nnesse lufian and gode &ae;lmihtigum eadmodlice &t;eowian and for eall cristen folc &t;ingian gelome. 
 And &t;&ae;t hi bocum and gebedum geornlice fylgean and bodian and bysnian godes riht georne. 
 And &t;&ae;t hy l&ae;ran gelome  swa hy geornost magan  &t;&ae;t gehadode regollice and l&ae;wede lahlice heora lif fadian to &t;earfe hym sylfan. 
 And ealle cristene men we l&ae;ra&d; swy&t;e georne  &t;&ae;t hy inwerdre heortan &ae;fre god lufian and rihtne cristendom geornlice healdan and godcundan lareowan geornlice hyran and godes lara and laga smeagean and spirian oft and gelome hym sylfum to &t;earfe. 
 And we l&ae;ra&d;  &t;&ae;t &ae;lc cristen man geleornige huru  &t;&ae;t he cunne rihtne geleafan ariht understandan  and paternoster and credan  be &d;am &t;e he wylle &ae;fter for&d;si&d;e mid cristenra gemanan on gehalgedan restan and gebedr&ae;denne habban o&d;&d;on &ae;r on life husles beon wyr&d;e  for&d;am he ne by&d; wel cristen  &t;e &d;&ae;t geleornian nele  ne he nah mid rihte o&t;res mannes to onfonne &ae;t fulluhte ne &ae;t biscopes handa  &ae;r he hit geleornige  &t;&ae;t he wel cunne. 
 And we l&ae;ra&d;  &t;&ae;t man wi&d; healice synna and wi&d; deoflice d&ae;da scylde swy&t;e georne on &ae;ghwylcne timan; and bete swy&t;e georne be his scriftes ge&d;ehte  se &d;e &t;urh deofles scyfe on synna befeolle. 
 And we l&ae;ra&d;  &t;&ae;t man wi&d; fulne galscype and wi&d; unrihth&ae;med and wi&d; &ae;ghwylcne &ae;wbrice warnie symble. 
 And we l&ae;ra&d; and bidda&d; and on godes naman beoda&d;  &t;&ae;t &ae;nig cristen man binnan syx manna sibbf&ae;ce &ae;fre ne gewifige on his agenum cynne ne on his m&ae;ges lafe  &t;e swa neahsibb w&ae;re  ne on his wifes nydmagan  &t;e he sylf &ae;r ahte  ne on his gef&ae;deran ne on gehalgodre nunnan. 
 Ne on &ae;l&ae;tan &ae;nig cristen man ne gewifige &ae;fre ne na ma wifa  &t;onne an  h&ae;bbe  ac beo be &d;&ae;re anre &t;a hwile  &t;e heo lybbe  se &d;e wylle godes lage gyman mid rihte and wi&d; hellebryne beorgan his sawle. 
 And we l&ae;ra&d; eac georne manna gehwylcne  &t;&ae;t he godes ege h&ae;bbe symle on gemynde and d&ae;ges and nihtes forhtige for synnum  domd&ae;g adr&ae;d&ae; and for helle agrise and &ae;fre him gehende ended&ae;ges wene. 
 And we l&ae;ra&d; swy&t;e georne freonda gehwylcne  &t;&ae;t he fram synnum georne gecyrre and o&d;rum mannum unrihtes styre  and &t;&ae;t he ofer ealle o&d;re &t;ing lufige his drihten  and &t;&ae;t he oft and gelome h&ae;bbe on gemynde  &t;&ae;t mannum is m&ae;st &t;earf oftost to gemunanne  &t;&ae;t is  &t;&ae;t hy rihtne geleafan  swa we &ae;r cw&ae;dan  anr&ae;dlice habban on &d;one so&d;an god  &t;e is waldend and wyrhta ealra gesceafta  and &t;&ae;t hy rihtne cristendom rihtlice healdan. 
 And &t;&ae;t hy godcundan lareowan geornlice hyran and godes larum and lagum geornlice fylgean. 
 And &t;&ae;t hy godes cyrcan &ae;ghw&ae;r georne gri&d;ian and fri&d;ian and mid leohte and lacum hy gelome gegretan and hy sylfe &t;&ae;r georne oft and gelome gebiddan to Criste. 
 And &t;&ae;t hy godes gerihta &ae;ghwylce geare mid rihte gel&ae;stan and freolsa and f&ae;stena rihtlice healdan. 
 And &t;&ae;t hy sunnandaga cypinga and folcgemota on &t;am halgan d&ae;ge georne geswican. 
 And &t;&ae;t hi godes &t;eowas symle werian and weor&d;ian. 
 And &t;&ae;t hy godes &t;earfan frefrian and fedan. 
 And &t;&ae;t hi wydewan and steopcild to oft ne ahw&ae;nan  ac georne hy gladian. 
 And &t;&ae;t hi el&d;eodige menn and feorrancumene ne tyrian ne ne tynan. 
 And &t;&ae;t hi o&d;rum mannum unriht ne beodan ealles to swy&d;e  ac manna gehwylc o&d;rum beode &t;&ae;t riht  &t;&ae;t he wylle  &t;&ae;t man him beode  be &d;am &t;e hit m&ae;&d; sy. 
 A sy godes nama ecelice gebletsod and hym symle sy wuldor and weor&d;mynt &ae;fre to worolde.

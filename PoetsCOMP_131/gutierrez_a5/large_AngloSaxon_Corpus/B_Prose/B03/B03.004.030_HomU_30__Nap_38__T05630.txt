 And do gehwa mid his scriftes ge&t;eahte georne on godes est to gode  &t;&ae;t he don m&ae;ge.

 And cl&ae;nsige his heortan gehwa fram &ae;ghwilcum ni&d;graman and hetelican yrre. 
 And  gif he hwam abulge  gebete &t;&ae;t georne and girne to godes &t;eowum  &t;&ae;t hy him si&d;&d;an absolutionem macigan.

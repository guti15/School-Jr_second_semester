 To m&ae;ssepreostum. 
 Eala  feala is &t;ara  &t;e sacerdhades on unriht georna&t; m&ae;ssepreosthades for gitsuncge woroldgescryna and na cunnan na  &t;&ae;t hi cunnan sceoldan. 
 Be &t;am spr&ae;c se witiga swi&t;e egeslice and &t;us cw&ae;&t;: ue sacerdotibus  qui comedunt peccata populi. 
 &T;&ae;t is on englisc: wa &t;am sacerdum  he cw&ae;&t;  &t;e freta&t; folces sinna. 
 &T;&ae;t sindon &t;a  &t;&ae; na cunnan folce wi&t; sinna gewarnian and sinna gestyran and nima&t; heora sceattas to teo&t;ingan and eac on manegum o&t;rum &t;ingum; and na&t;or ne hi mid bodengum wel na l&ae;ra&t; ne mid bisnuncgum wel na t&ae;ca&t; ne mid gebeddr&ae;dan forge&t;ingia&t;. 
 Ah hit is swi&t;e micel &t;earf  se &t;e &ae;r &t;ysum misdyde  &t;&ae;t he hit heonanfor&t; hit georne gebete and rihtl&ae;ce hine sylfne  be &t;am &t;e he wille beon wi&t; god geborgen  o&t;&t;e he sceal mid ealle forwyr&t;an on &t;am toweardan life. 
 Bitere scel hit him wyr&t;an forgolden  &t;&ae;t he for deadum mann nim&t;  buton he hit mid m&ae;ssan and mid sealmsange and mid &ae;lmessan inlice forgilde. 
 Hali drihten gemiltsige us eallan  amen.

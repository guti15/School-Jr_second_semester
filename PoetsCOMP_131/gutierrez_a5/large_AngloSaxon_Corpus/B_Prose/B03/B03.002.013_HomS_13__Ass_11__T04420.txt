 Gehyra&d; nu  men &t;a leofestan  hu &t;as halgan bec eow myngia&d; and eow &t;one weg &ae;tywia&d; and eac in gel&ae;da&d; to heofena rice  and hu ge magon &t;a yfelu &t;isses middaneardes forbugan and gode gefylstendum to &d;am ecan life becuman. 
 Clypa&d; ure drihten &t;urh his witegan swi&d;e sti&d;lice to &d;am preostum  &t;e godes folce wisian scylan  &t;&ae;t heo eow myngian and cy&d;an eow so&d;f&ae;stnesse weg  and &t;us cwe&d;: Clama  ne cesses  quasi tuba; exalta vocem tuam et adnuntia populo meo peccata eorum  &d;&ae;t is on urum ge&t;eode: Clypa  ne ablin &d;u  ac ahefe up &t;ine stefne swa beme and cy&d; minum folce heora synna. 
 Gif &t;u &t;onne nelt &t;am unrihtwisan his synna cy&d;an  &t;u bist his sawle scyldig. 
 Her ge magon gehyran  bro&d;ru &d;a leofestan  &t;&ae;t ealle godes m&ae;ssepreostas and biscopas and o&d;re godcunde lareowas synd on mycelre frecednysse  gif heo eow godes bebodu ne cy&d;a&d;. 
 Be &d;am cwe&d; drihten &t;urh &t;one witegan: Audiens ex ore meo sermonem meum  adnuntiabis eis ex me  non ex te  &d;&ae;t is on urum ge&t;eode: Of minum mu&d;e gehlystendum &t;u bodast hym mine spr&ae;ce of me  n&ae;s of &t;e. 
 Nu ge magon ongitan  hwylcne ege &t;a m&ae;ssepreostas habba&d; toweardne  &t;onne god wile girnan to hym ealra &t;ara sawla  &t;e hym her on life bet&ae;hte synd. 
 &T;onne we witan  &t;&ae;t anra manna gehwylc m&ae;g earfo&d;lice be his agenre sawle gescead agyldan on domes d&ae;g. 
 For&t;am us is micel &t;earf  &t;&ae;t we eow &t;one weg cy&d;en and &t;&ae;t si&d;f&ae;t &ae;tywen  &t;e ge &t;urh magon to &d;am ecan life becuman. 
 And eac eow is to warnienne  for&d;an gif hit us is frecenlic  &t;&ae;t we hit eow ne cy&d;en  hit is eow micele mare frecednes  &t;&ae;t ge nellen gefyllan  &t;&ae;t we eow to eowre sawle &t;earfe cy&d;a&d;. 
 &T;onne &t;urh &t;one witegan is gecweden: Gif &t;u bytst &t;am synfullum  &t;&ae;t he of his unrihtan wegum gecyrre and he gecyrran nelle of his unrihtwisnessum  he swelt and &t;u h&ae;fst alysed &t;ine sawle fram his witum. 
 Ealra &ae;rest sceal &ae;lc cristen man witan and on his heortan trumlice healdan &t;&ae;t &ae;reste bebod and &t;&ae;t m&ae;ste  &t;&ae;t is  &t;&ae;t he lufige his drihten mid ealre heortan and mid eallum mode and mid eallum m&ae;gne. 
 And o&d;er bebod is &t;ysum fulgelic  &t;&ae;t is  &t;&ae;t &ae;lc man lufige his nextan swa hine sylfne. 
 Nu wen&d; manig man  &t;&ae;t &t;is sy gecweden be his freondum  &t;e him gesibbeste synt and &t;urh m&ae;gr&ae;denne nehste. 
 Ac hit nis be &d;am gesibban na swi&d;or gecweden  &t;onne be &d;am fremdan. 
 Ac ure &ae;lc is o&d;res nehsta &t;urh &t;one rihtan geleafan  &t;e we to urum drihtne habba&d;  and we synd gebro&d;ru gecigede  for&d;y &t;e god is ure f&ae;der on heofonum. 
 &T;y cwe&d; se h&ae;lend on his godspelle: Omnia qu&ae;cumque vultis  ut faciant vobis homines  ita et vos facit illis  &d;&ae;t is on urum ge&d;eode: Don ge o&d;rum mannum &ae;lc &d;ara &t;inga  &t;e ge willan  &t;&ae;t hy eow don.

 Gif ge &t;onne &t;as &d;reo forman bebodu on eower mod asetta&d; and mid healicre arwur&d;nesse and mid micelum gefean onfon willa&d;  &t;onne magon ge mid godes fultume becuman to o&d;rum godum d&ae;dum. 
 Nu is eac to witanne  bro&d;ru &t;a leofestan  &t;&ae;t we scylon &t;as lenctentide mid swi&d;e micelre begymene healdan  &t;&ae;t we &t;urh nan &t;ing n&ae;nne d&ae;g &t;is f&ae;sten ne abrecon  ne on &ae;te  ne on drence  butan sunnandagum. 
 On &d;am nis nan f&ae;sten geboden.

 On o&d;re tide is on &ae;ghwylces mannes willan and on his scriftes dome gesett  hu he for his synnum f&ae;ste. 
 On &d;isse tide gif man ne ful f&ae;st  man forgym&d; godes agen bebod. 
 On o&d;erre tide se &d;e wel f&ae;st  gestryn&d; him mede &ae;t gode. 
 On &t;isse tide se &d;e rihtlice ne f&ae;st  he gestryn&d; him helle wite  butan hit for untrumnesse o&d;&d;e for geogo&d;e sy  for&d;am &t;e ure drihten gehalgode &t;as ilcan dagas &t;urh Moysen and Heliam and &t;urh hine sylfne  &t;&ae;t heora &ae;lc gef&ae;ste feowertig daga and feowertig nihta. 
 Earnostlice is to warnienne wi&d; &t;&ae;t  &t;e manega men on gewunan habba&d;  &t;&ae;t synd &t;a &d;e wena&d;  &t;&ae;t hy f&ae;stende synd rihtlice  gif hy eta&d;  sona swa hy &t;&ae;t belltacen gehyra&d; &t;&ae;re nigo&d;an tide  &t;&ae;t is seo nontid. 
 Ac hit nis na to gelyfanne  &t;&ae;t hy f&ae;stende synd rihtlice  butan hy &ae;fter hyra m&ae;ssan &t;&ae;s &ae;fenes tid wur&d;unga gebiden. 
 Ac &t;a &t;e &t;is f&ae;sten a riht healdan willa&d;  &t;onne scylon hy &ae;rest gan to heora scriftan and hym unforwandodlice his synna ealle geandettan  &t;e he &ae;fre fram geogo&d;e o&d; &d;as elde gedon h&ae;f&d;  o&d;&d;e on ge&t;ance  o&d;&d;e on worde  o&d;&d;e on weorce  ealle be his wisunge gebetan and a geswican.

 &T;onne we ealle synt anes drihtnes &d;eowas and mid anum wyr&d;e gebohte  &t;&ae;t is mid his agenum blode  &t;e he for eall mancyn ageat  and gif we wel do&d;  &t;onne mote we ealle to anre eadignesse becuman.

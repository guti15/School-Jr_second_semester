 Be Godes &t;eowum. 
 Leofan men  ic bidde gehyra&d;  hw&ae;t ic wylle secgan &t;urh Godes gyfe us eallum to &t;earfe  gecnawe  se &t;e cunne  and ic bidde eow  leofan men  do&d;  swa ic l&ae;re  hlysta&d; swy&d;e georne  hw&ae;t ic nu secge. 
 Eallum cristenum mannum is mycel &t;earf  &t;&ae;t hi Godes lage fyligean and godcundre lare geornlice gyman. 
 And huru gehadodum is ealra m&ae;st &t;earf  for&d;am &t;e hi sceolon &ae;g&d;er ge bodian ge bysnian Godes riht georne o&d;rum mannum. 
 Nu wille we l&ae;ran Godes &t;eowas georne  &t;&ae;t hi hy sylfe w&ae;rlice be&t;encan and &d;urh Godes fultum cl&ae;nnysse lufian and Gode &ae;lmihtigum eadmodlice &t;eowian and for eall cristen folc &t;ingian gelome. 
 And &t;&ae;t hi bocum and gebedum geornlice fyligean and bodian and bysnian Godes riht georne. 
 And &t;&ae;t hi l&ae;ran gelome  swa hi geornost magan  &t;&ae;t gehadode menn regollice libban and l&ae;wede lahlice heora lif fadian to &t;earfe heom sylfum. 
 And gif hit geweor&d;e  &t;&ae;t folce mislimpe &t;urh here o&d;&t;on hunger  &t;urh stric o&d;&d;e steorfan  &t;urh unw&ae;stm o&d;&d;e unweder  &t;onne r&ae;dan hi georne  hu man &t;&ae;s bote sece to Criste mid cl&ae;nlicum f&ae;stenum and mid cyrcsocnum and mid eadmedum benum and mid &ae;lmessylenum. 
 And &ae;fre hi sylfe beon heom ge&t;w&ae;re and swi&d;e anr&ae;de for Gode and for worulde  swa swa hit awriten is: Quasi cor unum et animam unam habentes. 
 And gyf m&ae;ssepreost his agen lif rihtlice fadige  weaxe his wyr&d;scype  and gyf he elles do  gebete hit georne.

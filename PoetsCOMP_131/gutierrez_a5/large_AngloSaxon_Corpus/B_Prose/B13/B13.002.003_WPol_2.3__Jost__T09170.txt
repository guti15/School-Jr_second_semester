 Incipit de synodo. 
 Deus in adiutorium meum intende  ter. Gloria patri. Kyrri eleison. Pater noster. Et ne nos inducas. Adiuua nos  Deus  adiutorium nostrum. Benedicamus Domino. Benedicite. Benedictio. 
 Omnipotens Deus sua uos clementia benedicat et sensum in uobis sapientiae salutaris infundat. Catholice uos fidei documentis enutriat et in sanctis operibus perseuerabiles reddat. Gressus uestros ab errore conuertat et uiam uobis pacis et caritatis ostendat  quod ipse prestare dignetur. 
 Bisceopum gebyre&d; on sino&t;e &ae;rost &t;inga  &t;&ae;t hi smeagan ymbe anr&ae;dnesse and so&d;e gesibsumnesse heom betweonan  and hu hi cristendom magan fyrmest ar&ae;ran and h&ae;&t;endom swy&d;ost afyllan; and h&ae;bbe &ae;lc bisceop canonboc to sino&d;e.

 Bisceopum gebyra&d;  &t;&ae;t &ae;lc o&t;erne warnige  gyf he hw&ae;t be o&d;rum gehyre o&d;&d;e sylf agyte  and &ae;lc o&t;erne b&ae;ftan werige and nan o&d;rum his &t;earfe ne hele  ac weor&d;&t;ige &ae;lc o&t;erne wordes and d&ae;de  and beon  swa heom to gebyre&d;: Quasi cor unum et anima una. 
 Bisceopum gebyre&d;  &t;&ae;t symle mid heom faran and mid heom wunian wel ge&t;ungene witan  huru sacerdhades  &t;&ae;t hi wi&d; r&ae;dan magan for Gode and for worulde and &t;&ae;t heora gewitan beon on &ae;ghwylcne timan  weald hw&ae;t heom tide.

 Bisceopum gebyre&t;  &t;&ae;t hi ne beon to gliggeorne  ne hunda ne hafeca hedan to swy&d;e ne woruldwlence ne idelre rence. 
 Bisceopum gebyre&d;  &t;&ae;t hi ne beon to feohgeorne &ae;t hadunge ne &ae;t halgunge ne &ae;t synbote ne on &ae;nige wisan on unriht ne strynan. 
 Bisceopum gebyra&d;  gif &ae;nig o&d;rum abelge  &t;&ae;t man ge&t;yldige o&d; geferena some  butan heom sylfe geweor&d;an m&ae;ge  and na sceotan na to l&ae;wedum mannum ne ne scendan na hy sylfe.

 Bisceopum gebyre&t;  &t;&ae;t hi &ae;fre on &ae;nine man curs ne settan  butan hy nyde scylan; gyf hit &t;onne &ae;nig for micclum gewyrhtum nyde gedo and man gebugan nelle &t;onne gyt to rihte  &t;onne cy&d;e hit man eallum geferum  and hi ealle &t;onne settan on &t;&ae;t ylce and him &t;&ae;t cy&d;an; gebuge &t;onne sy&d;&d;an and gebete &t;e deoppor  gif he Godes miltse and bletsunge recce.

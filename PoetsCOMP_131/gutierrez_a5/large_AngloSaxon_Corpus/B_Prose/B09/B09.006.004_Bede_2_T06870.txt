 Ond &t;urh &t;&ae;t se foresprecena biscop Sanctus Paulinus &t;&ae;re cirican scire onfeng mid &t;&ae;s arcebiscopes h&ae;se &amp; Eadboldes &t;&ae;s cyninges; &amp; he &d;a h&ae;fde  o&d;&t;&ae;t he on his tide &t;&ae;t heofonlice rice gestah mid w&ae;stme his &t;&ae;s wundurlican gewinnes. 
 On &t;&ae;re cyricean he forlet his pallium &t;&ae;t he onfeng fram &t;am Romaniscan papan  &amp; his lichama on sibbe reste&d;. 
 Forlet he on his cyricean &ae;t Eoforwicceastre Iacobum &t;one diacon; w&ae;s &t;&ae;t cyriclic wer &amp; halig on eallum &t;ingum: &amp; he sy&d;&d;an langre tide on &t;&ae;re cyricean w&ae;s wuniende  &amp; micle hlo&t;e &t;urh his lare &amp; fullwihte &t;am ealdan feonde afyrde. 
 &amp; &t;one tun  &t;e he oftust on eardode  wel neah Cetrehtan geen to d&ae;ge mon his naman cneode&d;. 
 &amp; for&t;on &t;e he w&ae;s on cyricsonge se gel&ae;redesta  &amp; &ae;fter f&ae;ce sibbe tide comon eft on Nor&d;anhymbra m&ae;g&d;e &amp; &t;&ae;t rim weox &t;ara leafsumra  &t;&ae;t he w&ae;s monigra magister ciriclices sanges &ae;fter Romane &t;eawe &amp; Cantwara. 
 &amp; he &t;a eald &amp; dagana full  &t;&ae;t is godra d&ae;da  &ae;fter &t;on &t;e halige writu spreca&d;  &t;et he f&ae;dera weg w&ae;s fylgende.

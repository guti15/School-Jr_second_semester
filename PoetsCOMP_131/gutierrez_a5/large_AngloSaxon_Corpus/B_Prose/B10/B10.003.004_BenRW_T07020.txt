 BE &T;AM &T;AT &AE;ALL RIHTWISNESSE ON &T;YSSERE REGOLE NIS GESTA&T;ELOD. 
 &T;as regole we write  &t;&ae;t we &t;urh hyre gehealsumnesse on mynstre sumne del sceawie  &t;&ae;t we habbad arwyrdnesse on &t;eawe od&d;e anginn of rihtfulle life. 
 Seo &t;e fullfremednesse haligere drohtnunge habben wile  &t;&ae;t synd gesette to lyfes bysene haligera f&ae;dere lare  &t;&ae;re gemynd &amp; gehealdsumnes gel&ae;t &t;a mynecene to full&t;ungenre fremednesse. 
 La  hwylc lare l&ae;t &t;one mann to regolice life  hwylc boc od&d;e sermun of &t;&ae;re ealden &ae; od&d;e of &t;&ae;re niwe nis swide riht regol to mancynnes life? 
 Od&d;e hwilc boc  &t;e ure lareowas macede  &t;e were on rihte gelafe  &t;&ae;t ne segd  &t;&ae;t we &t;urh rihtne rynolne cume to &t;am &t;e us gesceop? 
 Od&d;e &t;a collatiuns  &t;&ae;t Iohannes Cassianus awrat  &amp; &t;ere haligere manna lif  &t;e on Uitas Patrum is geredd &amp; &t;e regol ures haliges fader Basilies  hw&ae;t is hit elles bute getymbrunge &amp; tol haligre manne &amp; &t;ere mynecene  &t;e wel &amp; rihte libba&d; &amp; gehyrsume synde. 
 To sceame &amp; to ascyndnesse hi synd us  &t;e asolcene synd &amp; yfele &amp; gymeleaslice mid unrihte libbad. 
 Swa hwilc swa onnettende efst to &t;am heofonlice &ae;&t;ele gefremme &ae;rest &t;urh Godes fultum &t;isne lytle regol  &t;e her awriten is  to anginne godre drohtnunge. 
 &amp; swa &ae;t nyxtan &t;urh Godes fultum &t;u cymst to mare ge&t;in&d;e godre magne &amp; lare &t;&ae;re halgene  &t;e we bufe embe spece  &t;&ae;t a gewur&d;e ece lif to leane &amp; wunung mid Gode ealle &t;am  &t;e &t;isse regole bly&d;elice fylia&d;.

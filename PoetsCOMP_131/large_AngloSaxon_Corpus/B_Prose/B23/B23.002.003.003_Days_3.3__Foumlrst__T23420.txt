 &D;reo dagas syndon on XII mon&d;um mid &t;rim nihtum  on &t;am ne bi&d; nan wifmann akenned. 
 And swahwylc w&ae;pnedmann on &t;am dagum akenned bi&d;  ne forrota&d; his lichama n&ae;fre on eor&d;an  ne he ne fula&d; &ae;r domesd&ae;ge. 
 Nu is an &t;ara daga on &ae;ftewyrdne Decembre  and &t;a twegen on foreweardan Ianuarie &t;am mon&d;e. 
 And feawe synd  &t;e &t;as geryne cunnan o&t;&t;e witan.

 Eahtatina nihtum &ae;r hlafm&ae;ssan gange&d; se styrra up se is gehaten canes  &t;&ae;t is se hara steorra. 
 He bi&d; firenre gecynde and weallende. 
 Hi beo&d; &t;urh &t;&ae;re sunnan h&ae;tu and &t;&ae;s steorran to &t;am swi&t;e getwifealdede &t;&ae;t hi scina&d; swa swi&d;e on midde niht swa on midne d&ae;g. 
 And hira lichaman weaxa&d; and swella&d;  and hwilon bersta&d;  &t;&ae;ra daga beo&d; &t;reo and fiftig &t;&ae;t se steorra bi&t; uppe  for &t;am by&t; &ae;ghwilc man &t;e unhalre &t;e him blod l&ae;te on &t;issum dagum and hit sume heora feore negediga&d;. 
 And nele nan man &ae;t nanre tide &t;e riht cann in &t;a tid mannum blod l&ae;tan  and eallum cristenmannum on &t;a tid is blodl&ae;tincg forboden  eahtatina nihtum &ae;r hlafm&ae;ssan and fif and &t;rittig nihta ofer hlafm&ae;ssan. 
 Dagas dies. 
 &T;a hwile.

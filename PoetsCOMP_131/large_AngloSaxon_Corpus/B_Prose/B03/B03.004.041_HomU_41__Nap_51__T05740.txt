 To eallan folke. 
 Leofan menn  lagia&d; gode woroldlagan and lecga&d; &t;&ae;rtoeacan  &t;&ae;t ure cristendom f&ae;ste stande  and &t;&ae;t ures hlafordes kinedom up arise  and &t;&ae;t ealles folces fri&d; wyr&d;e betere  &t;onne hit git sig. 
 Ic hi aginne  endie se  &d;e wylle o&d;&d;e bet cunne. 
 Rihtlaga is  &t;&ae;t man o&d;ran gebeode  &t;&ae;t he wylle  &t;&ae;t man him gebeode ah &t;&ae;t is &t;am rican la&d;. 
 &T;is is seo lagu  &t;e we healdan sculan: &ae;rest we sculan &ae;nne god lufian and wyr&d;ian and &ae;lcne o&d;er oferhogian; and we sculan &ae;nne cristendom ealle healdan and &ae;lcne h&ae;&d;ene dom mid ealle oferhogian; and we sculan ealle &ae;nne cynehlaford rihtlice healdan and lif and land samod ealle wyrian; and we sculan iornlice riht up ar&ae;ran and unriht iorne afyllan. 
 &T;onne wylle we &ae;rest  &t;&ae;t godes riht for&d; ga and woruldriht sy&d;&d;an; and we wylla&d;  &t;&ae;t mann fri&d; and freondscipe rihtlice healde for gode and for worulde. 
 And  gif man abrece &t;&ae;t  &t;e botwyr&d;e syg  bete hit iorne. 
 Open &t;yf&d;e and hlafordes searwu and ab&ae;re mor&d; &ae;fter woruldlagu is botleas &t;ing. 
 Manslagan and manswican and &t;a  &t;a heora hlaford forl&ae;ta&d;  &t;&ae;r him m&ae;st neod by&d; on folces neode  and wordlagan and wedlogan beo&d; ealle gode la&d;e  buton hi hit gebetan for gode and for worulde. 
 Godcunde bote sece man iorne to his scrifte; and  gif his scrifte ofhagie  sece man to &d;am leodbiscope; and  gif man fur&d;or scule  to &t;am arcebiscope; and sy&d;&d;an to &d;am papan  gif me for micelre neode scule; ne cunne we him sy&d;&d;an ne fur&d;or t&ae;can  butan to &t;am hextan  &t;&ae;t is  to gode sylfan. 
 And we wylla&d;  &t;&ae;t &ae;ighw&ae;r cyricgri&d; stande and gehalgodes cinges handgri&d; efen unwemme. 
 R&ae;de ge nu for&d; lagan gode fyr&d;or. 
 Ic wolde  gif me to anhagode. 
 Halig drihten  gemildsa us eallan.

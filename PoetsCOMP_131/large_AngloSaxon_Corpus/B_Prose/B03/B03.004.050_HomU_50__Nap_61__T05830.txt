 Be cristendome. 
 A Cristo enim cristiani sunt nominati  Cristus autem caput nostrum est  et nos membra eius; Crist is ealra cristenra manna heafod  and ealle cristene men syndon to Cristes limum getealde  gyf hy heora drihtne gecwema&d; mid rihte. 
 And hy scylan swy&t;e georne cristendom &ae;fre healdan mid rihte and Cristes cyrcan secan gelome heom sylfum to &t;earfe and Cristes gerihta rihtlice gel&ae;stan. 
 And &t;&ae;t is an &ae;rest  &t;&ae;t man geteo&d;ige &ae;ghwylce geare &t;&ae;t  &t;&ae;t god sende &t;onne on geare folce to &t;earfe on corne and on flexe and on gewelhwylcon w&ae;stme. 
 And arise seo &ae;certeo&d;ung a  be &d;am &t;e seo sulh &t;one teo&d;an &ae;cer &ae;r geeode  be godes miltse and be &d;&ae;s cynges and be ealles cristenes folces and be &d;&ae;re steore  &t;e Eadgar cyng gelagode. 
 And sy &ae;lcere geogo&d;e teo&d;ung gel&ae;st be pentecosten be wite and eor&d;w&ae;stma be ealra halgena m&ae;ssan. 
 And romfeoh gel&ae;ste man &ae;ghwylce geare be Petres m&ae;ssan; and  se &d;e hit ne gel&ae;ste  sylle &t;&ae;rtoeacan xxx peninga and bringe sende to Rome and gylde &t;am cynge on Engla lage cxx scillinga. 
 And cyricsceat gel&ae;ste man on Engla lage to Martines m&ae;ssan: and  se &d;e &t;&ae;t ne gel&ae;ste  forgylde hine mid twelffealdan and &t;am cynge cxx scillinga. 
 And sulh&ae;lmessan gebyra&d;  &t;&ae;t man gel&ae;ste be wite &ae;ghwylce geare  &t;onne huru xv niht beo&d; agan ofer eastertid. 
 And leohtgescot gel&ae;ste man be wite to Cristes m&ae;ssan and to candelm&ae;ssan and to eastron  do oftor se  &d;e wylle. 
 And saulscat is rihtast  &t;&ae;t man gel&ae;ste aa &ae;t openum gr&ae;fe. 
 And freolsa and f&ae;stena be wite healde man rihtlice. 
 And ealle godes gerihta fyr&d;rige man georne  ealswa hit &t;earf is. 
 And  gyf hwa &t;&ae;t nelle  gewylde man hine to rihte mid woroldlicre steore.

 To middanwintra. 
 Ostende nobis Domine. 
 Her seg&d; &t;is halige godspel be &t;&ae;re hean medomnesse &t;isse halgan tide &t;e nu onweard is  &amp; us l&ae;re&d; &t;&ae;tte we &t;as halgan tiid gedefelice &amp; cl&ae;nlice weor&d;ien  Godes naman to lofe &amp; to wuldre  &amp; ussum sawlum to ecre h&ae;lo &amp; to frofre  for&t;an&t;e wuldres cyning hine selfne geea&d;medde &t;&ae;tte he of heofonum on eor&t;an astag  &t;&ae;t he on menniscum lichoman acenned w&ae;re. 
 For&t;am se arf&ae;sta f&ae;der ne wolde l&ae;tan his gesceaft forweor&d;an &t;a&t;e he to his agenre anlicnesse gesceop  ac he hie wolde alysan of &t;&ae;re heardan h&ae;fte &amp; of deofles &t;eowdome. 
 Swa sanctus Lucas se godspellere be &t;an wrat &amp; cw&ae;&d;: In &t;a tiid w&ae;s mycel gemot gebannen fram Aguste &t;am casere &t;&ae;tte gewriten w&ae;re eall ymbhwyrft middangeardes. 
 &T;&ae;t gewrit w&ae;s &ae;rest ongunnen fram &t;&ae;re m&ae;g&d;e gerefan &t;e Syria hatte  &t;&ae;s mannes nama w&ae;s Cyrinus. 
 &amp; &t;a eodon ealle  anra gehwylc on his ceastre  &t;&ae;tte hie onfengon &t;am bebode &t;e &t;a gebannen w&ae;s &t;&ae;tte &ae;ghwylc mann sceolde &t;am casere gaful gildan. 
 &T;a ferde se eadga Ioseph fram Galilea  of &t;&ae;re ceastre &t;e Nazaret hatte  to Dauides ceastre sio is nemned Bethlem  for&t;am&t;e he w&ae;s Dauides cynnes  &t;&ae;tte hie &t;&ae;r sceoldon onfon &t;am bebode &t;&ae;s gafoles &t;e &t;a gebannen w&ae;s  mid Marian &t;e him w&ae;s to wife beweddod; &amp; &t;a w&ae;s hio bearneacenu of &t;am halgan gaste. 
 &T;a hie &t;a to Bethlem comon  w&ae;ron &t;a &d;a dagas gefylled &t;&ae;t hio bearn cennan sceolde. 
 &amp; &t;a cende hio sunu  hire frumbearn  on &t;as niht &t;e nu toniht w&ae;s  &amp; hio mid cla&d;um hine bewand &amp; on binne alegde  for&t;an &t;&ae;r ne w&ae;s o&d;eru stow on &t;am gisthuse &t;&ae;t hio &t;&ae;t cild meahte onasettan. 
 On &t;am ilcan lande on &t;a niht w&ae;ron hyrdas w&ae;ccende &amp; hioldon &t;a w&ae;ccan &t;&ae;re nihte ofer hiora ewede. 
 &T;a com him to dryhtnes engel &amp; bi him gestod &amp; hie ymbscan godcundes leohtes byrhto. 
 &T;a w&ae;ron &t;a hyrdas swi&d;e forhtiende mid mycle egsan &amp; hie him swi&d;e ondredon. 
 Se engel hie &t;a frefrede &amp; swa cw&ae;&d;: Ne forhtia&d; ge  ne eow ondr&ae;da&t;. 
 For&t;y ic eow secge mycelne gefean  se bi&d; eallum folcum gefea  for&t;an&t;e eow tod&ae;g is h&ae;lend acenned  &t;&ae;t is dryhten Crist  on Dauides ceastre. 
 &amp; ic eow secge &t;&ae;tte ge meta&d; &t;&ae;t cild on cla&d;um bewunden &amp; on binne aseted. 
 &D;a w&ae;s sona &ae;fter &t;an wordum mid &t;am engle heofonlices herges m&ae;ngeo  hergende God &amp; swa cwe&d;ende: Gloria in excelsis Deo. Wuldor sie Gode on heannesse &amp; sybb on eor&t;an &t;am mannum &t;e godes willan sien wyrcende. 
 Nu we gehyrdon on &t;yssum godspelles segenum &t;&ae;tte Agustus se casere  se w&ae;s nemned o&d;re naman Octauianus  he ricsode ofer ealne middangeard &amp; anwealda w&ae;s on eor&d;an on &t;a tiid &t;a ure h&ae;lend Crist w&ae;s acenned. 
 On &t;an w&ae;s getacnod &t;&ae;t he com on menniscum lichoman se &d;e be him sylfum &d;us cw&ae;&d;: Data est mihi omnes potestas in celo et in terra. Me is seald nu miht on heofonum &amp; on eor&d;an. 
 Eft he cw&ae;&d; be his mihte: C&ae;lum et terram ego impleo. Ic gefylle heofon &amp; eor&t;an. 
 &amp; &t;a swa w&ae;s woruldbebod gebannen fram Aguste &t;am casere swa w&ae;s eft &ae;fter &t;am Cristes bebod gastlice l&ae;ded geond ealne middangeard &t;urh his &t;a halgan apostolas &amp; &t;a godan lareowas  &t;am&t;e Godes willan wyrcea&t; &amp; Godes bebodu l&ae;ra&d;. 
 &amp; hira lar w&ae;s ges&ae;d geond ealne middangeard to eor&d;an gem&ae;rum  swa him Crist bebead &t;&ae;t hie mancynn l&ae;rden &amp; swa cw&ae;&d;: Euntes in uniuersum mundum  predicate euangelium omni creature. Ga&d; geond ealne middangeard  secga&t; lifes bebodu eallum &t;eodum. 
 Swa &t;&ae;t w&ae;s &t;&ae;t Agustus se casere he ricsode &ae;r Cristes cyme ofer ealne middangeard  swa ure h&ae;lend Crist nu hafa&d; aldordom &t;issa ondweardra rica &amp; &t;ara toweardra &amp; &t;ara &t;e &ae;r w&ae;ron  for&t;an&t;e he is ealles middangeardes cyning &amp; ece wealdend. 
 Manegu wundor gelumpon in Agustes rice. 
 &T;urh &t;a wundor w&ae;s getacnod Cristes cyme on middangeard. 
 &T;&ae;t w&ae;s sum &t;ara wundra &t;a se casere com to Rome mid sigef&ae;ste gefean &amp; mid blisse  &d;a &ae;t &t;&ae;re &d;riddan tide &t;&ae;s d&ae;ges  &t;&ae;t w&ae;s &ae;t underne  &t;a w&ae;s mannum on heofonum gesine gyldnes hringes onlicnes ymbutan &t;a sunnan. 
 &amp; on &t;am hringe w&ae;s getacnod &t;&ae;t on his rice acenned wolde bion se &ae;&d;eling se is rihtlice nemned so&d;f&ae;stnesse sunna  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne ure h&ae;lend Crist  &t;&ae;t he mid his f&ae;gernesse gewlitgode &t;a sunnan &t;e us nu d&ae;ghwamlice lyhte&d; &amp; hie gesceop &amp; mid his mihte ealne middangeard r&e;ce&d; &amp; styre&d;. 
 &T;&ae;t gelomp swa ilce &t;&ae;t se casere on his rice forgeaf ealle scylda Romwara folce. 
 &T;a w&ae;s on &t;an getacnod &t;&ae;t on his rice wolde cuman on middangeard se &d;e mancynne forgifan wolde ealle hira synna &amp; uncysta &t;urh rihtne geleafan &amp; &t;urh so&d;e hreowe. 
 Ond on &t;&ae;s caseres dagum w&ae;ron genydde to rihtum &t;eowdome &amp; to rihtre hyrnesse ealle &t;a esnas &t;e fram hira hlaforde &ae;r gewiton &amp; him hyran noldon; &amp; swa hwylce swa ne woldon hlafordas habban  &d;a w&ae;ron &t;urh rode dea&d;e gewitnode. 
 &amp; on &t;am w&ae;s &t;a getacnod &t;&ae;t &t;urh Cristes lare mancynn sceolde bion under&t;eoded anes Godes hyrnesse &amp; anes Godes willan wyrcean  &amp; swa &t;a&t;e ne willa&d; rihtum geleafan onfon  &t;a bio&d; geni&d;rade in helle tintrego. 
 &amp; in Agustes dagum wear&d; swa mycel sybb geworden on middangearde &t;&ae;t men w&ae;pn ne w&ae;gon  for&t;am&t;e he in sybbe wel gesette middangeardes rice  &amp; mid wisdomes cr&ae;fte sio sibb w&ae;s geseted geond ealne middangeard. 
 &amp; he eac sende his cempan wide geond manega m&ae;g&d;a &t;&ae;tte yfle men ne dorston nanwyht to teonan don for hyra egsan. 
 In &t;&ae;re myclan sybbe w&ae;s getacnod &t;&ae;re so&d;an sybbe cyme in middangeard  &t;&ae;t w&ae;s ure h&ae;lend Crist &t;e us gesybbode wi&d; englum &amp; us ge&t;ingode wi&d; Godf&ae;der &amp; us to sybbe gela&d;ode  &amp; he swa cw&ae;&d;: Beati pacifici quoniam filii Dei uocabuntur. Eadige bio&d; &t;a sybsuman men for&t;an&t;e hie bio&d; Godes bearn genemnde. 
 &D;rim wisum ungelice w&ae;ron mannum beboden on &t;&ae;s caseres dagum  &t;&ae;t is &t;onne &t;&ae;t &ae;ghwylc man sceolde gaful gildan  &amp; ealle men sceoldon hit gildan ge rice ge heane  &amp; &d;am gafole mon ne onfeng &ae;t &ae;negum men butan in his sw&ae;sum e&d;le. 
 On &t;an w&ae;s getacnod &t;&ae;t we sculon in &t;rim wisum Gode rihtes geleafan gaful agildan  &t;&ae;t is on wordum &amp; on ge&t;ohtum &amp; on d&ae;dum  &amp; God &t;am gafole ne onfeh&d; butan on ussum sw&ae;sum e&d;le &t;&ae;t is gehw&ae;&d;er ge in &t;am inneran ge in &t;am utteran  &t;&ae;t we mid inneweardre heortan &amp; mid ea&d;modre in God gelyfen  &amp; &t;one geleafan mid godum weorcum gefyllen &amp; mid mu&t;e ondettan. 
 Swa se eadega Paulus be &t;an cw&ae;&d;: Corde creditur ad iustitiam ore autem confessio fit in salutem. Mid heortan man sceall gelyfan &t;&ae;t he m&ae;ge to so&d;f&ae;stnesse becuman  &amp; mid mu&d;e ondettan &t;&ae;t he h&ae;bbe ece h&ae;lo. 
 Se godspellere s&ae;de &t;&ae;t middangeard w&ae;re gewriten &t;a Crist w&ae;s acenned. 
 On &t;an w&ae;s getacnod &t;&ae;t he com on menniscum lichoman se wolde his &t;ara gecorenra naman awritan in ecre gemynde eadges lifes  swa he him gehet &amp; swa cw&ae;&d;: Gaudete et exultate quia nomina uestra scripta sunt in c&e;lis. Geblissia&d; &amp; gef&ae;gnia&d; for&d;an&d;e eowre naman synt awritene on heofonum. 
 Cyrinus w&ae;s &t;&ae;s caseres gerefa in &t;&ae;re m&ae;g&d;e sio is nemned Syria. 
 &AE;rest manna he l&ae;rde &t;one casere &amp; wi&d; him ge&t;eahtode &t;&ae;t ealle men him sceoldon gafol gyldan  &amp; he sylf &ae;rest &amp; sy&d;&t;an eall folc &ae;fter &t;an. 
 In &d;am he getacnode &t;one eadegan Petrus  Cristes aldor&t;egn  for&t;am&t;e fram him com sio &ae;reste andetnes &ae;r Cristes naman  &amp; he Gode ageaf &t;&ae;s rihtan geleafan gafol in &t;am worde &t;e he to Criste cw&ae;&d;: Tu es Christus  filius Dei uiui. &d;u eart Crist  lifiendes Godes sunu. 
 Swa &t;onne nu anra gehwylc eadiglice Gode agife&d; rihtne geleafan &t;onne he andette&d; f&ae;der &amp; suna &amp; &t;am halgan gaste mid geleafan &amp; mid hyhte &amp; mid Godes lufan. 
 Us is to witonne &t;&ae;tte &t;&ae;t w&ae;s geleaflice gestihtod &t;&ae;t Ioseph for to Bethlem fram Galilea &t;&ae;tte &t;&ae;r w&ae;re Crist acenned  for&d;on&t;e Betlem is genemned Domus panis Hlafes hus. 
 Ryhtlice sio stow w&ae;s swa gelice naman genemned for&t;an&t;e on hire w&ae;s acenned lifes hlaf se &d;e middangearde lif brohte  swa he be him sylfum cw&ae;&d;: Ego sum panis uiuus qui de c&e;lo descendit. Ic eom se lifigenda hlaf se &d;e of heofonum astah. 
 So&d;lice he is se cwica hlaf for&t;an&t;e &ae;lmihtig God he gefylle&d; &t;a cl&ae;nan heortan &amp; his &t;ara gecorenra mod mid ecre wynsumnesse gastlices lifes gefean. 
 &D;a Ioseph &amp; Maria comon to Bethlem  &d;a w&ae;s sancta Maria bearneacenu  nal&ae;s fram Iosephe ac of &t;&ae;s halgan gastes gestihtum  swa se engel hire &ae;r s&ae;gde &amp; swa cw&ae;&d;: Spiritus sanctus superueniet in te. Se halga gast cyme&d; ufon on &t;e  &amp; &t;&ae;s hyhstan m&ae;gen &t;e utan ymbsyle&d; &amp; innan gefylle&d;  for&t;an &t;&ae;t of &t;e bi&d; acenned  &t;&ae;t bi&d; halig nemned  Godes bearn. 
 Ure h&ae;lend Crist is nemned in &t;am godspelle &t;&ae;t he w&ae;re sancta Marian frumbearn  nales &t;&ae;t hio &ae;nig o&d;er bearn &ae;fre h&ae;fde butan Criste  ac he is frumbearn nemned for&t;an he is fruma eallra gesceafta  ond he ealle gesceop. 
 N&ae;nig &ae;r him of helle astah  ne &ae;nig &ae;r him of dea&d;e aras  &t;&ae;t he eft ne sceolde dea&d;es byrgan. 
 &T;&ae;t mon leorna&d; on gewritum &t;&ae;t monige arison of dea&d;e  hie sceoldon hw&ae;&d;re eft dea&d;es byrgan butan Criste sylfum  &amp; eft sculon arisan &t;y nehstan d&ae;ge. 
 We gehyrdon &t;&ae;t ure h&ae;lend w&ae;s on binne aseted &t;a he w&ae;s acenned. 
 &t;&ae;t w&ae;s sio stow &t;&ae;r man nytenum hira andlifan sealde. 
 Sio binne getacnode Godes wiofod  &t;&ae;r bi&d; &t;am halgum nytenum  &t;&ae;t is &t;am geleaffulum mannum  bi&d; seald &t;&ae;t gastlice gereord Cristes lichoman  &d;e he us to wedde forlet &t;&ae;tte we sien d&ae;lnimende Godes rices  swa he sylfa be &d;am cw&ae;&d;: Qui manducat carnem meam et bibit sanguinem meum  in me manet et ego in illo. Se &d;e ete&d; minne lichoman &amp; min blod drince&d;  he wuna&t; on me &amp; ic on him. 
 &T;as word us syndon swi&d;e to ongitanne &t;&ae;tte we uncl&ae;nlice ne onfon &t;am halgan geryne Cristes lichoman &amp; his blodes  ac we sculon us sylfe &ae;r cl&ae;nsian &amp; gedafenlice libban &amp; &t;onne cl&ae;nlice &d;icgan &t;&ae;t halige geryne  &t;&ae;t God on us wunige &amp; we on him. 
 &amp; we hyrdon &ae;r on &t;am godspelle &t;&ae;t hyrdas w&ae;ron on &t;am ilcan lande w&ae;ccende  &amp; bi him stod dryhtnes engel &amp; hie ymbscan heofonlices leohtes byrhto. 
 &T;a hyrdas getacnodan &t;a godan lareowas &amp; gedefe aldoras &d;a &t;e geornlice healda&d; &t;&ae;t Cristes yrfe &t;&ae;t w&ae;ron &t;a geleaffullan menn. 
 &amp; gif hie arf&ae;stlice healda&d; &amp; wel l&ae;ra&t; &t;&ae;t geleaffulle Godes folc  &t;onne bio&d; hie rumlice onlyhte mid godcundre gife  &amp; &t;a gastlican gerynu him bio&d; leohtlicor ontyned &t;onne o&d;rum mannum  for&t;an&d;e hie mid Godes lufan healda&d; &d;a getreowan gesomnunge Godes folces. 
 &D;a hyrdas him &t;a ondredon swi&d;e  ac se engel hie frefrede &amp; swa cw&ae;&d;: Nolite timere. Ne &d;urfon ge eow ondr&ae;dan  for&d;on&t;e ic eow secge micelne gefean. 
 &E;fne swa he cw&ae;de: Micel is gefea  swa he n&ae;fre ne bi&d; geendod  ne he ne bi&d; Iudeum anum seald  ac he bi&d; eallum &t;eodum &t;am &d;e on God gelyfan willa&d;. 
 For&d;an is gedafenlic &t;&ae;t swa hwylc man swa wille gefean habban &t;&ae;t he hine &d;yde to &d;am gefean se is ece gefea  &t;e l&ae;s him gefea aspringe. 
 For &t;am gefean englas heredon dryhten &amp; swa cw&ae;don: Gloria in excelsis Deo. Wuldor sie on heanessum Gode &amp; sibb on eor&d;an &t;am mannum &t;e godne willan habba&d;. 
 &AE;r &t;&ae;t w&ae;re  &t;&ae;t ure h&ae;lend w&ae;re on menniscum lichoman acenned  &ae;r we h&ae;fdon &ae;fre wonisse &amp; unsybbe wi&d; englum  &amp; we w&ae;ron aworpene &amp; ascadene fram hiora beorhte &amp; fram hiora cl&ae;nnisse &t;urh earnunge &t;&ae;re &ae;restan scylde &amp; &t;urh o&d;re synna d&ae;ghwamlice  &amp; &ae;fter &d;am &t;e heofones cyning underhnah ures lichoman tyddernesse  ne forsawan &t;a englas usse untrumnesse ne usse tiedernesse  ac hie sona cyrdon to ure sibbe &amp; ure lufan  for&t;an&t;e hie gesawon &t;&ae;t &d;urh Cristes acennednesse heofona rices e&d;el scolde gefylled bion.

 &T;&ae;t is se goda willa &t;&ae;t man o&d;rum &t;&ae;s unne &t;e he wille &t;&ae;t him gelimpe  &amp; &t;&ae;t he on o&d;res gesyntum blissie &amp; gefeo swa he on him sylfum wille.

 We gehyrdon &t;&ae;tte ure h&ae;lend h&ae;fde mycle ea&d;modnesse.

 He w&ae;s &t;earfendlice mid cla&d;um bewunden &t;&ae;t he us of synna bendum alysde.

 Ne geuncl&ae;nsie us &ae;nig fyrenlust  ne us to Gode wrege scandlic ge&t;oht  ne ure mod gedrefan m&ae;ge grim wea  ne us &ae;fest gewundian m&ae;ge  ne oferhygd  ne gitsung eor&d;licra lusta. 
 Ac hergen we &amp; wuldrien urne dryhten on cl&ae;num ge&d;ohtum &amp; on halgum wordum &amp; on so&d;f&ae;stum weorcum &amp; on rihtum geleafan  for&t;an&t;e his miht is ufor &t;onne heofon &amp; bradre &t;onne eor&d;e &amp; deopre &t;onne s&ae; &amp; leohtre &t;onne heofones tungel. 
 Ne geneal&ae;ce&d; him n&ae;nig yldo ne sorh ne sar ne dea&d;  ne his rices bi&d; &ae;fre &ae;nig ende.

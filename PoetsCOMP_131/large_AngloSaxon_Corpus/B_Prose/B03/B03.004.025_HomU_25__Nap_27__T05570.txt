 To eallum folce. 
 Eala leofan men  understanda&d;  &t;&ae;t so&d; is  hit w&ae;s nu fela geara  &t;&ae;t on &t;isse &t;eode rixode unrihta fela  and we eac for&d;am habba&d; fela bersta and bismra gebiden  and gif we &ae;nige bote gebidan sculon  &t;onne mote we &t;&ae;s to gode earnian bet  &t;onne we &ae;r &t;isum didon. 
 For&d;am &ae;r &t;isum w&ae;s gehwar swicdom swi&d;ra  &t;onne wisdom  and &t;uhte hwilum wisost  se &t;e w&ae;s swicolast and se &t;e litelicost cu&d;e leaslice hiwian unso&d; to so&d;e and undom deman o&d;rum to hyn&d;e. 
 Ne bearh nu for oft gesib &t;am sibban &t;e ma  &t;e fremdan  ne f&ae;der his bearne ne hwilum bearn his agenum f&ae;der  ne bro&d;or o&d;rum; ne ure &ae;nig his lif ne fadode  swa swa he scolde. 
 And &t;i is &t;isse &d;eode fela hearma ons&ae;ge. 
 Ne dohte hit &ae;r &t;isum inne ne ute  ac w&ae;s here and hungor  bryne and blodgite on gewelhwilcan ende oft and gelome; and us stalu and cwalu  hol and hete and ripera reaflac derede swi&d;e &t;earle  and us unwidera for oft weoldon unw&ae;stma. 
 For&t;am on &t;isum earde w&ae;s  swa hit &t;incan m&ae;g  unrihta fela and tealte getriw&d;a &ae;ghwar mid mannum. 
 Fela syn forsworene and swi&d;e forlogene and wed eac abrocene oft and gelome; and &t;&ae;t is gesyne on &t;isse &t;eode  &t;&ae;t us godes irre hetelice on sit  gecnawe  se &t;e cunne. 
 Ac uton don  swa us &t;earf is  beon ymbe &t;a bote  geswican unrihtes and gebugan to rihte  &t;onne geearnige we  gif we &t;&ae;t willa&d;  sona godes mildse. 
 Uton helpan ure silfra &t;a hwile  &t;e we magon and motan  &t;i l&ae;s we forwur&d;an  &t;onne we l&ae;st wenan. 
 La leofan men  hw&ae;t  we ful georne witan  &t;&ae;t nu fela geara men na ne rohton for oft  hw&ae;t hi worhton. 
 Ac wear&d; &t;es &d;eodscipe swi&d;e forsingod &t;urh mor&d;d&ae;da and &t;urh mand&ae;da  &t;urh stala and &d;urh strutunga  &t;urh gitsunge and &t;urh gifernessa  &t;urh mansilena and &t;urh fela misd&ae;da  &d;urh manslihtas and &t;urh m&ae;gslihtas  &t;urh hlafordswicas and &t;urh searocr&ae;ftas  &t;urh swicdomas and &t;urh undomas  &t;urh hadbricas and &d;urh &ae;wbricas  &t;urh siblegera and &t;urh mistlice forligru. 
 And eac syn &t;urh a&d;bricas and &t;urh wedbricas forloren and forlogen ma  &t;onne scolde. 
 Ac uton geswican georne unrihtes and wi&d; god &t;ingian  swa we geornost magon: he us gefilste &t;urh his mildheortnesse  swa his willa sy  amen.

 To m&ae;sseprostum. 
 Sacerd scel on his scrifscire wislice and w&ae;rlice l&ae;dan and l&ae;ran &t;a godcunde heorde  &t;e he heald scel. 
 &AE;i&t;er he sceal ge wel bodian ge wel bysnian o&t;rum mannum  and &ae;i&t;er he scel &ae;t godes dome gescead agildan ge his sylfes d&ae;da ge ealles &t;&ae;s folces  &t;e he to godes handa healdan sceal. 
 &T;y he na m&ae;ig na wandian  gif he riht de&t;  na&t;or ne for heanum ne for ricum  &t;&ae;t he mannum &t;&ae;t rihtlicost na secge  for&t;am ne &t;et he naht  forsceame he &t;&ae;t riht to secgenne; and forsugie he hit  biterlice scel hit him wyr&t;an forgolden on &t;am toweardan life. 
 &T;eh ura hyrda hwylc an scep foriyme  we wylla&t;  &t;&ae;t he hit forgilde; hw&ae;t gefara&d; &t;onne &ae;t godes egeslican dome &t;a hyrdas  &t;e na cunnan &t;a godcundan heorde healden  &t;e hig heoldan sceoldan Criste to handa  ah hi na cunnan na&t;or &t;urh larleaste ne l&ae;dan ne l&ae;ran ne leacnian? 
 Mid hwam forgilda&t; hi hig &t;onne  l&ae;t we nu? 
 Wa heom &t;onne  &t;&ae;t hi &ae;fre underfengon &t;a godcunde heorde  &t;e hi gehealdan sceoldan. 
 Hu m&ae;ig blind man o&t;erne l&ae;dan  o&d;&d;e hu m&ae;ig ungel&ae;red mann o&t;erne l&ae;ran? 
 Wa &t;am  &t;e godcunde heorde underfeh&t; and na&t;&ae;r gehealdan ne can ne hine sylfne ne &t;a heorde  &t;e he healdan scolde to godes handa; and wyrst &t;am  &t;e can and nele.

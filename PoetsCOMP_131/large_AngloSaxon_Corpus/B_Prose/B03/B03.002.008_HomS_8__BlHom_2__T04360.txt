 Cw&ae;&t; se godspellere  mid &t;y &t;e se H&ae;lend geneal&ae;hte Gericho  &t;&ae;t leoht cyrde to &t;on blindan. 
 &T;&ae;t tacna&t; &t;&ae;t seo godcundnes onfeng ure tydran gecynde.

 Cleopian we nu in eglum mode &amp; inneweardre heortan  swa se blinda dyde  &amp; cwe&t;an  Miltsa me  Dauides sunu  miltsa me. 
 Smeagean we nu &amp; &t;encan hw&ae;t &t;&ae;t tacnode  &t;&ae;t seo menigo styrde &t;&ae;m blindan &t;&ae;t he cleopode.

 &T;a cuma&t; oft &t;urh deofles sceonessa &ae;r to manna heortan  &ae;r Drihtnes weorc &t;&ae;r wunian mote; &amp; hie beo&t; on heora mode mid mislicum ge&t;ohtum onstyrede  &t;&ae;t seo stemn &t;&ae;re heortan bi&d; swi&t;e gedrefed on &t;&ae;m gebede.

 Cw&ae;&t; se godspellere  H&ae;lend ferde &t;&ae;r for&t;  &amp; &t;a gehyrde &t;one blindan cleopian  &amp; he sona gestod  &amp; &t;&ae;t wundor worhte &t;&ae;t he &t;one blindan onlyhte.

 Drihten &ae;lmihtiga God  wyrhta &amp; wealdend ealra gesceafta  ic bidde &t;e for &t;inre micelan mildheortnesse &amp; for &t;&ae;re halegan rode tacne  &amp; for Sancta Marian m&ae;g&d;hade  &amp; for Sancte Michaeles gehyrsumnesse  &amp; for ealra &t;inra halgana lufan &amp; heora earnungum  &t;&ae;t &t;u me gewissige bet &t;onne ic awyrhte to &t;e. 
 &amp; gewissa me to &t;inum willan &amp; to minre sawle &t;earfe bet &t;onne ic sylf cunne &amp; gesta&t;ela min mod to &t;inum willan &amp; to minre sawle &t;earfe. 
 &amp; gestranga me wi&d; &t;&ae;s deofles costnungum &amp; afyrra fram me &t;a fulan galnysse &amp; &ae;lce unrihtwisnysse &amp; gescylde me wi&d; minum wi&d;erwinnum gesewenlicum &amp; ungesewenlicum. 
 &amp; t&ae;c me &t;inne willan to wyrcenne  &t;&ae;t ic m&ae;ge &t;e inweardlice lufian toforon eallum &t;ingum mid cl&ae;num ge&t;ance &amp; mid cl&ae;num lichaman for&t;on &t;e &t;u eart min sceoppend  &amp; min alesend  min fultum  min frofer  min trewnes  &amp; min tohopa si &t;e lof &amp; wylder nu &amp; a a a to worulde buton &ae;ghwilcum ende  Amen.

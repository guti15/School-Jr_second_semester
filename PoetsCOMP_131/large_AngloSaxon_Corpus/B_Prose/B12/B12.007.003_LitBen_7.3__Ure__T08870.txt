 De officio tertio hore.. 
 On undern we sculon God herian for&d;am on underntiman Crist w&ae;s &d;urh &t;ara Iudea dom to dea&d;e fordemed and toweard &t;&ae;re rode gel&ae;d  &t;e he sy&d;&d;an on &t;rowode for ealles middaneardes alysednysse. 
 And eft &ae;fter his &ae;riste on Pentecostenes d&ae;g com se halga gast on underntiman ofer &d;a apostolas &t;&ae;r hi &ae;tg&ae;dere gesamnode w&ae;ron  and hi ealle sona gefyllede wurdon swa swy&d;e mid Godes gyfe &t;&ae;t hi ealra gereorda getingnesse h&ae;fdon  and heora lar wear&d; geond ealne middaneard sy&d;&d;an gecy&d;ed and ged&ae;led &t;eodum to helpe. 
 Undern is d&ae;ges &d;ridde tid  &t;onne is eac rihtlic &t;&ae;t we to &t;&ae;re &t;riddan tide &t;a halgan &d;rynnesse geornlice herian.

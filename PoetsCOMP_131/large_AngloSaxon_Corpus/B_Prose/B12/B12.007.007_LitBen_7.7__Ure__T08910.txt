 De completorio.. 
 On foranniht we sculon God herian &ae;r we to bedde gan and gemunan &t;&ae;t Crist on byrgene neah forannihte bebyrged wear&d; and &d;&ae;r his lichaman on gereste swa lange swa his willa w&ae;s. 
 &T;onne age we &t;&ae;s micle &t;earfe &t;&ae;t we &t;&ae;t ge&t;encan and us sylfe on &d;one timan Gode bet&ae;can &ae;r we to bedde gan and hine biddan &t;&ae;t he us gedefre reste geunne and wi&d; deofles costnunga gescylde swa his wylla sy.

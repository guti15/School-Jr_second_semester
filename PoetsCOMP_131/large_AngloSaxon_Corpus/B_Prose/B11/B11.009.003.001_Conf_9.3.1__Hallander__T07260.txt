 Myn drihten god &ae;lmihtig  ic &t;e eom andetta minra synna &t;ara &t;e ic on minre gemeleste wi&t; &t;e geworhte. 
 Min dryhten god &ae;lmihtig  ic &t;e eom andetta bote for mines lichoman unsyfernessum &t;ara &t;e ic d&ae;ges &amp; nihtes worhte  ic eom gewita mor&t;res &amp; ic eom wedloga &amp; manra a&t;&ae; gewita  ic hit &t;e min god andette for ealle mine d&ae;da ge godes ge yfles. 
 Min drihten god &ae;lmihtig  ic eom oferfangen mid synnum to wyrmlice for&t;on &t;e ic forgemeleasade &t;ine beboda. 
 Ic eom andetta bote for ealles mines lichoman gecynde &amp; for ealle &t;a w&ae;stmas &d;e minan synfullan lichoman gesette synd. 
 Nu ic &t;e bidde  min drihten  for ealra &t;inra haligra lufan  &t;eah ic &t;e &t;us synful to cige for minum nyd&t;earfum &t;&ae;t &t;u geh&ae;le mine sawle for minum synnum. 
 Min drihten god  for ealra &t;inra apostola weor&d;unge ic &t;e halsige &t;&ae;t &t;u me ne forl&ae;te on &t;&ae;m egesfullan domesd&ae;ge  &t;eah ic awacode. 
 Min drihten god  for minre tyddernesse &t;eah ic &t;ine bebodu ne heolde. 
 Nu ic &t;e &t;eah halsige  min drihten god  for &t;&ae;re &t;rowunge &t;e &t;u &t;rowodest for ealra manna h&ae;lo  &amp; ic &t;e halsige  &ae;lmihtig god  for &t;inum wuldorlican wuldre  h&ae;le me for so&t;f&ae;stra sawla onfenge. 
 Min drihten god  for &t;on &t;u w&ae;re in byrgenne geset  ne l&ae;t me belucan on synfulra monna &e;ardungstowe. 
 Nu ic minum gewyrhtum &t;us wace truwige for minum synfullum d&ae;dum. 
 Min drihten god &ae;lmihtig forgif me minra synna liss&ae; for minre nyd&t;earfe. 
 Min drihten god &ae;lmihtig for&d;on &t;u w&ae;re eadig on &d;inre modor inno&t;e ne forl&ae;t me. 
 Min sanctus Michael archangelus beo &t;u me &t;ingere to &t;&ae;m heofenlican scyppende to &ae;lmihtigum gode for minum m&ae;nigfealdum synnum. 
 Min drihten god  sele me gedefe hreowe &amp; so&t;e andetnesse ealra minra synna &t;ara &d;e ic on minre gemeleste gedyde wi&t; weras o&t;&t;e wi&d; wif o&t;&t;e on diglum me mid synfyllum d&ae;dum gefylde. 
 Min drihten god  ne l&ae;t me synfulne man deoflum gegan &t;eah hi me &t;us synlice costedon for&d;on &t;u on syx dagum gescope heofonas &amp; eor&t;an &amp; ealle weoroldgesceafta. 
 Min drihten god  for ealr&ae; &t;&ae;re gesceafte &t;e &t;u on syx dagum gesceapen h&ae;fdest &amp; &t;a &t;u gerestes on &t;&ae;m seofo&d;an d&ae;ge  Min drihten god  for &t;inan restingd&ae;ge geh&ae;l mine sawle fram deofles anwealde of &t;em synfullan ham for&t;on &d;&ae;r bi&d; sorhful wop. 
 Min drihten god  ne l&ae;t me aslidon on &t;a synfullan eardungstowe &t;eah mine gewyrhta &t;us wace syn for minum gemelystum. 
 Nu ic &t;e bidde  min drihten  &t;&ae;t &t;u me selle god ingehyd &amp; gemynd &t;&ae;t ic gelyfe on &d;e &amp; on &d;a ealle &t;e for &t;inum naman &t;rowodon. 
 Min drihten  ic &t;e bidde for ealra &t;inra apostola arwyr&t;nesse &t;&ae;t &d;u me ne sceade of &t;&ae;m so&t;f&ae;stan &d;e on &d;e gelyfa&t; on f&ae;der &amp; on suna &amp; on &t;&ae;ne halgan gast. 
 Nu ic halsige &t;a heofanlican f&ae;mnan Sanctam Marian  swylce ic Michahel &amp; Gabriel &amp; Raphael  Iohannes  &amp; Petrus  &amp; Paulus  &amp; Andreas  Iohannes &amp; Iacob  Matheus &amp; Iudas  Philippus &amp; Bartholomeus  Thomas &amp; Iacob  Symon &amp; Taddeus. 
 Nu ic halsige &t;a cristes &t;egnas for hiora ealdorlican setle &t;&ae;t ge me synfulne man ne sceadon on &t;a earmfullan &t;ystra for minum yflum weorcum. 
 Per te Ihesu Xriste saluator mundi qui in trinitate perfecta uiuis et regnas in secula seculorum  Amen.

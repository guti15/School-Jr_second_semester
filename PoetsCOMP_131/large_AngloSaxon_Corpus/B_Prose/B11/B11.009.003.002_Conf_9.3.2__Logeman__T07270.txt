 Confessio et Oratio.  Eala &t;u &ae;lmihtiga god unasecgendlicere mildheortnesse  eala &t;u god unametenre arf&ae;stnysse  eala &t;u god scyppend &amp; geedsta&t;eliend ealles manncynnes  &t;u &t;e afeormast fram fulum synnum &t;&ae;ra heortan &t;e &t;e andetta&d; &amp; &d;a &t;e hi sylfe wrega&d; &ae;tforan gesih&d;e &t;inre mildheortnysse &t;a &t;u alyst fram bende &ae;lcere unrihtwisnysse &t;ine mihte. 
 Ic bidde mid ealre geomerunge &t;&ae;t &t;u &d;urh menigfealdnysse &t;inra mildheortnyssa me forgife &t;&ae;t ic hluttre andetnysse &ae;tforan &t;e don mote be eallum minum synnum &t;e min ingehyd me nu wreg&d;. 
 &amp; ic so&d;e d&ae;dbote &ae;tforan &t;e don mote be eallum &t;am synnum &t;e ic gesyngode on yfelum ge&t;ohtum &amp; on &t;wyrum smeageongum  on yfelre ge&t;afunge &amp; on unrihtwisum ge&t;eahte  on uncl&ae;nre lust fullunge &amp; on fracodre gewilnunge  on ydelum wordum &amp; on yfelum d&ae;dum. 
 Ic syngode on gesih&d;e &amp; swi&d;e on hlyste  eac on sw&ae;cce  on stence &amp; on hrewunge. 
 &T;u leofa drihten ealle lima me gesceope to menniscum bricum gem&ae;te &amp; gelimplice ac ic earming hi awende to deoflicum weorcum &amp; mid synnum hi befylde &amp; &t;e forseah drihten. 
 Ic on ofermettum oft swi&d;e agylte &amp; &t;urh upahefednysse me toforan o&t;rum tealde &amp; &t;ine beboda drihten dyde me unwur&d;e. 
 &amp; ic nolde wegan &t;in wynsume geocne  &d;ine leohtan byr&t;ene on minum b&ae;ce ferian &amp; ic unw&ae;rlice syngode oft &t;urh modignysse. 
 Ic on gyfernysse &amp; on oferfylle &amp; on druncennysse wi&d; &t;e drihten agylte oft &amp; gelome on untiman. 
 Ic eac earming ofer &ae;lcum gemete on fulum forligre &amp; on fracodre galnysse me sylfne befylde ge on sawle ge on lichaman. 
 Ic on gitsunge mid gitsigendum mode swi&d;e oft agylte &amp; beswac o&d;re &ae;t heora &ae;htum &t;urh &t;a unrihtwisan gitsunge &amp; ic unriht oft spr&ae;c arleaslice &amp; riht forsuwode &t;urh &t;one sylfan leahter. 
 Ic &t;urh weamodnysse worhte fela yfela &amp; &t;urh manslihtas me scyldigne dyde wi&d; &t;e min h&ae;lend &t;a &t;a ic &t;in hand geweorc unwyrcan dorste &amp; dea&d;e bet&ae;can. 
 Nu synd mine handa &t;urh &t;one hefian gylt mid manna blodum &t;e ic &t;urh gebeot oft &amp; &t;urh hatheortnysse her on life ageat yfele befylede &amp; f&ae;ste gebundene swarum gyltum &t;urh &t;a sylfan weamodnysse. 
 Ic on unrotnysse oft eac agylte &amp; swi&d;or ceorude &t;onne min sawul behofode &t;a &d;a ic &ae;hta forleas o&d;&d;e leofne freond o&d;&d;e me hw&ae;t mislamp on &t;ises lifes ryne. 
 &amp; ic &t;a ongean &t;e drihten dyrstiglice ceorode &t;urh &t;a unrotnysse &t;e is dea&d;es wyrcend. 
 In syngode gelome &t;urh asolcennysse &t;a &t;a me god ne lyste don ne gan to godes huse ne nan ellen niman to &ae;nigum godan weorce. 
 Ac ic leofode min lif lange on asolcennysse butan godum weorcum &amp; godum biggenge. 
 Ic on idelum wuldre eac swylce agylte on gylp licere spr&ae;ce &amp; gylpes cewte &amp; wolde beon gehered &t;eah &d;e ic herigendlic n&ae;re. 
 &amp; on wlence ic ferde &t;urh &t;&ae;t idele wuldor &amp; manna lyffetunge ic lufode to swi&d;e &amp; on menigfealdre glencge ic glencgde minne lichaman &amp; mid sweartum synnum mine sawle awl&ae;tte &amp; wolde beon wi&d;utan swa&t;eah wur&d;lic ge&t;uht. 
 On eallum &t;isum heafodleahtrum ic healice agylte &amp; o&d;rum l&ae;ssum gyltum ic gelome syngode &amp; ic ea&d;e ne m&ae;g mine gyltas atellan. 
 Ic bidde swa &t;eah drihten &t;&ae;t hi beon &t;e geandette mid so&d;re behreowsunge &amp; &t;u mine sawle geh&ae;le fram eallum synnum  &t;u &d;e eart sawla alysend. 
 &D;u sealdest us h&ae;lend healicne truwan &t;urh ezechiel cwe&t;ende &t;inne so&d;an witegan  gif se arleasa de&d; so&d;&ae; d&ae;dbote be eallum his synnum &t;e he &ae;r geworhte &amp; ealle mine beboda gehylt &amp; de&d; rihtwisnysse &amp; dem&d; rihtlice he leofa&d; his life &amp; he nane swylt. 
 &amp; ic ne gemune ealra his mand&ae;da ac he leofa&d; on his rihtwisnysse &t;e hesylf geworhte. 
 Eac &t;u sylf drihten s&ae;dest on &t;inum godspelle &t;&ae;t mare bliss bi&d; on heofonum be anum synfullan men gif he gecyr&d; to &t;e &t;onne be nigon &amp; hundnigantigan &t;e nanre d&ae;dbote ne behofia&d;. 
 Underfoh me nu drihten for &t;inre mildheortnysse &amp; a&t;weah me &t;urh &t;&ae;s halgan gastes gife fram eallum minum synnum &amp; sele me sta&d;olf&ae;ste heortan &t;&ae;t ic &ae;lc yfel onscunige &amp; &ae;lce unrihtwisnysse &amp; ic &t;ine bebodu lufige &amp; &t;e leofa  drihten. 
 Nel &ae;t &t;u me &ae;fre bugan to &t;am &ae;rran leahtrum &t;e ic receleas gefremode fullice for oft ac geedsta&d;ela on me &t;u &ae;rf&ae;stosta h&ae;lend swa hw&ae;t swa ic amyrde &t;urh manfulle d&ae;da. 
 Gescylde me  drihten  wi&d; &t;one swicolan deofol &t;&ae;t he me earmne oft ne beswice for&t;an &t;e min tyddernys ne m&ae;g him wi&d; standan butan &t;u leofa h&ae;lend on lihte mine heortan &amp; mid &t;inre mihtigan handa me gehealde wi&d; hine. 
 &D;u miht eall &t;&ae;t &t;u wylt swa swa &ae;lmihtig god &amp; &t;u naht ne swincst &t;eah &t;u gescylde us heald me for &t;ig leofa to lofe &t;inum naman &t;&ae;t ic on godum weorcum wunian mote o&d; mines lifes ende mid so&d;um geleafan. 
 Ic bidde &t;e nu  h&ae;lend  &t;&ae;t &t;u geh&ae;le mine sawle &t;&ae;t ic d&ae;lnimend beo on &t;am forman &ae;riste swa &t;&ae;t min sawul of synnum nu arise &t;&ae;t heo &t;e orsorhre on &t;am o&t;rum &ae;riste lichamlice arise to &t;am ecan life. 
 Syle me nu scyppend ges&ae;lig ge&t;anc &amp; &t;a halgan mihte &t;e men &t;urh ge&t;eo&d; geleafan &amp; hiht &amp; halige lufe  modes snoternysse &amp; mihtig ge&t;yld  gemetegunge &amp; anr&ae;dnysse  rihtwisnysse &amp; mildheortnysse  cl&ae;nnysse &amp; cystignysse  sibbe &amp; arf&ae;stnysse  so&d;f&ae;stnysse &amp; welwillendnysse wuldres  drihten. 
 &amp; syle me forgifennysse ealra minra synna &amp; &t;&ae;t ece lif &ae;fter &t;isum l&ae;nan life. 
 H&ae;lend crist  gehyr &t;as word &t;u &d;e leofast &amp; rixast mid &t;am &ae;lmihtigan f&ae;der &amp; &t;am halgan gaste butan anginne &amp; ende  Amen.

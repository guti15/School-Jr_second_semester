 Ne cw&ae;&t; he na  hwi ehtst &t;u minra manna  ac hwi ehtst &t;u min  for &t;am &t;e he gefredde hys halgena sarnyssa. 
 Eft he cw&ae;&t;: esuriui enim  et dedistis mihi manducare: me hingrode  and ge sealdon me etan. 
 Him hingra&t; on hys &t;earfum  and swa hw&ae;t swa we do&t; godes &t;earfum on godes naman  &t;&ae;t we do&d; gode sylfum. 
 Eac &t;onne we ymbe god smeaga&d;  and to hym hopia&d;  we beo&d; mid him  swa swa paulus cw&ae;&t;: ure drohtnung is on heofonum. 
 Eft cw&ae;&t; ure drihten be hys gecorenum: gaudete  quia nomina uestra scripta sunt in caelo; &d;&ae;t is on englisc: blissia&d;  for &t;am &t;e eowre naman synd awritene on heofonum. 
 Eft he cw&ae;&t;: non delebo nomina eorum de libro uit&ae;: ic ne adiligie heora naman of lifes bec.

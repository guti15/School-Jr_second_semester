 To Eallum Folke.

 &amp; &d;in ricedom ofer us rixie symble  &amp; &d;in willa gewyr&d;e swa swa on heofonum eac swa on eor&d;an. 
 Geunn us to &t;issum d&ae;ge d&ae;ghwamlices fostres. 
 And us gemildsa swa swa we miltsia&d; &t;am &d;e wi&d; us agylta&t;; &amp; ne l&ae;t &d;u us costnian ealles to swy&d;e  ac alys us fram yfele  amen. 
 Gloria patri et filio et spiritui sancto. 
 A sy lof &amp; wuldor f&ae;der &amp; suna &amp; halgum gaste  swa swa on fruman w&ae;s &amp; gyt is &amp; symble bi&d; in ealra worulda woruld  amen. 
 Leofan men  utan don swa us mycel &t;earf is  habban anr&ae;dne geleafan &amp; fulne hiht on urne Drihten. 
 And se &d;e &t;urh Ledenspr&ae;ce rihtne geleafan understandan ne cunne  geleornige huru on Englisc &amp; cwe&d;e &t;us gelome: Credimus in unum Deum  patrem et filium et spiritum sanctum. 
 We gelyfa&d; on &ae;nne God &ae;lmihtigne &t;e ealle &t;ingc gesceop &amp; geworhte. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &amp; georne witan &t;&ae;t Crist Godes sunu to mannum com for ealles mancynnes &d;earfe. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t hine cl&ae;ne m&ae;den geb&ae;re  Sancta Maria  &t;e n&ae;fre nahte weres gemanan. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t he mycel ge&d;olode &amp; sti&d;lice &t;rowode for ure ealra neode. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t hine man on rode ahenge &amp; hine to dea&d;e acwealde &amp; hine sy&d;&d;an on eor&d;an bebyrigde. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t he to helle ferde &amp; &d;&ae;rof gehergode eal &t;&ae;t he wolde. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t he sy&d;&d;an of dea&d;e arise. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t he &ae;fter &t;am to heofonum astige. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &amp; georne witan &t;&ae;t he on domes d&ae;g to &d;am miclan dome cym&d;  &amp; we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t ealle deade men sculon &t;onne arisan of dea&d;e &amp; &t;one myclan dom ealle gesecan. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t &d;a synfullan sculon &t;anon on an to helle faran &amp; &d;&ae;r a sy&d;&d;an mid deoflum wunian on byrnendum fyre &amp; on ecan forwyrde  &amp; &d;&ae;s &ae;nig ende ne cym&d; &ae;fre to worulde. 
 And we gelyfa&d; &t;&ae;t &d;a godan &amp; wel cristenan &t;e her on worulde Gode wel gecwemdon &t;onne on an sculon into heofonum faran &amp; &d;&ae;r sy&d;&d;an wununge habban mid Gode sylfum &amp; mid his englum a on ecnesse  amen. 
 &AE;lc Cristen man ah mycle &t;earfe &t;&ae;t he &d;is wite &amp; &d;isses rihtlice gelyfe; &amp; se &d;e his credan elles ne cunne  geleornige huru &t;&ae;t he hine &d;us cunne. 
 And utan don swa us mycel &t;earf is  habban &ae;fre rihtne geleafan &amp; fulne hiht on God &ae;lmihtigne &amp; lufian hine ofer ealle o&d;re &t;ing &amp; his willan a wyrcan swa we geornost magan. 
 &T;onne geleana&d; he hit us swa us leofost by&d;.

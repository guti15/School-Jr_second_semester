 Eadward cyng gret &ae;igelmer biscop &amp; Geor&d; eorl &amp; ealle mine &t;egenas on &ae;stengle freondlice. 
 &amp; ic cy&d;e eou &t;&ae;t ic habbe geunnen Baldwine abbote onne menetere wi&d; inne Seint Eadmundes byrig al swa freolice on ealle &t;ing to habben al swa me mine on hande stonden ower on enig minre burge alre freolukeost. 
 God seo eow alre freond.

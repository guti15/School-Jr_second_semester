 Willem king gret Aylmer biscop &amp; Rauf erl &amp; ealle mine theines on Northfolc &amp; on Suffolc frendlike. 
 And ic kythe ihu that ic habbe unnen Baldewyne abbot withinnen Saint Edmundesbiri to thene ministres beofte ane menetere also frelike on alle thinge to habben also Edward king mine mey him hunnede alder frelikest. 
 God be mid ihu.

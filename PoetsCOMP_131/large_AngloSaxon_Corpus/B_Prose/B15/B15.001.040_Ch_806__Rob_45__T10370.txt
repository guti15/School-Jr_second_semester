 Eadgares cyninges freolsboc to Tantune. 
 Her ys geswutelod on &t;ysum gewrite hu Eadgar cyning mid ge&t;eahte his witena geniwode Tantunes freols &t;&ae;re halgan &t;rynnesse &amp; sancte Petre &amp; sancte Paule into Wintanceastre to &t;am biscopstole ealswa Eadweard cyning hit &ae;r gefreode &amp; geu&t;e &t;&ae;t &ae;g&t;er ge twelfhynde men ge twyhynde weron on &t;am Godes hame &t;ara ylcan gerihta wyr&t;e &t;e his agene men sindon on his agenum cynehamum. 
 &amp; man ealle sp&ae;ca &amp; gerihtu on &t;&ae;t ylce gemet gefe to Godes handa &t;e man to his agenre drifh. 
 &amp; &t;es tunes ciyping &amp; seo innung &t;ara port gerihta gange into &t;ere halgan stowe ealswa heo &ae;r dyde on myra vldrena dagon &amp; &ae;lfeage biscope gehafod w&ae;s &amp; gewylcvm &t;ara &t;e &t;es landes breac. 
 Se &t;e &t;isne freols geycean wille geyce God his gesynta to langsumun life her &amp; on ecnesse. 
 Gif hwa &t;onne &t;urh gedyrstignesse &amp; deofles o&t;&t;e his lima lare &t;ysne freols abrecan wille o&t;&t;e on o&t;er awendan buton he hit &ae;r his fordsi&t;e gebete sy he mid awurgednesse ascyred fram ures Drithnes gemanan &amp; ealra his halgena &amp; on helle susle ecelice getintragod mid Iudan &t;e Cristes lewa wes. 
 &D;onne gesealde A&d;elwold biscop his cynehlaforde twa und mancussa goldes &amp; anne sylfrene lefel on fif pundum wi&t;e niwunge &t;yses freolses &amp; &ae;lf&t;ryde his gebeddan healf hund mancussa goldes wi&t; richtes &ae;rendes fultume. 
 &amp; &ae;r w&ae;non wi&t; freolse gesealt on Eadwardes dege cinges syxtig hide landes  X &ae;t Crawancumbe &amp; X &ae;t Cumbtune &amp; &ae;t Bananwille XX &amp; XX &ae;t Scealdeburnan stoce. 
 &amp; eft Eadward cyning gesealde &t;&ae;t land &ae;t Cumbtune &amp; &ae;t Bananwille &t;an hiwon &ae;t Ceodre wi&t; &t;an lande &ae;t Carintune. 
 &T;is wes gedon &ae;t Ceodre on &t;ere halgan eastertide. 
 &T;y geare weron agangene DCCCC geara &amp; eahta &amp; hundseofontig fram Cristes acynnednesse &amp; &t;e teo&t;an geare his cynelican anwealdes on gewitnesse &t;ara witena &t;e hiora noman herwi&t; neo&t;an awritene syndon.

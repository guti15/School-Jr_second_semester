 &T;at witen alle &t;at ever been  &t;at &t;is charter heren and seen. 
 &T;at I &t;e king Adelstan Has yaten and given to seint John Of Beverlike &t;at sai I yow  Tol and theam &t;at wit ye now  Sok and sake over al &t;at land &t;at es given into his hand  On ever ilke kinges dai  Be it all free &t;an and ay  Be it almousend  be all free Wit ilke man and eeke wit mee. 
 &T;at wil i be him &t;at me scop Bot til an ercebiscop  And til &t;e seven minstre prestes &t;at serves God ther saint John restes. 
 &T;at give i God and seint John  Her befor you ever ilkan. 
 All my herst corn ineldeel To uphald his ministre weell. 
 &T;a four threve be heven kinge Of ilka plough of estriding. 
 If it swa betid  or swa gaas  &t;at ani man her again taas  Be he baron  be he erle  Clark  prest  parson  or cherel. 
 Na be he na &t;at ilk Gome  I will forsaye &t;at he come &t;at wit ye weel or and or Til saint John mynstre dor. 
 And &t;ar i will swo Crist me red &t;at he bet his misded  Or he be cursed son on on Wit al &t;at servis saint John. 
 Yif hit swa betid and swa es  &t;at &t;e man in mansing es  I sai yow over sourti daghes Swilke &t;an be sain John laghes &t;at &t;e chapitel of Beverlike Til &t;e scirif of Everwike Send &t;air writ son onan  &t;at &t;is mansedman betan. 
 &T;e scirref &t;an say i &t;e  Witouten any writ one me Sal minen him swo Crist me red And into my prison lede  And hald him &t;at is mi wilt Til he bet his misgilt. 
 If men reises newe laghes In any o&t;er kinges daghes  Be &t;ay fromed  be &t;ay yemed Wit &t;ham of mynstre demed  &t;e mercy of &t;e misdeed  Gif i saint John  swo Crist me red  Yif man be cald of limes or lif  Or men chalenges land in strif Wit my bodlaik  wit writ of right  Y wil saint John have &t;e might. 
 &T;at man &t;ar for noght fight in feeld  Now &t;er wit staf no wit sheeld  Bot twelve men wil i &t;at it telle Swo sal it be swo heer ibelle. 
 And he &t;at him swo werne may Overcomen be he ever and ay. 
 Als he in feld war overcomen  &t;e cravantise of him be nomen. 
 &T;at &t;at i God and saint John Her befor iow ever ilkon. 
 If man be founden slan idrunkend  Sterved on sain John rike  His aghen men withouten swike His aghen bailiffs make &t;e fight  Nan o&t;er coroner have &t;e might  Swa mikel fredom give i ye  Swa hert may think or eghe see. 
 &T;at have I thought and forbiseen I will &t;at &t;er ever been Samening and mynstre lif Last follike witouten strif  God help alle thas ilk men &t;at helpes to &t;e thowen  Amen.

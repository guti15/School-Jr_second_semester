 Willelm kyng gret mine eorlas and ealle mine &t;egnas on &t;an sciran &t;&ae;r Ealdred arcebiscop h&ae;f&t; land ofer and land inne freondlice. 
 And ic ky&t;e eow &t;&ae;t ic wille he beo his biscoprices weor&t;e and his socne tolles and teames binnan burh et butan ofer his men and ofer his manna land on mine socne swa full and swa for&t; swa he firmest h&ae;fde on Eadwerdes d&ae;ge kinges mines m&ae;ges on eallan &t;ingan. 
 And ic nelle nanan men ge&t;olian &t;at ynn fram hande drafe &ae;nig &t;&ae;ra &t;inga &t;&ae;s gehennd rihte habbene ah ne &t;at man him &ae;t &ae;nigan &t;ingan misbeode nenan his manna. 
 And gif hit &ae;nig man de&d; frencisc o&d;&d;e flemisc o&d;&d;e englisc geky&t;e me fore and ic him c&ae;de sone fulle bote.

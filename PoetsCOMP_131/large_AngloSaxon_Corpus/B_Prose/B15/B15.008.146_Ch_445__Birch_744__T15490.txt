 &T;is sent &t;a landimare to archet &t;are westere fif hide. 
 Arest suthward of ealdmannes wyrde at langhecgan to sugging made. 
 &T;ane on i richte to stirthel on &t;ane alr bene&t;en scealden for&d; &amp; &t;anen anlang higweges &tbar; of higeweges ande funtemel &t;ry akeres wid eastem &t;ane broc &t;onne anlang funtmeales o&t; eadelmes melne of funtemel to &t;ane twam &t;ornen &t;at for&d; on irichte to stigel hege ouer stirtel &amp; &t;onun on irichte to ginum hocum to &t;rem land sharen &t;at on archet hamm. 
 And &t;anen cagbroc up anlang cagbroges to land share hegen &t;onne anlang heges to ealdmannes wyr&t;e.

 Her is on &t;is write Keteles quide. 
 &T;at is &t;at ic an Stistede after mine tyme for mine fader soule and for Sefledan into Cristes kirke. 
 And ic wille &t;at mine men ben alle fre &amp; Mann myne refe &t;at he sitte on &t;e fre lond &t;at ic him to honde habbe leten his time euer fre and after his time folege &t;at lond &t;en o&t;ere. 
 And ic an into &t;ere kirke &t;at lond &t;at Withrich hauede vnder hande and Lewine and Siric and Goding so so geard go&d; to Leueriches hyge and &t;at no mam him ne forwerne &t;an vtgang. 
 And ic wille &t;at alle &t;o men &t;e ic an fre &t;at he habben alle &t;inge &t;e he vnder hande habben buten &t;at lond. 
 And ic an &t;at lond at Herlinge Stigand Archebisscop mine louerd so it stant buten &t;o men ben alle fre and ten acres ic an into &t;e kirke. 
 And gif ic ongein ne cume &t;an an ic him to min heregete an helm and a brenie and hors and gereade and sverd and spere. 
 And ic wille after &t;e forwarde &t;at ic and Eadwine and Wlfric after mine time fon to alkere &t;inge &t;e min ower is &t;er on tune butan so mikel so ic an into &t;e kirke &t;at is &t;at erninglond &t;at Alfwold mine man haue&d; vnder hande and he sitte on &t;at other his time. 
 And sithen al &t;at lond &t;at him to honde bege&d; folege mid &t;e o&t;ere into &t;e kirke. 
 Gif Eadwine min em wille helden se felageschipe mid me &amp; Wlfric min em ymbe &t;at lond at Me&t;eltune gif wit him ouerbiden fon we to &t;at londe at Thorpe into &t;at forwarde &t;at vre bo&t;ere time go &t;at lond at Metheltone for vre heldren soule and vre awene soule into seinte Benedicte at Holm and &t;at lond at Thorpe into seynt Eadmundes biri. 
 And &t;at is min and mine sustres Boten  &t;at forwarde  gif ic mine day do her his &t;at ic fon to &t;at lond at Keteringham and an marc goldes o&t;er &t;e wrth and gif ic hire ouerbide &t;anne schal ic habben &t;at lond at Somerledetone. 
 And &t;at ilke forwarde ic and Gode mine suster habbed speken  gif he me ouerbide gripe he to &t;at lond at Walsingham buten ten acres &t;o schulen into &t;ere kirke and gif ic libbe leng &t;anne hio &t;anne schal ic habben &t;at lond at Prestone. 
 And ic an Godric mine brother &t;at lond at Hemfordham so it me on hande stand and Kockeshale. 
 And he scal for &t;at lond at Strattune gifen Alwy mine cnihte to pund. 
 And ic and Algif mine stepdouhter wit habben wrouth &t;e forwarde ymbe &t;at lond at Anhus &t;at so wether vnker so lengere libbe habbe &t;at lond so mikel so wit &t;er habben. 
 And gif vnc ban fordsith sceot on Rome weye &t;anne go &t;at lond for me and for Sefle&d; and for Algiue into seint Eadmundes biri bute &t;o men ben alle fre. 
 And ic an Harold erl after mine time &t;e halue lond at Moran so ful and so forth so ic it richtlike on Godes witnesse and mani manne mid mine wife bigat. 
 And ic it sithen nawer ne forswat ne forspilde. 
 And ic bidde &t;e &t;urh &t;ene Drichtin se &t;e and alle schefte schop gif ic ongen ne cume &t;at &t;u it nefre ne let welden mine vnwinan after me &t;e mid unrichte sitte&d; &t;eron and nitta&d; it me euere to vn&t;anke. 
 And ic an &t;e lond at Fretinge after &t;at ilke forwarde &t;at &t;u &t;e self and Stigand Archebiscop mine louerd wrouhten. 
 And ic an Alfric mine prest and mine cuthen &t;at lond at Rissewrthe. 
 And gif ani man si so disi &t;at wille mine quide bereuen  God him fordo on domesday and alle his halegan.

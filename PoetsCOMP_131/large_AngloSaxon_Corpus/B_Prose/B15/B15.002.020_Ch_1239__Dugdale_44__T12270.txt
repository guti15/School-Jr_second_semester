 In nomine Domini. Ic Lulle &amp; ecke &t;a hisetnesse &t;am hiwm to sancte Augustine for hi &amp; for hire bearn libbinde  &amp; for hire freond &amp; for alle &t;a &t;e hi for&t;ingie. 
 &T;an is &t;is seo isetnesse of Natindone XXX hambre clenes meltes  &amp; twa fodre wodes  &amp; iiii wetheres  &amp; a weimel cheses  &amp; a sester hingges &amp; V gys  &amp; X henfugeles  &amp; l C hwetene sura lhafa  &amp; XX clanra hlafa. 
 &D;onne is &t;is &t;ara gode lean &t;&ae;t &t;a hiwen don scealon  &t;&ae;t hi agan ealle to cyrican &amp; gesingan hore psaltere sealme elce dage &t;e hia &t;a god &t;icgen &amp; &ae;lce messeprest ane messe  and &ae;lce dage hire pater noster &amp; se &t;e &t;is heade god hine healde.

 Eadward kyng gret Aylmer bisscop &amp; Aelfger erl and Toly and alle mine &t;eynes on Suffolke and Norfolke frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ou &t;at ic wille &t;at &t;at lond at Cunegestone lige into Seynt Eadmundes minstre and alle &t;e &t;inge &t;at &t;er to mid rithte bire&d; mid sake and mid sokne so ful and so forth so ihe alle for Gode witen &t;at it mid rithte in bure&d;.

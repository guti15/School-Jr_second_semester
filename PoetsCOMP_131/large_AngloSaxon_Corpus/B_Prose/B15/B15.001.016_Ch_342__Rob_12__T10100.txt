 Regnante imperpetuum domino nostro Ihesu Christo. 
 Rixiende ure Dritte Halende Crist  ich Atheldred mid Godes giue Westsaxne king mid leue and e&t;eafunghe mine &d;are seleste wiotene  ich forgiue and selle for me selfne minre saule to alesnesse minne &d;am leueste and itreweste alderman Elfstane Alchene idal landes in &t;are istowe &t;e is inemned be Chiselburne fif hide him to habenne and to brukende on elche halue. 
 &T;at is &t;anne &t;at it bie isien fre of al ikenelricre and alderdomelere &t;inghe an iwitradenne an of elchene &t;inghe buten fierde and angieldes. 
 And het it acheliche fre &t;urgwine habbe suelcman suo al se ich it habbe. 
 Gief donne huelman be segen &t;at he &t;is giue and sale ieche o&d; manifelde wille iache him almigti God alle goode here for wolde and his igaste furch agiue &t;a ache reste in &d;am towarde liue. 
 If &t;at ilimpe &t;at oniman &t;urch deules lore and for &t;eses middelerdes idle &t;inghe on onni idale ilitel o&t;er michel &t;is ibreke o&t;er iwanie wite he hine fram alle leaffulle inne &t;ese iworlde asceaden and he des sel in domes deghe beforen Criste rich agieldende bute he it are her on worlde mid richte ibete. 
 &D;is land is &t;isen imare &t;us ute &t;insald  arest on landscarhlinc to Cheselburne  &t;annen anlang streames  &t;anne up of streame on anne linkes haued  &t;anen be su&d;e ceatwanberge on &t;anne hlinc  of &t;anne ihlinche on anne castel at swindone uuewearde. 
 Of &t;e castele to burnstowe  &t;anen up anlang burnstowe  &t;anne on anne linkes heued  &t;anen to anne castele  of &t;o icastel on &t;ere herepa&t;  westward op &d;e herepa&d; on &t;at hlincreawe. 
 &T;anen on anne crundel to burnstowe  on anne cinep on &d;an ger&d;e lande nordewarde  &t;anen wi&d; anne crundeles  of &t;i crundele on des heges hirnen. 
 &T;anen eft to herepa&d;  of &t;e ere&d;e on hendune beorch  on Deflisch  of dune anlang streames  up on streame on anne furch  on anne stancastel  of &t;i castele on blieqgmannes beorg  of &t;a iberge on landscare hlinc. 
 And sex made eres be Frume wi&d; Deuliscmad ier&d; to &t;ise lande. 
 &D;ises landes freols was iwriten in &t;are stowe &t;at is inemned at wudegate beforen &t;ese wetene &t;e here namen her bene&t;en amerkede standen.

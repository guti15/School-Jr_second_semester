 Wyt all that es and es gan &t;at ik king Adelstan As gyven als frelich as I may And to &t;e capitell of seint Wilfrai  Of my free devotion &t;air pees at Rippon On ilke side &t;e kyrke a mile  For all ill deedes and ilke agyle  And within &t;air kirke yate At &t;e stan &t;at Grithstole hate. 
 Within &t;e kirke dore and &t;e quare &t;air have pees for les and mare. 
 Ilkan of &t;is stedes sal have pees Of frodmortell and il deedes &t;at &t;air don is  tol and tem  With iren and with water deme And &t;at &t;e land of seint Wilfrai Of alkyn geld fre sal be ay. 
 At na nan at langes me to In &t;air Herpsac sal have at do  And for ik will at ya be save I will at &t;ai alkyn freedom have. 
 And in al thinges be als free As hert may thynke or eygh may se At te power of a kynge Masts make free any thynge. 
 And my seale have I sett &t;erto For I will at na man it undo.

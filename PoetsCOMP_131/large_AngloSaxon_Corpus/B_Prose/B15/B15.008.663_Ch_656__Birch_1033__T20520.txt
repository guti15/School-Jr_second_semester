 &T;is sant &d;a landimare at &t;ortune  &t;e ier&d; in to Hamtune  arest on arcetham. 
 Of archethamme on Rumanhelle  &t;anen of bre&t;ling made &tbar; on wiger&d;es stapel  &t;anen to tudesleghe of &t;ere lege be kinges imare on blinnesfeld. 
 &T;anen be wde on &t;ere hinc imare anlang mares &tbar; on ealdmannes wyer&d;e &t;anan on &t;a seales  of &t;an sealen &t;at eft on arcetham. 
 And &t;is sent &t;e tweire hide landimare &t;e cyna achte &t;e ealde. 
 Arest on iwern broc  of &t;ane broke on &t;e lang dich  &t;an on &t;ene weie  anlang weies to late mere. 
 &T;ane onlang sledes on &d;a smale &t;ornes  &t;anne on &d;a dich  &t;anne swo be diche on cranemere  of cranemere eft on iwern broc.

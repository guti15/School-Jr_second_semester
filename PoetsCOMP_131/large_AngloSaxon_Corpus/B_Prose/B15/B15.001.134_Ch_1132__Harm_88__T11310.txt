 &amp; ic ky&t;e eou &t;&ae;t it is min fulle unne &t;&ae;t Ailric hafet gegyfen &t;at land at Greneford Crist &amp; Sainte Petre in to Westminstre for &t;are forewarde &t;e &t;ase bro&d;era &amp; he gemaked habbet.

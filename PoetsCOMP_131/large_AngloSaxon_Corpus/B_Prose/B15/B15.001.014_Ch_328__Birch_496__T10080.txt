 Id est an wiwarawic quae ante subjecta erat to wii &amp; to leanaham &amp; et febresham: i sealtern &amp; ii wena gang mid cyninges wenum to blean &d;em wiada. 
 &amp; iiii oxnum gers mid cyninges oxnum  an wiwarawic xxx statera kasei et item x statera in alia wiwarawic &amp; xx lamba &amp; xx fehta. 
 Ab occidente cyninges folcland quod abet wighelm &amp; wulflaf ab aquilone cu&d;rices dun herege&d;eland ab oriente wighelmes land a meritie biscepes land. 
 Hec sunt prata to wassingwellan stocmed healf be nor&d;an hegforde be sturemeda sue &d;er to limpa&d;. 
 &D;is siondan &d;es landes boec et Wassingwellan &d;et E&d;elbearht cyning Wullafe sealde his &d;egne wi&d; o&d;rum sue miclum lande et Mersaham. 
 Se cyning sealde &amp; gebocade Wullafe fif sulung landes et Wassingwellan wi&d; &d;em fif sulungum et Mersaham. 
 &amp; se cyning dyde &d;et land et Mersaham him to folclande &d;a hie &d;em landum iehwerfed hefdan butan &d;em merscum &amp; butan &d;em sealtern et Fefresham &amp; butan &d;em wioda &d;e to &d;em sealtern limp&d;. 
 &D;is siondan &d;es landes boc et Wassingwellan.

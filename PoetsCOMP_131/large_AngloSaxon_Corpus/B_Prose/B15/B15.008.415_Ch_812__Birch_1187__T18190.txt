 &T;is is &t;e landmarke to Romesye. 
 Erest up andlang strete &t;are &t;at &t;urstan seit and so to fareburne up andlang fareburne anon to carebrok. 
 Of carebrokes on &t;a rugedich andlang &t;are rugedich to &t;are la&t;emere  and si&t;en oure bi bisshopes marke to ris brigge. 
 Of mearkbrok andlang mearkbrok to cumerantreuwe  of cumerantreowe on marchbrokesheued on &t;ane brode mere. 
 Of &t;ane mere sugrist in nan &t;a willan  of &t;an willan of gryndenbrok. 
 Of gryndenbrok on stennes paeg andlan pa&t;es hut on rusfeld &t;anne andlang hegges oue hit comeg to Wirmesie. 
 Of Wirmesye to bodestan  fram bodestan suthe andlang lauen. 
 Fram &t;e lauen in &t;e dich bi &t;ugen &t;anne andlang dich in non &t;e wi&t;ige  of &t;an wi&t;ie in an &t;e heat andlang heae in &t;an dede lake. 
 Of &t;are dedelake to huntesige. 
 Of huntesige in and &t;an alde tersten andlang &t;ar ealde terste ond &t;e hit come&t; in &t;are streit &t;are &t;urstan seyt. 
 &T;e gyldene melle so on &t;ane ledene speketh and &t;aye angrenene boges an &t;are goleggede hause&t; mid golde war on gesald sanwel stedte. 
 Edmond Ae&t;eling he on &t;are ministre lig&t; and &t;e kyng wite&t; &t;ane sette &t;ane wode calue sealde in to &t;are stowe. 
 On &t;an gerard &t;&ae;t noman &t;are on ti&t;e nanede hut of &t;am hurede.

 He hafa&d; geweor&d;ad mid cynedome Angulseaxna Eadred cyning &amp; casere totius Brittanni&e; Deo gratias for &d;em weolega&d; &amp; ara&d; gehadade &amp; l&ae;wede &t;a &d;e mid rihte magon ge&ae;rnian.

 Eadred cyng &t;y teoge&d;an gere &t;es &d;e he gehalgad wes gesette &t;as handsetene  anno cominicae incarnationis DCCCCLV. 
 &T;es landes is into &AE;&t;elwoldingtune V hida. 
 &amp; &t;is synd &t;a landgem&ae;rum  on earninga str&ae;te &ae;t &t;am stapelan &amp; &t;onne nor&t; &ae;fter strate to &t;ere dic on Ceastertuninga gem&ae;rie  andlanges &t;ere dic in on &t;a ea to healfan strame &amp; &d;onne eft andlanges &t;ere ea to goldege.

 &amp; &d;onne su&d; efter &t;em ri&d;ige to &t;em m&ae;re &amp; swa su&d;rihte to &t;em litle lawe &amp; &t;onne get su&d;rihte on &t;a lytlan &t;yrnan &amp; of &t;ere &t;yrnan westrihte to &t;em coppedan &t;onne  eft on earninga str&ae;te.

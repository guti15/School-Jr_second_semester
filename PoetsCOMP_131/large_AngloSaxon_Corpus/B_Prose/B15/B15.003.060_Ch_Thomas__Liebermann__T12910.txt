 &D;is syn &t;a gerihto and &t;a laga  thet archebiscop ah ofer eal Euerwic bynan burh an butan. 
 &T;&ae;t is  aerest Legera&t;orp and on nordhealf Munecagate &amp; fra &T;urbrandes hus eal up on Walbegate and eal Clementesthorpe an Sancte Marie circa. 
 Mid sace &amp; mid socne  mid tolle &amp; mid teme  and alcne &t;ridde penig  &t;e cumde up on Walbegate and on Fiscergate. 
 And &t;ridde penig of &t;one fiscoup scrasudwrasas ford yb dic and &t;onne &t;ridde  &t;e up cum&d; of les Gildegarde. 
 And alc man  &t;e mid coupfare cume to Euerwyc  eastan odde nordan  sudan odde westan  mid horsum odde wegnon gifen heora toll &t;an  &t;e his mid rihte scylon: kynge  &t;e ah mid rihte  &amp; biscope &t;e ah. 
 And fare alc man &t;e come wille he to kynges scire  wille he to biscopes  be Godes leoue and &t;es kynges. 
 And swa hwilc coupman so cumth into biscopes scire and coupad in kinges scire  gyfe &t;an kinge &t;&ae;t tolle  gif hit sy tolles wurde. 
 And gif ani man formaele hine sylfne of biscopes scire innan kynges scire  habbe se king. 
 And ealle &t;a unriht &d;e man wirced  gehaded man odde lawede  hoc est se l&ae;reda misbeode freolses daes odde fastendas  and se lawed on mana&d;e and on unrihthemincge and on unrihtweorcum. 
 &T;e man wirces binnan burh &amp; butan  ne &d;eo kinges greua ne eorles to &t;issum giltum  buton biscop and ercediacon  be Godes blezsunge and &t;a erbiscopes. 
 And twa minetras hafy se biscop in Euerwic. 
 &D;is is to gewitnesse Arngrim Monachus &amp; Oudergrim &amp; Clibern &amp; Wlfstan &amp; Ouldalf &amp; Ulfkil &amp; Ouderben &amp; Harwolf &amp; Lisolf an Gluneorn &amp; Beornolf &amp; Ulf &amp; eal seo burhware on Euerwic. 
 And se ercebiscopes hirde &amp; Huga sciregreuan and Willem of Snotingham and Beringar &t;aes kynges arendracan &amp; Ilbeard of Hittawuda and Wilelm de Percia and Wilelm Tysun and ealle &t;es kincges men. 
 And habbe he Godes grame &t;e &t;is undo; Amen.

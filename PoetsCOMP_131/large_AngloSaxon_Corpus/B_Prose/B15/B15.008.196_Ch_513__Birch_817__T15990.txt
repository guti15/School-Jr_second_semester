 &T;is beth &t;e landmere of Domerham. 
 At &t;are aissh at penegeforde from &t;an stede endlang brokes to &t;is erlisford of &t;an ford' to brinesforde and so endelang mereborne to &t;are heghe brugge and soa endelang &t;is elde weyes to brocliford. 
 And endlang brocliford to wulewe and so endelang &t;er wite lak' up on kingberwes and &t;anne rigt up an brokhurste and endelang &t;are lake to ther hide. 
 Of lospushale and so bi talayate an &t;anne to &t;ar dich bi weste widemere and soa endelang rigges to upiate and from upyate endelang dich to wideyate. 
 Ant &t;anne to &t;an gore and &t;anne so to cotelesburgh' and &t;anne so to ther seuenestrete and of &t;er seuenestrete to coteleshed and soa endelang &t;er strete dich to wilteneweie. 
 And endelang weyes to &t;an langeberghe ant &t;anne rigt in the wide dich and of &t;er dich ouer knolle to acclei and so to hedstokke and &t;anne to &t;es drakenhorde in on &t;ar siderer. 
 And so endelang &t;are dich to &t;an frimde dich and so in on &t;e mere and of &t;an mere in on &t;an landschere stok and of &t;are stokke in on coppenthorn and &t;anne to &t;an ellene bem. 
 And so walke stille and &t;anne to prestes yate endlang weies up on holebrok' hulle and so to &t;e sladisheuede of &t;e sladisheuede in to &t;an aissh'. 
 To pennegaford &t;are &t;er ongunne yat. 
 Stant in wiltschire dorseteschire and hampteschire.

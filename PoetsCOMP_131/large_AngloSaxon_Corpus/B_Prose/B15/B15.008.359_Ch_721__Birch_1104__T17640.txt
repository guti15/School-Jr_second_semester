 &T;is beth &t;e landmere. 
 Erest of &t;an welle &t;aron otheri endlang stremes on &t;e redecliue northward &t;are op on ame put of &t;an putte on an withi of &t;an withi on an stonberwe. 
 Of &t;an berwe est up on &t;e rigge on hothurne stanberwe &t;anen to &t;an herpo&t;e endelang herpo&t;es on &t;e stanbrugge &t;ar up on landscherlake on holangcombe heued &t;anen up on dich. 
 &T;anen on midde &t;e doune endlang doune on iffingknap medeward &t;anen on iffingberlake of &t;er lake on &t;e dich of &t;an dich on &t;e ifre northward eft on &t;e welle endlang rewe &t;are eft on otheri.

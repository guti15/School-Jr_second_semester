 &AE;t Eanulfestune  Eadrice &amp; Wulfrune. 
 Albuscente hac consentiente Eadgaro basileo Aelfhere que Merciorum ego Oswold largiflua dei clementia antistites quandam rusculi partem III silicet mansas tribus tamen in locis diuisam  cui uocabulum est &ae;t Eanulfestune o&t;er healf hid &amp; &ae;t Uferan Str&ae;tforda on &t;&ae;re gesyndredan hide &t;one o&t;erne &ae;cer &amp; &ae;t Fachanleage &t;one &t;riddan &ae;cer feldlandes. 
 &amp; healfne &t;one wudu on easthealfe &t;&ae;s weges &amp; &t;one &ae;t &t;&ae;re eor&d;byrig &amp; on easthealfe Afene eahta &ae;ceras m&ae;dwa &amp; forne gean biccenclife XII &ae;ceras m&ae;dwa &amp; &t;reo &ae;cras benor&d;an Afene to myllnstealle Eadrico meo compatri &ae;ternaliter concessi et post uit&e; su&e; terminum duorum derelinquat cleronomo eorumque uit&e; finito curriculo ad usum primatis in Wiogornaceastre redeat immunis. 
 &T;onne is ealles &t;&ae;s landes &t;reo hida &t;e Oswald biscop boca&d; Eadrice his &t;egne on &t;a gerad wyrce &t;&ae;t he wyrce &t;&ae;t &t;&ae;t land seo unforworht into &t;&ae;re halgan stowe twegra monna d&ae;g &ae;fter him. 
 Anno dominicae incarnationis DCCCCLXVI scripta est hec carta his testibus consentientibus quorum inferius nomina notantur.

 Qua propter ego Swi&d;uulf episcopus &amp; &d;a higan &ae;t Hrofes cestre dabunt Biorhtuulfo presbytero aliquam partem terre in provincio Canti&ae; in regione que vocatur Haddun. 
 H&ae;c sunt &ae;tenim termini histius predicti agelli circum acentia bisceopes mearc an nor&d;an east be hunesbiorge o&d; ciolmundes mearce &amp; &d;es landes mere swe su&d; ober haddune middewearde &amp; ober &d;ane ealdan tunsteall o&d; cinges mearce &amp; su&d; be cinges mearce o&d; &d;ane myclan del nor&d;an &amp; west be cinges mearce o&d; ciolmundes wioda &amp; west be &d;y wioda andlanges &d;are rode o&d; &d;ane pyt &amp; east fram &d;y pytte &d;urh &d;ane wioda wi&d; su&d;an hunesbiorge &amp; wen weg &d;y lande ober ciolmundes land to ealdan strete. 
 H&ae;c sunt prata que ad illam terram pertinent I et bioccan lea  &amp; an su&d; healfe strodes an cyninges medum &d;a &d;e &d;&ae;r to belimpa&d;. 
 Eadgar cynincg of his agenre handa sealde &t;as boc Leofrice on &t;ara gewitnesse &t;e her beni&t;an standa&t;.

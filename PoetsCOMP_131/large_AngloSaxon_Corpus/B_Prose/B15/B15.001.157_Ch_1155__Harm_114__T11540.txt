 Edward kyng gret Leuen bissop and Leuen erles and alle myne &t;eignes on Staffordscire frendliche. 
 And ic segge ou &t;at myne prestes atte Hampton &t;at ic habben me treudam giuen ham. 
 Nou wille ic &t;at he ben and here mynstre frede and here &t;ing &t;er be heren richeli to be frid mid sac and mid socne so ful and so ford so ic formest it hadden in alle &t;ing. 
 And ic wille giuen ham &t;an &t;at ham enyng man enyng on rich boed.

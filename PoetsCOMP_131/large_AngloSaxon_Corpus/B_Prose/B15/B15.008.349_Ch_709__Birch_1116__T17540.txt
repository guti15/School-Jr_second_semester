 This is th&ae;re anre hyde land boc &ae;t monawyrhthyge the eadgar cyng gebocade &ae;lfrice his thegene on ece yrfe. 
 This synd there are hyde landgem&ae;ru to Monawurthige &ae;rust an Monawurthiges ford. 
 Of tham forda up on Strem an &ae;stcumb. 
 Of &ae;stcumbe anriht gem&ae;re an Pendancumb up an Pendacumb to therewille of than wille on riht gem&ae;re inan thene thyrlan &ae;st of tham &ae;ste in on Beocceles put of tham pytte on riht g&ae;m&ae;re to tham f&ae;gran wille of tham wylle on dune an stream to Codw&ae;ala forda. 
 Of tham forda andune strema on smalabroc andland smalabroces to Lihtenes forda of Lihtenes forda up on stream to Bectcelesstile of Bectcelesstile on riht gem&ae;re an tha hola dic. 
 Of there dic suth on th&ae;ne herpoth. 
 Thenne and lad herpothes to Heantune suth weardon. 
 Of Heuntune on riht gem&ae;re to th&ae;re bradan apulduran  on geriht m&ae;re on thet hwite styb. 
 Of than stybbe an clenan mor w&ae;stwearne of cl&ae;ne more w&ae;steweardan an thane stream. 
 Thanne up on stream. 
 Eft on Monawyrthigesford and cl&ae;na mor eal intro Monawurthige et Ruwa mor healf and seo l&ae;s on stoccum  &amp; seo wur&ae;dden on harhrycge.

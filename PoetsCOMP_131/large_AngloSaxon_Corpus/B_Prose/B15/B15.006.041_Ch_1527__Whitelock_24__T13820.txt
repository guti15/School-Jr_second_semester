 &T;at is erst for his soule Palegraue into seynt Eadmund and Witingham half and half &t;e bisscop. 
 And alle mine men fre and ilk habbe his toft and his metecu &amp; his metecorn.

 And ic an Wingefeld mine bro&t;eres sunes Vlfketel &amp; &t;urfketel. 
 And ic an Lefquene fiftene acres at Palegraue &amp; an toft. 
 And Wlwine habbe &t;at lond &t;e he mines hafde. 
 &amp; Alfwold habbe mid ton &t;e he her hauede XVI acres mid tofte mid alle.

 &amp; he geu&d;e &t;&ae;t man &t;&ae;t land on eallum &t;ingon for ane hide werode swa swa his yldran hit &ae;r gesetton &amp; gefreodon  w&ae;re &t;&ae;r mare landes w&ae;re &t;&ae;r l&ae;sse. 
 &amp; he bead on Godes naman &t;&ae;t na&d;er ne &t;&ae;re stowe bisceop ne nanes bisceopes &ae;ftergenga &t;&ae;t land n&ae;fre of &t;&ae;re stowe geutode ne hit nanan portmen wi&d; nanan sceatte ne wi&d; ceape gesealde. 
 &amp; he bead &t;urh Godes &ae;lmichtiges myclan m&ae;gen&t;rymm &t;&ae;t nan his bearna ne nan heora &ae;ftergengcana &t;&ae;t menster &ae;fre leng mid preostan gesette. 
 Ac &t;&ae;t hit efre mid munecan stode swa swa he hit mid Godes &ae;lmilhtiges fultume gesette &t;a &t;a he hit &t;a modigan preostas for heora mand&ae;don &t;anan ut adrefde &amp; &t;erinne munecas gelogode &t;&ae;t hi Godes &t;eowedom &ae;fter sancte Benedictes t&ae;cinge &amp; d&ae;ghwamlice to Gode cleopodon for ealles cristenes folces alidsednesse. 
 Ealles &t;&ae;s landes is an hund hida ac &t;a godan cynegas &amp; &t;a wisan &ae;lc &ae;fter o&t;ran &t;&ae;t ylce land swa gefreodon Gode to lofe &amp; his &t;eowan to brycen &amp; to fostorlande &t;&ae;t hit man &ae;fre on ende for ane hide werian sceolde. 
 Se &t;e &t;ysne freols healdan wille God &ae;lmihtig hine gehealde her &amp; on ecnesse. 
 Gif hwa &t;onne &t;urh &ae;nige dyrstignesse o&d;&d;e &t;urh deofles lare &t;isne freols abrecan wille o&d;&d;e &t;as gesetednesse on o&d;er awendan durre se he awyrged mid eallan &t;an awyrgednessan &t;e synd awritene on eallan halgan bocan. 
 &amp; sy he ascyred fram ures Drichtnes gemanan &amp; ealra his halgana &amp; sy he gebunden &t;a hwile &t;e he libbe on &t;isam life mid &t;an ylcan bendan &t;e God &ae;lmihtig &t;yrh hine sylfne bet&ae;chte his halgan apostolan Petre &amp; Paule &amp; &ae;fter his awyrgedan for&d;si&d;e ligge he efre on healle grundleasan pytte. 
 &amp; byrne he on &t;an ecan fyre mid deofle &amp; his englan a butan &ae;lcan ende butan he hit &ae;r his for&d;si&d;e gebete  Amen.

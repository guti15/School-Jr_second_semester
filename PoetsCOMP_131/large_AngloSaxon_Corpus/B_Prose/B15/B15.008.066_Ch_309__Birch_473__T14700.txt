 &T;is is &t;ara III hida boc &ae;t Wordige Donum regis A&d;ulfi. 
 &T;is synd &t;a landgem&ae;ra to Wordige &t;&ae;t is of icenan andlang &t;&ae;ra dic up to &t;&ae;re hylle. 
 Of &t;&ae;re hylle andlang &t;&ae;re dic. 
 Eft sona up to heafod stoccan. 
 Of heafod stocca andlang str&ae;t to lusan &t;orne. 
 Of lusan &t;orne to deopan delle up to kinges stane. 
 Fram kincges stane up to holan stane. 
 Of holan stane up to fyrd geate. 
 Of fyrd geate to wic herpa&d;e. 
 Andlang wic herp&ae;&d;es &ae;ft to kynges stane. 
 Of kynges stane to ysan pyttan. 
 Of isan pyttan to twelf &ae;keran. 
 Of twelf &ae;keran ut ford bufon scortan hlince &ae;t &t;&ae;s furlanges ende. 
 And swa for&d; to &t;&ae;re byrig and swa into hydiburnan.

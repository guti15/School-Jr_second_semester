 &T;is bet &t;e landmere. 
 To Milne. 
 Erist of todanbrigge of &t;are muchel dich on mordrancombe. 
 Of mordrancombe on slacombe. 
 Of slacombe on boreswelle. 
 Of boreswelle on &t;an merkeden ok to feger ok &t;anen on &t;inaswelle of Ineswelle on Worhanan berghe. 
 On Wolfberghe. 
 &T;anen on Wolfpol. 
 Of Wolfpole endlang stremes on schippeburg westward on harenapildorewei &t;ere he out lit &t;anen on merestan. 
 Of &t;an stone endlang mereweies to likan trowe. 
 &T;anen est lang dich to redwines thorne. 
 &T;anen on baddanpille. 
 Of &t;an pille on berleighe mideward &t;anen endlang hagen on petanberberwe &t;anen on &t;a stanenpile of &t;ere stanenpile eft on todanberghe on the muchel dich.

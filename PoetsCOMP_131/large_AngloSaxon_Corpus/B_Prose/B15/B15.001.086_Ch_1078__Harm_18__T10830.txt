 Eadward kyng gret Aylmer bisscop &amp; Alfger erl &amp; Toly and alle mine theynes on Suffolke frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ihu &t;at ic wille &t;at &t;e half nigende hundred sokne &t;e ic habbe ihiuen God and Seynt Eadmunde mine mey &t;at it liggen into &t;at halegen minstre at Eadmundes biri so ful and so forth so mine moder it fermist aihte. 
 And it Alfrich Withgares sune hire to hande biwiste and it sithen me seluen on honde stoden. 
 &amp; ic ne am becnowe &t;at ic ani man uthe &t;enen ut neyther ne hamsokne ne grithbreche ne forstal ne fichtwite ne hebberethef ne non &t;ere rithte &t;a thider in mid rithte ibere&d; be fullen wite.

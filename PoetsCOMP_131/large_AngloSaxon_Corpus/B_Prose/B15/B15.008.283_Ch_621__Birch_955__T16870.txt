 &D;is sind &t;a landgemearo to Pyrianforda. 
 &AE;rest of pyrianforda andlang w&ae;gan on fisces burnan. 
 Andlang burnan on &t;one mearc hagan up be hagan o&t; &t;a gedela west on gerihta to snawes mere wuestewearden &t;onne on &t;one ho foreweardne &t;onne on gerihta ofer ufandune &t;onne on &t;one gyrd weg andlang weges on &t;one meac hagan. 
 Up be hagan on weor&t;e hege for&d; be &t;an hege on weor&t; apeldre &t;onne on per leage &t;onne su&t; to efes on &t;one ealdan hrunan &t;&ae;t to hagan op be hagan to eceles hamme &t;onne a be hagan to mylen felda. 
 &T;onne a be hagan to mintfelda &t;onon on gerihta on mintbyrge &t;&ae;r to burnan &amp;lang burnan on &t;a flexwaran on &t;one hagan &t;onon ut on &t;one felde &tbar; ut to milgemete &t;onon &t;a ferh&t;e forwearde &t;onon to &t;&ae;re twicene. 
 Andlang twicene on weahhes fen &t;onon ut on wegan andlang wegan on pyrianford &amp; si &t;&ae;t wuda hyr&t;sum into pyrianforda.

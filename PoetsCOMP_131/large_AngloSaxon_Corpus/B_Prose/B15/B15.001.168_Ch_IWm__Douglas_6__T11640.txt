 William kyng grete mine biscopes and mine erles and alle mine &t;eynes one &t;e schiren &t;er Baldewyne abbot haueth lond and men inne frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ou &t;at ic an &t;at he be his abbotes riches wrthe and his sake and his sokne binnen porte and buten so ful and so forth so Eadward kyng mine mey it &t;ider inne uthe on alle &t;inge. 
 And ic nelle &t;afien &t;at him oni man of honde teo no&t;ing &t;at ic him unnen habbe.

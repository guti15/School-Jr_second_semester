 Ic Eadward king ki&d;e on &d;ise write alle lefful manne &d;at ic red habbe wi&d; mine erchebiscopes and biscopes and earles and wi&d; alle mine &t;eynes. 
 &D;at is &d;at ic wille &d;at seynt Eadmundes minster mine meyes  and se tun &d;e &d;at minster inne is  be ekelike on &d;at ilke fredome &d;at Cnut king and Hardcnut kyng mine bro&d;er &d;iderin u&d;en. 
 Ne neuer se stede ne wr&d;e loged mid o&d;ere hodes manne &d;anne mid moneke  ne non &d;ere biscop &d;e euermor schal ben on Nor&d;folc o&d;er on Suffolc ne habbe neuere non onsting on &d;at minstere ne so wyd so &d;a tunes merkes go&d; &d;e &d;at minster inne stond. 
 And &d;o monekes chesen hem alder of hem seluen so so riht is. 
 And ic wille &d;at se socne and se sake &d;at ic hem unnen habbe stonde unawend euer so wer so he nu habben lond o&d;er men o&d;er iget bigeten schalen &t;urg Godes fultume and godere manne. 
 Nu bid ic alle Godes frend ei&d;er &d;e &d;at nu libben ore &d;e &d;at after hus schulen comen &d;at he for Godes eye neuere ne ben on stede ne on stalle &d;er euere undon wr&d;e &d;at ure fordgengles u&d;en and &d;at we unnen habben into &d;at heli minster. 
 And igif &d;an euere ani man so drsti wr&d;e &t;urgh &d;ese defles lore &d;at he &d;is wi&d;quenchen wille and aweygen  si he amansumod and on helle susle bischemt bute he er his liues ende &d;e deppere bete.

 &T;es beth &t;e land mere to Idemestone &t;are X hide. 
 Erest to &t;an elde were. 
 Of &t;an elde were endlanges &t;ar wiresheued to &t;an weie. 
 Of &t;an weie wires ouer &t;an dene to &t;an holon of &t;an holon to Piscrundel &t;anen on &t;an wey endlang weies on &t;an dich corf. 
 And forth ouer wirfuldoune suthward to &t;an crundele &t;anen endelang weies at litelhulle to &t;an stane &t;anne vp ouer handoune to &t;an birgilsond. 
 &T;anen vp on menhulle of menhulle est to &t;er strete endlang &t;er strete to ernebergh &t;anne south to &t;a stone. 
 Of &t;an stone ouer hendoune on &t;e furs. 
 Endlang &t;er furghs to &t;an bourne &t;anen endlang borne as hit schet vp to &t;an knolle at Smalwey &t;anne west to kingesdich for to hit kemet to fimbergh. 
 &T;anen &t;er north endelang weyes on hehelmeslandschere &t;anne endlang wei wires ouer brouningbergh northward on northond &t;e weies X acres &t;anen on &t;e dich &t;ar hit geth eft on &t;e eldewere.

 On vre Drichtines name  ic Ailmer biscop kithe alle manne ihwat ic habbe vnnen into sancte Eadmunde. 
 &T;at is &t;at lond at Hindringham and &t;at lond at Langham and &t;at lond at Hildoluestone &amp; &t;at lond at Suanetone mid alle &t;e &t;inge &t;at ic &t;erto bigeten habbe and &t;ertoeken half hundred marc silueres. 
 And ihu so ic wende mine cuide ic wille &t;at &t;is stonde euere vnawent mine soule to lisidnesse. 
 And se &t;e &t;ise quide wenden wille wende God his ansene him from on domisday.

 Eadward king gret Alfric biscop and alle mine &t;eynes on Norfolke &amp; on Suffolke frendlike. 
 And ic kithe ihu &t;at ic wille &t;at Vui abbot be &t;et minstres wir&d;e at Seynt Eadmundes biri and alle &t;inge &t;e &t;er to bireth on lande &amp; on sake and on sokne and on alle &t;inge so ful and so forth so it firmest &t;ider inne lay. 
 &amp; ic wille &t;at se freols stonde into &t;at minstre unawent &t;e Cnut king &t;ider inne uthe &amp; sithen Hardcnut kyng mine brother. 
 &amp; ic nelle &t;at efre ani bisscop ani &t;ing him &t;eron ateo.

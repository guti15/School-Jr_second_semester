 &T;is sand &t;are XVI hyde landimare at Corf &t;e Adred king bokien Wihsige his &t;egene on elche halue. 
 Arest of sa on anne dich  anlang dich. 
 Upp on swuren. 
 &T;wert ouer swuran on &tbar; &t;wers dich. 
 Of &t;are diche on ecge ford be ecge  on Alfricheswelle. 
 Of &t;ane welle  on anne weg. 
 Of &t;ane wige  on an dich. 
 Anlang dich north richte on wicun ni&t;er anland wicum streames. 
 Of &t;anne streame  on anne &t;orn north anlang dich. 
 On anne walle  onlang walles  on stanwei. 
 Of &t;ane dich  on anne dich. 
 Onlang diche  north irichte. 
 On wicum ni&t;er anlang wicunstreames. 
 Of &t;at streame  on anne &t;orn. 
 Of &t;ane &t;orne  on &t;a holendich. 
 Anlang dich uppen iricht &t;urch &t;ane wde on &t;are dich  on &t;ane rupemor. 
 Of &t;ane more  on &t;one crundel of &t;ane crundel up irichte on &t;at holenbedde. 
 Of &t;at holnebedde  on &t;are holne stoke. 
 Of &t;ane stocke  on &t;are stream anlang streames  on auenes broc. 
 Of &t;ane broke  on &t;ane ealde weg andlang weies  on hecgan sled. 
 Of &t;ane slede  on &t;are streame. 
 Anlang streames  on &t;at stanene bregge. 
 Of &t;are brigge on &t;are wei anlang weies of irichte on &t;ane richt wei. 
 Of &t;ane weie  on anne stan wal. 
 Of &t;ane walle nither irichte on Wickenford. 
 Onlang Wickenforde. 
 Uppen irichte on beambroc swo. 
 Up anlang streames. 
 Of &t;ane streame  on &t;at withi begh. 
 Of &t;ane wi&t;ibedde  on anne stan. 
 Of &t;ane stane  on &t;ane o&t;erne. 
 Of &t;an stane  on holen wicken. 
 Eft on &t;are stan. 
 Of &t;ane stan  on &t;a ealdene dich  anlang dich. 
 On &t;ane wal  anlang welles. 
 On struthgeardes cum anlang welles on struthherdes wege. 
 Of &t;ane wege eft on anne wal onlang welles. 
 On seuen willes &t;ry anlang streames ut a irichte on seortmannes pol.

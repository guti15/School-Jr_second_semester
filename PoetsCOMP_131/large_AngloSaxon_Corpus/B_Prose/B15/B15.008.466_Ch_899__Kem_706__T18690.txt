 &AE;rest of seuen pirien on &d;ere here wai  &d;e schet su&d;ward wi&d;uten acceslegle wur&d; ut wrindes holt and swa anlang Herewines to &ae;lfwines hlipgate. 
 Fram &d;ane hlipgate for&d; be is landschare inne Auene. 
 Swa for&d; be streme inne byssi. 
 Swa uppe bissy on wret. 
 Swa onlonghes wret &d;at it comet to Brisnodes landschare scutn. 
 For&d; be his landshare inne swinbroch. 
 For&d; be broke inne pumberig. 
 Ut &t;urh pumberig inne teflefor&d;. 
 For&d; mid streme &d;at it cumet to &ae;lfwerdes landimare at wutenham. 
 &D;annes of wigewen broke for&d; be Leofwines imare innen Auene. 
 For&d; be Auene &d;at it cumet to Fersefor&d; &d;es abbotes imare innen Mitford. 
 Of &d;anne forde gyet be &d;es abbotes imare. 
 Eft into Auene. 
 Swo in &d;er be Auene &d;at it cumet eft to &d;es abbotes imare to Werleghe. 
 Swa be &d;es abbotes imare to &ae;lfgares imare at Farnleghe. 
 For&d; be is imare o&d; &d;at it cumet to &d;es kinges imare at Heselberi. 
 For&d; be &d;es kinges imare &d;at it cumet to &ae;lfgares imare at Attenwr&d;e. 
 For&d; be is imare &d;at it cumet to Leofwines landimare at Coseham. 
 Of &d;an imare to &d;es aldremannes imare at Witlege. 
 For&d; be &d;anne imare &d;at it comet to &ae;lfwiges imare at Broctune to &d;anne wude &d;e iera&d; into Broctune. 
 Eft at seuen pirien. 
 For&d; be &ae;lfno&d;es imare innen &ae;&d;elwines imare at Chaldfelde. 
 Of his imare innen &ae;lfwines imare &d;e Horderes. 
 For&d; be his imare innen &ae;lphwines imare at Broctune.

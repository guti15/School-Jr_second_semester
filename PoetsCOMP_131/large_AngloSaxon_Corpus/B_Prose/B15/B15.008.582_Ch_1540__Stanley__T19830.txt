 &T;onne of stanbrycge east andlang &t;&ae;re lace &t;&ae; scyt wi&d; ceren burhg westan. 
 &T;onan nor&d; andlang &t;&ae;re lace &ae;t &t;am gemy&d;um; of &t;am gemy&t;an andlang &t;&ae;re lace &ae;t cerenforda. 
 Of cerenforda andlang lace on eoccen; &t;onne up andlang eoccen et landbroce. 
 &T;onne up andlang landbroce on &t;a m&ae;&d; dic; &t;onne up andlang dic; &t;onne eft on loccen.

 Eadgy&t; seo hlauedi Eadwardes kynges lefe gret al &t;at hundred at Wedmore frendliche. 
 And ich cy&t;e eow &t;at ic habbe gegefen Gyso biscop &t;at land at Merkerun &amp; alch &t;are &t;inga &t;&ae;s &t;e &t;ar mid richte to hire&d; into his canonican &ae;t Sancte Andrea &ae;t Wyllan mid saca &amp; mid socna swo full &amp; swo for&d; swo hit me sylfen on honde stod for Eadwardes kynges sawle &amp; for mine sawle. 
 And ic bidde eow &t;at ge deme me richtne dom of Wudemann &t;e ic min hors bitachte &amp; mi gauel haue&d; ofhealden six gear ei&d;er ge hunig &amp; eac feoch. 
 God ew gehealde.

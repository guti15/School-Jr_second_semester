 Edward kynge gret Eldred Erchebissop and Begard Bissop and Harald eorl and alle myne &t;eynes of Herefordshire and of Saloppshire. 
 And ich cou&t;e ow &t;at ich habbe geuen Seynte Marie moder Cristes munstres and myne clerkes of Bromfelde &t;at in &t;oen munstre wonyeth on Cristes &t;ewedome &t;at hoe boe on hore sake wor&t;e and hore sokene of hore lond an gry&t;es bruches and homesokene and forstalles and infonggene&t;eues and ulkes wytes worthe bynne burch and wit outentolles and teames of stronde and of streme. 
 And ich nulle &t;olyen &t;at enyman eny&t;yng ouersoe nou&t;er Bissop ne nan o&t;er man boten lokewo ysself wolle. 
 And ich nulle ge&t;awyen &t;at enyman &t;ys abreke by myne froshype.

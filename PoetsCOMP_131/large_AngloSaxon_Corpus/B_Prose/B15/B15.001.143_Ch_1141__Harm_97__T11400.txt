 Eadward kyngc gret alle mine bisceopes &amp; mine eorles &amp; mine &t;egenes on Barrocscire &amp; on Middelsexen freondlice. 
 &amp; ic ky&t;e eow &t;&ae;t ic habbe gegifan Criste &amp; Sancte Petre into Westmynstre Windlesoran &amp; Stane &amp; eall &t;&ae;t &t;&ae;r to her&d; binnan burh &amp; butan  mid saca &amp; mid socne  mid tolle &amp; mid teame &amp; mid infangenum &t;eofe  on wude &amp; on felde  be strande &amp; be lande  on str&ae;te &amp; of str&ae;te &amp; on eallan &t;ingan swa full &amp; swa for&d; swa hit me sylfan on hande stod. 
 &amp; ic nelle ge&t;afian &t;&ae;t &t;&ae;r &ae;ni mann &ae;nine onstync &t;&ae;r ofer habbe on &ae;nige &t;ingan butan se abbot &amp; &t;a mynekas to Sancte Petres neode.

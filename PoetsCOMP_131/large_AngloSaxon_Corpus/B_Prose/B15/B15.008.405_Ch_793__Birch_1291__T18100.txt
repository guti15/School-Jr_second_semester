 Erest up on see on hamberghes &t;anne on smaledich on merkemere. 
 On wirfelmere northward. 
 &T;anen suth on bremelwirnan. 
 &T;annen south endelang dich on bremelwil. 
 &T;anen of bremelwille on aishwel on &t;e hamheved. 
 &T;anen up endelang rewe on &t;e ellene stubbis. 
 &T;anen so bi heved on smalelinchewey of &t;an weye on bradenwel on merepul of merepulle on bisschopiswerthe. 
 And so endlang pillis on axen.

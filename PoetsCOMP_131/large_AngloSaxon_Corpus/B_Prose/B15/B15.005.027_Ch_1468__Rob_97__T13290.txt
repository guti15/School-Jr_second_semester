 Her switele&t; on &t;is write &t;e forwarde &t;at Ailmer &t;e biscopes brother hauede wrouht with Vui Abbot and wi&d; alle &t;en hird binnen seynt Eadmundes biri ymbe &t;at litle lond at Swanetone and &t;at halue lond at Hildoluestone. 
 &T;at is &t;at he selde hem on marc goldes to &t;e forwarde &t;at he habbe &t;at lond his day and ouer his day go &t;o londes eft ongeyn into sancte Eadmunde and &t;at halfe lond for&t;mide mid mete and mid manne and alle &t;inge buten alken gentale. 
 &D;ise sinden &t;e mannes names &t;e to witnesse sinden &t;ises  &t;at is Stigande bisscop and Vui Abbot and Lefstan &t;e dean and al se hired binnen seynt Eadmundes biri and Lefsi Abbot &amp; al se hird binnen Ely and Alfsy Abbot at sancte Benedice &amp; al se hird &t;erbinnen &amp; Alfwine &amp; Alfric and Edric and Godwine &amp; Fredegist &amp; Vlf aet Welle &amp; Scule Leofwoldes sune and Godwine at Cringelfor&d; &amp; Eadwine Vlfketeles sune. 
 &T;ise write sinden tua  on is binnen seynt Eadmundes biri and o&t;er haued Aylmer hymself.

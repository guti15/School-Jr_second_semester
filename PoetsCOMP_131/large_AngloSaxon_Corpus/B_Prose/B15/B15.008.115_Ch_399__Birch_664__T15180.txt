 Erest of the menewelle west ouer &t;an bourne on stroddunecrundel &t;anne on &t;e elde dich end lange dich on stancangoren &t;anen on smalegoren. 
 &T;anen on goteburgh. 
 &T;anen on bradenburgh &t;anen on aganpol. 
 &T;anen nether endlang brokes on egenwelle. 
 &T;anen on rigt to redencnolle &t;anen to moscrundel &t;anen to nelesburghe &t;anen to stoncroundel. 
 &T;anen to &t;an sladen. 
 &T;anen to Gildeneburgh &t;anen eft on &t;e merewelle of alwoldesmede tenacres auingmede &t;anen on Winterburne and so to Woderede erest on heddengraue &t;ane to athamme &t;anen to enlipanberwe. 
 &T;anen to haldanleghe &t;anen eft on heddengraue.

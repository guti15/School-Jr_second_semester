 Her suytele&t; on &t;ise wrtte wilke forwarde Stigand and Alger his prest Wrouhten &t;o he let him &t;at lond to hande at Playford. 
 &T;at is &t;at Alger prest habbe &t;at lond his day and ouer his day fonge Stigand &t;erto gif he leng libbe. 
 And ouer here bo&t;ere day go &t;at lond into seynt Eadmunde mid mete and mid manne and mid alle &t;inge so it &t;anne tyled beth buten alken gentale. 
 And he it ne may ney&t;er ne forsegen ne forwerken. 
 &T;is aren II witnesse  Alfric bisscop &amp; Vui Abbot &amp; al se hird binnen seynt Eadmundes biri and Stigand and Aelfwine Wluardes sune &amp; Alfric Withgares sune &amp; Eadric and Ordger &amp; Lemmer and manie o&t;ere. 
 &T;ise wrtte sinden tua  on is binnen seynt Eadmundes biri &amp; o&t;er haui&d; Stigand.

 Hir sinda dam landgem&ae;re into Sudwellan. 
 Of dam forda andlang greotan sid se ealdartreau &t;o est be eastan &amp; be sudan dam grete &t;on gerihte &d;urh niwatunes brok sofot on streotan up be broce be sudan sunningale holme &tbar; on bradan lege &t;e astey forde &t;an on to coitan of coitan andlang strat on holan broc andlang broces &tbar; up be &t;an hlmecan furlang wid innan &t;ane graf &tbar; ut on &t;a ac of &tbar; fre ac a be &t;an heafdan on &t;one nordran &t;orn of &t;am &t;orne on streatan andlang streatan &tbar; eft on &t;one ford. 
 Hir sint &t;a longemaere et Normantune. 
 Of streatan to &t;ere dichacce andlang &t;an hagan on &t;aene broc andlang broces &tbar; on &t;a dene andlang dene a be &t;an heafdan on dam lenbroc of &t;an on luse a be &t;are lucse on &t;a ealdarn streot andlang straet &tbar; eft on steotan. 
 Hir sindan hocer wuda of &t;e fre strat ofer &t;aene mor to dreas haga nordeweardan &t;anen up to dare aeppeltreou of &t;ere appelstreou on gerihte ofer mid &t;aene miclan beorh in on &t;aene mor on &tbar; fule fleot andlang fleotes &tbar; eft on streatan. 
 Hir sint &t;a landegmare to Fiscetune. 
 Andlang Streotan on Traentan on haseleford of dam forda andlang streot to moorn dune of sy forndune on gerihton on niwantunes broc &amp;lang broces &tbar; east on streotan. 
 Hir sint dam tunes de birad in to Sudwellan mid sacce &amp; mid sacne Fearnes feld.

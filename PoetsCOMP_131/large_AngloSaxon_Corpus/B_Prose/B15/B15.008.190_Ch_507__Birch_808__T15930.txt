 This sindon tha land gemere the Eadmund king gebocade in to sancte Eadmunde. 
 Thonne is th&ae;r &ae;rest suth be eahta treowen. 
 And thonne up be Alhmundes treowen. 
 And swa forth to Osulfes lea. 
 And swa forth on gerihte be manige hyllan. 
 And thonnen up Hamarlunda. 
 And swa forth to fouuer hagas &amp; sua &ae;fter than we se Lytlantune &amp; thonen ofer tha ea &ae;fter than wege to Bertenedene. 
 And swa on gerichte east to Holegate. 
 And swa forth an furlang be easten Bromleage &amp; thonan suth to niwantune meaduwe.

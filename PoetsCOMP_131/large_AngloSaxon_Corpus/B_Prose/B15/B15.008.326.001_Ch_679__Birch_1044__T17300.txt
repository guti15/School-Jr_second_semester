 &D;is sint &d;a landgemara to Scroppen &t;orpe. 
 And yuresby. 
 Of langanforda dic andlang dices &tbar; on &t;a miclan dic andlang dices on blidan et celfa se yeade. 
 Andlang blidan on beo lese su&d;erewearde &tbar; up da miclan strat andlang strat ut &t;urh beolease nor&d; ut &t;one feld &tbar; adun on blidan up be blidan on iddil. 
 Up be iddel on &t;one fulan broc andlang brocci eft on langanford. 
 &D;is sint &d;a landgemaree to Suttune. 
 Of fulanforda andlang stret to &t;am drape &tbar; on geriht mid &t;one draf on &t;one broc andlang broces on langan ford. 
 On gerihte of scyrinda midde weardne on iddel at brodan fleote &tbar; up a be iddel eft on fulan forde. 
 &D;onne is sisotef land binnan &d;isum gemaree.

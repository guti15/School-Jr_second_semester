 Erest on cristemalforde endlang afne on an litil dich endlang &t;ar dich eft on afne on clifwere &t;anen on &t;e withibed &t;anen on &t;e mere fourh and soa on rigt over dauntesbourne. 
 On &t;er ellene stub eft on &t;er brembelwernan eft on &t;er merhawen eft on swinesheved on &t;e grete mapildore &t;anen on &t;a olde oden missenne. 
 Endland &t;is clives on &t;en hen ayssh on hoddisclive foryerd bi wirtwalen on sand riddriate on &t;e hage &t;ar eft on grete tre endlangis hawyn on &t;e elde strete. 
 Eft on teonescanen southward &t;anne endlanges &t;are brodefurgh on &t;e hegeberghes in on &t;e toun northward endelang &t;ar forerthe be a aker heved &t;ar eft on cristemalforde.

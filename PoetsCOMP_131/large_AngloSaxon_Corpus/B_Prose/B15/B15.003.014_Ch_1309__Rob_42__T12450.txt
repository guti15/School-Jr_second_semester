 Hindehlyp  &AE;lfild &amp; Cynelm &amp; &AE;&t;elgerd. 
 Ic Oswald bisceop &t;urh Godes giefe mid ge&t;afunge &amp; leafe Eadgares Angulcyningces &amp; &AE;lfheres Mercna heretogan &amp; &t;&ae;s heorodes on Wiogornaceastre landes sumne d&ae;l &t;&ae;t synd III hida &t;e fram cu&t;um mannum Hindehlep is gehaten sumum wife &t;&ae;re is noma &ae;lfild for Godes lufon &amp; for uncre sibbe mid eallum &t;ingum &t;e &t;&ae;rto belimpa&d; freolice hiere d&ae;g forgieaf. 
 &amp; &ae;fter hiere d&ae;ge twam yrfeweardum &amp; &ae;fter heora for&d;si&t;e to &t;&ae;re halgan stowe into Wiogornaceastre &t;am biscope to bryce. 
 Si hit &ae;lces &t;inges freoh butan ferdfare &amp; walgeworc &amp; brycgeworc &amp; circanlade. 
 &T;is w&ae;s gedon ymbe nigon hund wintra &amp; seox &amp; seoxtig &t;&ae;s &t;e Drihtnes gebyrdtide w&ae;s on &t;y seofo&t;an geare &t;&ae;s &t;e Oswald bisceop to folga&d;e feng. 
 Sancta Maria &amp; sanctus Michahel cum sancto Petro &amp; eallum Godes halgum gemiltien &t;is haldendum. 
 Gif hwa buton gewrihtum hit abrecan wille God hine to rihtere bote gecerre. 
 Her is seo hondseten Oswaldes biscopes.

 Carta Willelmi Regis Senioris de libertate ecclesie. 
 Willelm kyng gret Walchelm biscop &amp; Hugan de Port &amp; Eadward scirgerefan &amp; Odan &amp; &AE;gelsi &amp; Saulf &amp; &AE;lfsi &ae;t H&ae;ccan &amp; Cole &amp; Eadric &amp; ealle his &t;egnas on Hamtunscyre &amp; on Wiltunscyre frondlice. 
 And ic ky&d;e eow &t;&ae;t ic wille &t;&ae;t Sancte Peter &amp; Walchelm biscop beon ealra &t;are lagena weor&d;e &t;e was &AE;lwine biscop on Eadwerdes dage kynges swa full &amp; swa he hi betst ahte. 
 Nu for &t;ig bidde ic eow &amp; festlice beode &t;&ae;t ge ne latan for me ne for nanan o&d;ran men &t;&ae;t ge ne ky&d;an &t;&ae;t so&d;este for &t;an ic nelle ge&t;olian &t;&ae;t &ae;nig man Sancte Petre ne Walkeline biscop &ae;nige o&d;re lage beode.

 Erest on smalmoresheued suthe end lang mors al so &t;e pil schet of red mor estward so south to pille endlang pilles on mirranford. 
 &T;anen on wernanford mudeward op on wernanstreme of &t;e pirie &t;anen liggith &t;ritti acres &t;is kingis and &t;anen geth hit est on werne. 
 Op endlang wernestremes on bradan mores heued &t;anen on &t;e smalehegrewe on widanleighe westward &t;anen on fautesham northward endlang weies on &t;e olde dich on swicombesheued on wolcombesheued for biethe of. 
 Wolschern westward &t;anen on holanwei upward on &t;an stone so eure bi eue on snapan westward &t;anen on stanwei vpward endlang weies on russeleighe upward so on henleighe westward &t;anen on wirtroneshoe on peret westward &t;anen on middemor endlang mores eft on smalemor. 
 &T;anen is &t;is merelande other manne &t;are kinge and &t;are bisschop on pulisswiran to &t;an seuen hidin at hamme to &t;e come to Hiwissh tuelfacres.

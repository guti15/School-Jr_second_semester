 From mulpol efter &d;e siche to &d;e forde. 
 Of &d;e forde up long gerdes into &d;e weie. 
 On o&d;e stude of rodberdes huse in &d;e strete onlong gerdes to waringes rudinge &d;onene onlon gerdes to &d;e imbe ac. 
 From &d;e imbe ac onlon gerdes to &d;e surftro. 
 From &d;e surftreo onlong gerdes to caue rudinge. 
 &D;enene onlong gierdes to &d;a linde. 
 &D;enene onlong gierdes to henne lega &d;ennene dunewardes onlong gerdes to hole fiche. 
 &D;onene uppard hole fiche to &d;am hauede. 
 From &d;am hauede onlong gierdes to &d;am stodfolde. 
 From &d;am stodfolda onlong &d;e olde weie to Edwines huse at &d;e fenne. 
 &D;onene onlong gierdes to wibbe cumbes hauede. 
 &D;enene onlong gierdes under dune to &d;e wulf putte. 
 Of &d;e wulf putte onlong gierdes to totes stige. 
 &D;onene onlong gierde to &d;am eowe.

 Wita &d;a &t;e nu beo&d; &amp; &d;a te cumene sy &t;at yc Osbern Execestres biscop gef leaua &d;am munche on Sancte Nicholaus minstre to hringinde hyre tyde be dage &amp; be nihte  hwanne hy efre willat swa swa belymp&d; to hire andebernisse  bute an Cristes masseniht  &amp; giester sunneue  &amp; Sancte Petres and Paules massedeg. 
 And &t;eos leaua &t;urh hyre abbed Henri  &amp; toforan him &amp; his munchun &amp; toforan eallum mynum canunche innan minum capitule of myne &amp; hyre eallre helf swa fastlice ys ysett  &t;at non man after &t;ys &d;as ysettinge undon ne mage. 
 And for &t;yse leaua  twygys elce gere &t;at is an Palmsunnendeg  &amp; Cristes upstigan deg  to processiun mid &t;am canunche hy gan sceule.

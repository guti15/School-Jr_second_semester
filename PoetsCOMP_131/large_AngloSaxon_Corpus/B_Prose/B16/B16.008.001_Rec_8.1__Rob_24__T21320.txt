 In nomine domini nostri Ihesu Dhristi. 
 Ic &AE;&t;elstan cyning selle &t;as boc into sancto Cu&d;berhte &amp; bebeode on Godes noman &amp; on &t;&ae;s halgan weres &t;&ae;t hio n&ae;fre nan monn of &t;isse stowe mid nanum facne ne reaflace ne afirre ne nane &t;ara geofona &t;e ic to &t;isse stowe gedoo. 
 Gif &t;onne hwelc monn to &t;&ae;m dyrstig beo &t;&ae;t he &t;isses hw&ae;t breoce o&d;&d;e wende beo he scyldig wi&t; God &amp; wi&t; menn &amp; d&ae;l neomende Iudases hletes Scariothes  &amp; on Domes d&ae;ge &t;&ae;s egeslican cwides to geheranne &amp; to onfone  discedite a me maledicti in ignem &ae;ternum et reliq.

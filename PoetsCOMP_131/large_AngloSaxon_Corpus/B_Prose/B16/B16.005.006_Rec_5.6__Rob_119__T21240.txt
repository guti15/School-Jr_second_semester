 &T;is forward was makid wid Ordric hordere  &t;at es &t;at aelc man in sancte Eadmundes Byri husfast on his owe land sal gifen to &t;e halegenes Bideripe &t;e hordor on peni at Petermasse on ginninge heruest. 
 An sea &t;at sit on oderes land sea sceal gifen oan halpeni for &t;at he aalle scolden sceren &t;e halegenes corn. 
 Fram &t;is sceal naefre no maen be scyr buton sancte Eademundes &t;ewes &amp; sea cnytes and &t;e preostes inne &t;eo singen at &t;o wyuedes. 
 God seonde heore frieond &t;e worden sancte Eadmund.

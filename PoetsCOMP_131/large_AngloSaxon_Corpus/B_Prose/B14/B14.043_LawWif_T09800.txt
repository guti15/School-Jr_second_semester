 Be wifmannes beweddunge. 
 Gif man m&ae;dan o&d;&d;e wif weddian wille  &amp; hit swa hire &amp; freondan gelicige  &d;onne is riht  &d;&ae;t se brydguma &ae;fter Godes rihte &amp; &ae;fter woroldgerysnum &ae;rest behate &amp; on wedde sylle &d;am  &d;e hire forsprecan synd  &t;&ae;t he on &d;a wisan hire geornige  &d;et he hy &ae;fter Godes rihte healdan wille  swa w&ae;r his wif sceal; &amp; aborgian his frind &d;&ae;t. 
 &AE;fter &d;am is witanne  hwam &d;&ae;t fosterlean gebyrige; weddige se brydguma eft &t;&ae;s; &amp; hit aborgian his frynd. 
 &D;onne sy&d;&d;an cy&t;e se brydguma  hw&ae;s he hire geunge  wi&d; &t;am &d;et heo his willan geceose  &amp; hw&ae;s he hire geunge  gif heo l&ae;ng sy &d;onne he. 
 Gif hit swa geforword bi&d;  &t;onne is riht  &d;&ae;t heo sy healfes yrfes wyr&d;e &amp; ealles  gif hy cild gem&ae;ne habban  bute heo eft w&ae;r ceose. 
 Trymme he eal mid wedde &t;&ae;t &t;&ae;t he behate; &amp; aborgian frynd &t;&ae;t. 
 Gif hy &t;onne &ae;lces &t;inges samm&ae;le beon  &d;onne fon magas to &amp; weddian heora magan to wife &amp; to rihtlife &d;am &d;e hire girnde; &amp; fo to &t;am borge se &d;e &d;&ae;s weddes waldend sy. 
 Gif hy man &d;onne ut of lande l&ae;dan wille on o&d;res &t;egnes land  &d;onne bi&d; hire r&ae;d  &d;&ae;t frynd &d;a forword habban  &d;&ae;t hire man nan woh to ne do  &amp; gif heo gylt gewyrce  &d;&ae;t hy moton beon bote nyhst  gif heo n&ae;f&d;  of hwam heo bete. 
 &AE;t &t;am giftan sceal m&ae;ssepreost beon mid rihte  se sceal mid Godes bletsunge heora gesomnunge gederian an ealre gesundfulnesse. 
 Wel is eac to warnianne  &d;&ae;t man wite  &d;&ae;t hy &d;urh m&ae;gsibbe to gel&ae;nge ne beon  &d;e l&ae;s &d;e man eft tw&ae;me  &d;&ae;t man &ae;r awoh tosomne gedydan.

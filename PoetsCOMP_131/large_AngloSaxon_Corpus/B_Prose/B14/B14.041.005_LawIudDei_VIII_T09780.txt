 Halsie man &d;onne man her. 
 Ic eow halsie &t;urh &d;onne F&ae;der &amp; &t;one Sunu &amp; &t;one Halgan gast &amp; &t;urh heowerne cristendom  &t;e ge underfengon  &amp; &t;urh &t;a halgan rode  &t;e Crist on &d;rowode  &amp; &t;urh &t;a halgan godspel &amp; &d;a reliquias  &t;e on &d;isse cyrcan syndon. 
 &T;&ae;t ge ne genedon  &t;&ae;t ge &t;is husl &d;icgon &amp; to &d;isson weofode ne gan  gif ge &t;is dydon  &t;e ge betogene syndon  o&d;&d;e wiston  hwa hit dide. 
 &D;is is o&t;er hals. 
 Ic halsie &d;e  man  &t;urh ures Drihtnes gefl&ae;scnysse h&ae;lendes Cristes &amp; &t;urh &t;ises w&ae;teres dom &amp; &t;urh &t;a halgan untod&ae;lendlican &t;rynnysse &amp; &t;urh &t;a halgan englas &amp; heahenglas &amp; &t;urh ealle Godes halgan &amp; &t;urh &t;one ondryslican domes d&ae;g &amp; &t;urh &t;a feower &amp; twentig gecorenra  &d;e d&ae;ghwamlice God heria&d;  &amp; &t;urh &t;a twelf apostolas &amp; &t;urh ealle halige &t;roweras &amp; ealle andetteras &amp; ealle halige f&ae;mnan &amp; &t;urh ealle &t;a heofonlican m&ae;gen&d;rymmas &amp; &t;urh &t;a halgan &t;ry cnihtas  &t;e d&ae;ghwamlice God heria&d;  &amp; &t;urh &d;a hunteontig &amp; feower &amp; feowertig &t;usenda &t;rowera  &t;e for Godes naman cwylmede w&ae;ron  &amp; &t;urh &t;&ae;t fulluht  &d;e &t;u on gefullod w&ae;re. 
 &T;&ae;t  gyf &d;u &ae;nig &d;ing &d;isse stale wite o&d;&d;e gewita w&ae;re  &d;onne gedylegie &d;in heorte  &amp; &t;is w&ae;ter &d;e ne underfo. 
 Ne &d;e nan scincr&ae;ft ne gehelppe togeanes &d;isum Godes dome. 
 For&d;an we &d;e &t;onne geornlice bidda&t;  Drihten h&ae;lend Crist  &t;&ae;t &d;u gedo her swilc tacn  gyf &d;es man scyldig sy  &t;&ae;t &d;is w&ae;ter hine ne onfo. 
 Do &t;u &t;onne  drihten h&ae;lend Crist  &t;is to loue &amp; to wuldre &d;inum &t;am halgan naman  &t;&ae;t &d;e oncnawon ealle men  &t;&ae;t &d;u eart gebletsod God  &d;u &d;e leofast &amp; rixast mid F&ae;der &amp; mid halgum Gaste on worulda woruld a butan ende.

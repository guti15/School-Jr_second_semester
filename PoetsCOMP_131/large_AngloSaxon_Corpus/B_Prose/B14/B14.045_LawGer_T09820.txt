 Be gesceadwisan gerefan. 
 Se scadwis gerefa sceal &ae;g&d;&ae;r witan ge hlafordes landriht ge folces gerihtu  be &d;am &d;e hit of ealddagum witan ger&ae;ddan  &amp; &ae;lcre til&d;an timan  &d;e to tune belimp&d;. 
 For&d;am on manegum landum til&d; bi&d; redre &d;onne on o&d;rum: ge yr&d;e tima hr&ae;dra  ge m&ae;da r&ae;dran  ge winterdun eac swa  ge gehwilc o&d;er til&d;. 
 Hede se &d;e scire healde  &t;&ae;t he fri&d;ige &amp; for&d;ige &ae;lce be &d;am &d;e hit selest sy; &amp; be &d;am he eac mot  &d;e hine weder wisa&d;. 
 He sceal snotorlice smeagean &amp; georne &d;urhsmugan ealle &d;a &d;ing  &d;e hlaforde magan to r&ae;de. 
 Gyf he wel aginnan wile  ne m&ae;ig he sleac beon ne to oferhydig; ac he mot &ae;g&d;er witan ge l&ae;sse ge mare  ge betere ge m&ae;tre &d;&ae;s &d;e to tune belimp&d;  ge on tune ge on dune  ge on wuda ge on w&ae;tere  ge on felda ge on falde  ge inne ge ute. 
 For&d;am to so&d;e ic secge: oferhogie he o&d;&d;e forgyme &d;a &d;ing to beganne &amp; to bewitanne  &d;e to scipene o&d;&d;e to odene belimpa&d;  sona hit wyr&d; on berne &t;&ae;t to &d;am belimpa&d;. 
 Ac ic l&ae;re  &t;&ae;t he do  swa ic &ae;r cw&ae;&d;: gyme &ae;g&d;er ge &d;&ae;s selran ge &t;&ae;s s&ae;mran  &t;&ae;t na&d;or ne misfare  gyf he wealdan m&ae;ge  ne corn ne sceaf  ne fl&ae;sc ne flotsmeru  ne cyse ne cyslyb  ne nan &d;era &d;inga &d;e &ae;fra to note m&ae;ge. 
 Swa sceal god scyrman his hlafordes healdan  do ymbe his agen  swa swa he wylle. 
 A swa he gecneordra swa bi&d; he weor&d;ra  gyf he wi&d; witan hafo&d; his wisan gem&ae;ne. 
 Symle he sceal his hyrmen scyrpan mid manunge to hlafordes neode &amp; him eac leanian be &d;am &d;e hy earnian. 
 Ne l&ae;te he n&ae;fre his hyrmen hyne oferwealdan  ac wille he &ae;lcne mid hlafordes creafte &amp; mid folcrihte: selre him his &ae;fre of folgo&d;e &d;onne on  gyf hine magan wyldan &d;a &d;e he scolde wealdan; ne bi&d; hit hlaforde r&ae;d  &t;&ae;t he &t;&ae;t &d;afige. 
 &AE;fre he m&ae;ig findan  on &d;am he m&ae;ig nyt beon &amp; &d;a nytte don  &d;e him fylstan scylan; huru is m&ae;st neod  &t;&ae;t he asece  hu he yrde m&ae;ge fyrme gefor&d;ian  &d;onne &d;&ae;s tima sy. 
 Me m&ae;ig in Maio &amp; Iunio &amp; Iulio on sumera fealgian  myxendincgan ut dragan  lochyrdla tilian  sceap scyran  bytlian  boteatan  tynan  tymbrian  wudian  weodian  faldian  fiscwer &amp; mylne macian.

 On l&ae;ngtene eregian &amp; impian  beana sawan  wingeard settan  dician  deorhege heawan &amp; ra&d;e &ae;fter &d;am  gif hit mot gewiderian  mederan settan  linsed sawan  wads&ae;d eac swa  wyrtun plantian &amp; fela &d;inga; ic eal geteallan ne m&ae;ig  &t;&ae;t god scirman bycgan sceal.

 Hit is earfo&d;e eall to gesecganne  &t;&ae;t se be&d;encan sceal  &d;e scire healt.

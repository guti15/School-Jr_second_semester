 Cnut cyning gret his arcebiscopas &amp; his leodbiscopas &amp; &t;urcyl eorl &amp; ealle his eorlas &amp; ealne his &t;eodscype  twelfhynde &amp; twyhynde  gehadode &amp; l&ae;wede  on Englalande freondlice. 
 &amp; ic cy&d;e eow  &t;&ae;t ic wylle beon hold hlaford &amp; unswicende to Godes gerihtum &amp; to rihtre woroldlage. 
 Ic nam me to gemynde &t;a gewritu &amp; &t;a word  &t;e se arcebiscop Lyfing me fram &t;am papan brohte of Rome  &t;&ae;t ic scolde &ae;ghw&ae;r Godes lof upp ar&ae;ran &amp; unriht alecgan &amp; full fri&d; wyrcean be &d;&ae;re mihte  &t;e me God syllan wolde. 
 Nu ne wandode ic na minum sceattum  &t;a hwile &t;e eow unfri&d; on handa stod; nu ic mid Godes fultume &t;&ae;t totw&ae;mde mid minum scattum. 
 &T;a cydde man me  &t;&ae;t us mara hearm to fundode  &t;onne us wel licode; &amp; &t;a for ic me sylf mid &t;am mannum &t;e me mid foron into Denmearcon  &t;e eow m&ae;st hearm of com. 
 &amp; &t;&ae;t h&ae;bbe mid Godes fultume forene forfangen  &t;&ae;t eow n&ae;fre heonon for&d; &t;anon nan unfri&d; to ne cym&d;  &t;a hwile &t;e ge me rihtlice healda&d; &amp; min lif by&d;. 
 Nu &d;ancige ic Gode &ae;lmihtigum his fultumes &amp; his mildheortnesse  &t;&ae;t ic &t;a myclan hearmas  &t;e us to fundedon  swa gelogod h&ae;bbe  &t;&ae;t we ne &t;urfon &t;anon nenes hearmes us asittan  ac us to fullan fultume &amp; to ahreddingge  gyf us neod by&d;. 
 Nu wylle ic  &t;&ae;t we ealle eadmodlice Gode &ae;lmihtigum &t;ancian &t;&ae;re mildheortnesse  &t;e he us to fultume gedon h&ae;f&d;. 
 Nu bidde ic mine arcebiscopas &amp; ealle mine leodbiscopas  &t;&ae;t hy ealle neodfulle beon ymbe Godes gerihta  &ae;lc on his ende  &t;e heom bet&ae;ht is; &amp; eac minum ealdormannum ic beode  &t;&ae;t hy fylstan &t;am biscopum to Godes gerihtum &amp; to minum kynescype &amp; to ealles folces &t;earfe. 
 Gif hwa swa dyrstig sy  gehadod o&d;&d;e l&ae;wede  Denisc o&d;&d;e Englisc  &t;&ae;t ongean Godes lage ga &amp; ongean minne cynescype o&d;&d;e ongean woroldriht  &amp; nelle betan &amp; geswican &ae;fter minra biscopa t&ae;cinge  &t;onne bidde ic &t;urcyl eorl &amp; eac beode  &t;&ae;t he &d;&ae;ne unrihtwisan to rihte gebige  gyf he m&ae;ge. 
 Gyf he ne m&ae;ge  &t;onne wille ic  mid uncer begra cr&ae;fte &t;&ae;t he hine on earde adw&ae;sce o&d;&d;e ut of earde adr&ae;fe  sy he betera sy he wyrsa. 
 &amp; eac ic beode eallum minum gerefum  be minum freondscype &amp; be eallum &t;am &t;e hi agon &amp; be heora agenum life  &t;&ae;t hy &ae;ghw&ae;r min folc rihtlice healdan &amp; rihte domas deman be &d;&ae;ra scira biscopa gewitnesse &amp; swylce mildheortnesse &t;&ae;ron don  swylce &t;&ae;re scire biscope riht &t;ince &amp; se man acuman m&ae;ge. 
 &amp; gyf hwa &t;eof fri&d;ige o&d;&d;e forena forlicge  sy he emscyldig wi&d; me &t;am &d;e &t;eof scolde  buton he hine mid fulre lade wi&d; me gecl&ae;nsian m&ae;ge. 
 &amp; ic wylle  &t;&ae;t eal &t;eodscype  gehadode &amp; l&ae;wede  f&ae;stlice Eadgares lage healde  &t;e ealle men habba&d; gecoren &amp; to gesworen on Oxenaforda. 
 For &d;am &t;e ealle biscopas secga&d;  &t;&ae;t hit swy&t;e deop wi&d; God to betanne  &t;&ae;t man a&d;as o&d;&d;e wedd tobrece. 
 &amp; eac hy us fur&d;or l&ae;ra&d;  &t;&ae;t we sceolon eallan magene &amp; eallon myhton &t;one ecan mildan God inlice secan  lufian &amp; weor&d;ian &amp; &ae;lc unriht ascunian  &d;&ae;t synd m&ae;gslagan &amp; mor&d;slagan &amp; mansworan &amp; wiccean &amp; w&ae;lcyrian &amp; &ae;wbrecan &amp; syblegeru. 
 &amp; eac we beoda&d; on Godes &ae;lmihtiges naman &amp; on ealra his haligra  &t;&ae;t nan man swa dyrstig ne sy  &t;&ae;t on gehadodre nunnan o&d;&d;e on mynecenan gewifige. 
 &amp; gyf hit hwa gedon h&ae;bbe  beo he utlah wi&d; God &amp; amansumod fram eallum Cristendome &amp; wi&d; &t;one cyning scyldig ealles &t;&ae;s &t;e he age  buton he &d;e ra&d;or geswice &amp; &t;e deopplicor gebete wi&d; God. 
 &amp; gyt we fur&d;or mania&d;  &t;&ae;t man Sunnand&ae;ges freols mid eallum m&ae;gene healde &amp; weor&d;ige fram S&ae;ternesd&ae;ges none o&d; Monand&ae;ges lyhtinge  &amp; nan man swa dyrstig ne sy  &t;&ae;t he a&d;or o&d;&d;e cypinge wyrce o&d;&d;e &ae;nig mot ges&ae;ce &t;am halgan d&ae;ge. 
 &amp; ealle men  earme &amp; eadige  heora cyrcan secean &amp; for heora synnum &t;ingian &amp; &ae;lc beboden f&ae;stan geornlice healdan &amp; &t;a halgan georne weor&d;ian  &t;e us m&ae;ssepreostas beodan sceolan  &t;&ae;t we magan &amp; moton ealle samod &t;urh &t;&ae;s ecean Godes mildheortnesse &amp; his halgena &t;ingr&ae;dene to heofena rices myrh&d;e becuman &amp; mid him wunian  &t;e leofa&d; &amp; rihxa&d; a butan ende.

 Halsuncge. 
 Ic eow &d;e halsige on F&ae;der naman &amp; on Suna naman &d;&ae;t is ure dryhten haelende Crist &amp; on &d;aes halgan Gastes &amp; for &d;&ae;re cristnesse  &d;e ge underfenge &d;u underfengan  &amp; for &d;a haligan &d;rinesse &amp; fer &d;a IIII godspelleras Matheus &amp; Marcus &amp; Lucas et Iohannes &amp; fer ealle &d;a halgan reliquias  &d;e gind ealne middangeard sindon  haligra martyra  &amp; fer ealle &d;a halgan Godes ciricean  &t;e her on weorolde gehalgode sien  &amp; fer naman &d;aere halgan faemnan sancta Marian. 
 &T;&ae;t ge to &t;ys husle ne gangen ne to &d;&ae;m ordale  gif ge scyld on eow &d;e witen &d;&ae;s &d;e eow &d;e man tih&d; o&d;&d;e on gewyrhtum o&d;&d;e on gewitnesse.

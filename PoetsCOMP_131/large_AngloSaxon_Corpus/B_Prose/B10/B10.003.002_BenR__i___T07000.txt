 Be godra weorca tolum. 
 &AE;rest man sceal god lufian of ealre heortan and of eallum mode and of eallum m&ae;gene and &ae;fter &t;on his nehstan swa swa hine sylfne. 
 Ne sceal man manslean  ne unriht h&ae;man  ne stelan  ne unalyfedlice gelustfullian  ne leasunga secgan  ac &ae;lcene man man sceal arwur&t;ian and ne sceal nan man don o&d;rum  &t;&ae;t he nelle  &t;&ae;t him man do. 
 Man sceal hys agenum lustum wi&d;sacun and folgian Cristes lare  his lichaman gehwa sceal cl&ae;nsian  &t;&ae;t ys mid forh&ae;fednyssed wyllan; estmyttas na to gr&ae;diglice man ne sceal lufian  ac f&ae;sten man sceal lufian. 
 Earmne man sceal hyrtan and nacode scrydan; untrume neosian and deade bebyrgean; geswectum helpan and sarigende frefrian. 
 And fram woruldwilnungum hine sceal gehwa fremdian and nan &t;inc beforan Cristes lufe settan. 
 Yrre ne sceal man fulfremman; yrsunge tide ne sceal man gehealdan; facn ne sceal man on heortan habban; lease sibbe ne sceal man syllan; so&d;e lufe ne sceal man forl&ae;tan. 
 Swerigean ne seal man  &t;e lys man forswerige; so&d;f&ae;stnysse of heortan and of mu&d;e man sceal symle for&d;bringan. 
 Ne sceal man yfel mid yfele gyldan  ne nanum men n&ae;nne teone ne don  ac &t;one &t;e him man yfel do  he sceal ge&t;yldelice hit ar&ae;fnian; his find man sceal lufian for Godes lufan; &t;a wyrgendan ne sceal man na ongeanwyrgean  ac me bletsian; ehtnysse for rihte man sceal eadmodlice &t;olian. 
 Ne sceal man beon ofermod  ne to druncengeorn  ne for&ae;te  ne toslapol  ne to slaw  ne to grorniende  ne to t&ae;lende. 
 Ac ealne is tohopan he sceal habban on God; &t;onne he hw&ae;t godes de&d;  &t;onne sceal he &t;&ae;t he all tellan to Gode  and &t;onne he hw&ae;t yfeles de&d;  he sceal witan &t;&ae;t &t;&ae;t cym&d; of him sylfum. 
 Domesd&ae;g he ge&t;encan sceal symle  and ellewitu he him sceal a ondr&ae;dan  and &t;&ae;s ecan lifes he sceal mid ealre geornfulnysse gyrnan  and &ae;lce d&ae;ge he him dea&d;es wenan. 
 His weorca he sceal giman on &ae;lce tide  &t;&ae;t &t;a gode syn  and he sceal ge&t;encan  &t;&ae;t he nahw&ae;r Gode digle ne bi&d;  ac he hine &ae;ghw&ae;r gesih&d;; &t;a yfelan ge&t;ohtas  &t;e him on mod becuma&d;  he sceal sona on Crist toslean and his gaslican lareowe andettan. 
 &T;onne he hi tosleh&d; on Criste  &t;onne he ge&t;enca&d; Cristes &t;rowunga and his wundra and mid &t;am ge&t;ohtum aflem&d; &t;a yfelan ge&t;ohtas. 
 His mu&d; he sceal fram &ae;lcum &t;weoran and yfelum wordum gehealdan; ne sceal he feala sprecan  ne yfel word ne leahtorb&ae;re; ne leahtor ne sceal he lufian. 
 Halige r&ae;dinge he sceal lustlice gehyran and gelome on gebedum beon; his for&d;gewitenan yflu he sceal d&ae;ghwamlice mid tearum Gode andettan and hi georne betan. 
 Ne sceal he his lichaman luste folgian; his agene willan he sceal onscunian  and his abbodes bebodum on eallum &t;ingum gehyrsumian  &t;eah hit si  swa swa hit ne gedafena&d;  &t;&ae;t he wyrs do  &t;onne he l&ae;re; hi sceolon gemunon &t;one drihtenlican cwyde  on &t;am he cw&ae;&d;: &t;&ae;t &t;&ae;t hie eow secgean  do ge &t;&ae;t and &t;&ae;t hi don  ne do ge hw&ae;t. 
 Ne se man lofgeorn  ne ne wilnigende  &t;&ae;t hys d&ae;da halige ges&ae;de syn  &ae;r hie halige geweor&d;an ac hicge  &t;&ae;t hit mid so&d;e ges&ae;d beon m&ae;ge; ne sceal Godes bebodu d&ae;gwamlice mid godum d&ae;dum gyfelled and his cl&ae;nnysse lufian; ne sceal he nanne man hatian; ne sceal he andian and &ae;fst healdan  ne geflyt lufian; ne sceal uphafednesse forfleon and his ealdras wyr&d;ian and his gingrun lufian; for Godes lufan for hyra fynd hie sceal gebiddan; &ae;r sunnan setlgange hie scylon to sibbe fon wi&d; &t;a unge&t;weran; and he n&ae;re ne ortrywe be Godes miltse. 
 Efne nu &t;is synt gastlices cr&ae;ftes tol and gebetla; &t;onne heo fram hus d&ae;ges and nihtes unablendelice beo&d; ealle gefyllede and on domesd&ae;ge eft bet&ae;hte  seo med us bi&d; fram drihtne agolden  &t;e he sylfa behet  &t;&ae;t his: &t;&ae;t m&ae;nnissce eage for heora micelnysse geseon ne mihte; ne manne eare gehyran  ne on mannes heortan ne astah  &t;e God &ae;lmihtig gegearwa&d; eallum &t;am  &t;e hine lufia&d;. 
 On mynstres claustre geferr&ae;dene sta&d;olf&ae;stnys ealle &t;as &t;enunge man began sceal and geornfullice wyrcean.

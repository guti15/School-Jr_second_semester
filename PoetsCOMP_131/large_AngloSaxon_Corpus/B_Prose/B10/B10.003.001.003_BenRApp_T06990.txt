 BE MUNECA CYNNE. 
 Syx synt muneca cynerena  &t;ara synt &t;reo &t;a selestan  &t;a o&t;ere &t;reo &t;a forcu&t;estan and eallum gemete to forbugenne. 
 &T;&ae;t forme cyn is mynstermanna  &t;e gem&ae;nan life drohtnia&d; on mynstre  gelice &t;am halgum  &t;e &t;&ae;m apostolum folgodon and ealle &ae;hta becypton and &t;earfum todeldon and swa for&d; heora gem&ae;ne lif heoldon  &t;&ae;t hy for&t;on &t;&ae;t word na cw&ae;don  &t;&ae;t hi agen &ae;nig &t;ing h&ae;fdon. 
 &T;urh &t;ara apostola angin and &t;ara  &t;e him folgodan  munuclif wurdon begunnene. 
 O&t;er cyn is muneca  &t;&ae;t is westensetlan  &t;e feor fram mannum gewita&d; and westestowa and &ae;l&ae;tu and anwunung gelufia&d;  geefenl&ae;cende Elian  &t;one m&ae;ran witegan  and Johannem  &t;one fulluhtere  &t;a &t;urhwununge on westenes inno&t;e heoldon. 
 Swilce westensetlan eornestlice &t;isne middangeard forhogia&t; and on westenes wununge gelustfullia&d;  be wyrtum anum libbende  o&t;&t;e be hlafe and wetere  se him bi&t; broht on gesettum timum. 
 Swa dihlice wuniende hy fram manna gesyh&d;e ascyria&t; and Godes agenre spr&ae;ce gelomlice bruca&t;  &t;&ae;m hy mid cl&ae;num mode hy sylfe be&t;eoddan and for his lufan &t;isne middangeard and manna ge&t;eodr&ae;denne forsawan. 
 &T;ridde cyn is ansetlena  &t;e on mynsteres wununge fulfremede sindon and hie sylfe on syndrigum husum for Godes lufan beluca&t; and hy sylfe fram manna gesyh&t;e ascyriende nara manna neal&ae;cynge na underfo&t;  ac on Godes besceawunge anre libba&t;. 
 Feor&d;e cyn is &t;ara  &t;e hy under leasum hiwe ansetlan telia&t;. 
 Be swa geradum Sanctus Casianus cwy&t;  &t;&ae;t hi mid ungesceade on &t;am frumwylme heora gecyrrednesse hy sylfe fulfremede talia&t;  ac hy swi&t;e recene awlacia&t; and swindende acolia&t;  &t;onne hy &t;a ealdan un&d;eawas and leahtras ascunian nella&t;  ne ea&t;modnesse iuc and ge&t;yldes na leng befeolan nella&t;  ne heora ealdra gebodum hyrsumian nella&t;  for&t;i ansetles wununge geceosa&t;  &t;&ae;t hi ge&t;w&ae;re and ea&t;mode ge&t;uhte syn and hi nan man gegremian ne dyrre. 
 Ne wyr&t; n&ae;fre fulfremed  se &t;e on &t;us niwan anginne ansetla beon wile. 
 Ne beo&d; his un&t;eawas gewanode mid &t;ysum anginne  ac swi&d;e miclum geeacnode; &d;onne heora un&d;eawas fram nanum men gebette ne beo&t;  eal hi weaxa&t; on yfel  swa swa atres unlibba on men  swa he leng &t;&ae;s attres &t;igene bedihla&d;  swa his untrumnes mare. 
 Nan man ne dear for arwyr&d;nesse &t;&ae;s ansetlan leahtras t&ae;lan; him synt eac &t;a ungesel&t;a leofran  &t;&ae;t he hy nyte  &t;&ae;nne he hi lacnige. 
 Fifte cyn is widscri&t;elra hleapera  &t;e under muneces gegyrlan &ae;ghwider scri&t;a&t;  swilce hy licetunge to ceape l&ae;dan; &d;a &t;urh nanes mannes sande ne fara&d;  fara&t; &t;eah geond missenlice &t;eoda  nefre sta&t;olfeste  n&ae;fre wuniende  nahwar sittende. 
 Idele and lease spel hi hywia&t; and mannum recca&t;  be wenan hi healda&t; God &ae;lmihtigne; sume martyra lima geahnia&t;  and an tweon is  hw&ae;der hy martyras syn; sume heora fnada and wr&ae;das gemicclia&t;  idel lof fram mannum begytende. 
 Sume sidfeaxe ga&t;  &t;&ae;t seo bescorene halignes ne sy weor&t;re  &t;enne seo unbescorene  &t;&ae;t hy &t;urh &t;&ae;t wi&d;metene syn Samuele and Elian and o&t;erum halgum  &t;e sidfeaxe w&ae;ron. 
 Sume secga&t;  &t;&ae;t hy micelne weordscype and rice magas on heora earde h&ae;fdon and &t;a eft geneosian willa&t;; ealle hi bidda&d; and wilnia&t; &t;a heafene &t;ysse gestreonfullan w&ae;dle  o&t;&t;e his lease hiw on halignesse wyr&d;. 
 &D;onne hy on heora d&ae;dum and on yfelum wordum arasode beo&d; and on sumon gemete onfundene  on gem&ae;num naman muneca ingehyd by&d; gehyrewed. 
 &T;&ae;t sixte muneca cyn is ealra forcu&t;ost and swi&d;ost forsewen  &t;&ae;t asprang on anginne cristendomes &t;urh Annanian and Saphiran  and mid &t;es apostoles Petres re&t;nysse wurdon forsl&ae;gene  for&t;am &t;e hy ascyria&d; hy sylfe fram mynsterlicum &t;eawum and heora agenum lustum filia&t;  hy synt egyptiscan gereorde genemnede sarabagite o&d;&d;e renuite  &t;&ae;t ys sylfedeman and widersacan. 
 Hi tymbria&t; him stowa and hy leasum naman mynstra hata&t;  nella&d; hy ealdras habban  ne be heora tecinge libban on &t;eowdome  ac be heora agenum lustum. 
 To geflites hi swinca&d; on weorce  no to&t;i  &t;&ae;t hi heora geswinces gestreon &t;earfum delen  ac &t;&ae;t hy feoh gestrynen and on hyrde lecgen; be heom cwy&d; Sanctus Hieronimus  swa hw&ae;t swa hy heora geswinces becypa&t;  &t;&ae;t by&t; maran wyr&d;es  swilce heora cr&ae;ft sy halig and na heora lif. 
 So&t;lice  se sylfa Hieronimus be heom cwy&t;  to geflites hy f&ae;sta&t; and  &t;&ae;t digle &t;ing beon scolde  to sige  &t;&ae;t is to bodunge and to getotes gylpe  gewyrca&t;. 
 Hy gelyst ealces ydeles  habba&t; side earmellan and pohhede hosa  sti&t;e reaf gelomlice hy anscunia&t;  f&ae;mnhadesmen hi geneosia&t;  preostas t&ae;la&t; and &t;onne freolstid bi&t;  hy beo&t; oferfyllede o&t; spiwe&t;an. 
 Betweoh cenobium and monasterium &t;yllic todal wyrh&d; Sanctus Casianus  &t;&ae;t monasterium m&ae;g beon gehaten anes muneces wunung  cenobium ne by&d; genemned buton manegra wunung. 
 &T;ara drohtnunge ic gesweotelige  swa swa ure for&t;f&ae;deras hit gesettan. 
 Hi witodlice  swa swa we &ae;r cw&ae;don  &ae;rest &t;is lif forseo&d; and ealles &t;ysses lifes unalyfednessa  and halige drohtnunge on gem&ae;num life healda&t;  heora lif hy adreoga&t; on halgum gebedum  on r&ae;dincgum  on smegeongum  on w&ae;ccum  on f&ae;stenum  ne synt hy na to&t;undenne mid &ae;nigre modignesse  ne synt hy &ae;ttrene &t;urh andan  ac hy synt gemetf&ae;ste and gl&ae;de and sceamf&ae;ste  and ge&t;w&ae;rum life hy lifia&t;  heora ge&t;ohtas him betweonan anwreo&d;  gem&ae;nelice hy smeaga&t; and rihta&d;. 
 Nan heora eor&d;lices nan &t;ing synderlice nah  mid deorwyr&t;um reafum and gefagedum ne beo&t; hy gescrydde  ac mid unweor&t;um and anfealdum; ba&t;a hy n&ae;fre bruca&t; for heora lichoman luste  ac seldon for heora untrumnesse neode. 
 Hy nahwider fara&t; butan &t;&ae;s abbodes r&ae;de  ne nan &t;incg beginna&t; butan &t;&ae;s ealdres ge&t;afuncge; hy wyrca&t; mid heora handum  &t;&ae;t hyra lichoma mid afedd sy and heora mod fram drihtne ne sy gehremmed. 
 Mid heora handum wyrcende heora sealmas singa&t;  swylce hy heora geswinc mid godcundre gladunge gefremmen. 
 &T;&ae;t &t;&ae;t hy wyrca&t;  hy bet&ae;ca&t; hit heora teo&t;ingealdrum  &t;&ae;t hy butan hoge and care syn ealra &t;inga  &t;e to heora lichoman belimpe&d;  o&t;&t;e on &ae;te  o&t;&t;e on scrude  o&t;&t;e on o&t;rum neadbehefum &t;ingum. 
 &D;a teo&t;incgealdras &t;&ae;t ylce &t;am prafestan bet&ae;ca&t;  se prafast &t;onne &t;&ae;t mid micelre embhydignesse fada&t; and gegearewa&t;  swa hw&ae;t swa heora lif for lichamlicre untrumnesse behofa&t;; se sylfa profast gescead agylt &t;em  &t;e hy f&ae;der nemna&t;  &t;&ae;t is &t;&ae;m abbode. 
 &T;a federas &t;&ae;nne scealon beon forem&ae;re on andgite  on &t;ylde  on gesceade; hi &t;onne ea&t;mode and nan &t;incg modige framiga&t; heora bearnum  mid miclan heora gebodes ealdorscipe and mid micclan willan heora beorna hyrsumnysse. 
 Hy gegaderia&t; hy swi&t;e gelomlice d&ae;ges and nihtes mid micclan ofste to heora tidsangum  gesealdum beacne; mid micelre gymenne heora heortan heora tida singa&t;  o&t; &t;&ae;s sealmsanges ende butan &ae;&t;rote &t;urhwuniende. 
 Eft hy gaderia&t; hy on nixtnig  &t;&ae;t hy raca gehyren &ae;t heora f&ae;der. 
 Hi &t;onne be heora race gehyra&t; mid micelre gecneordnesse and mid healicum swigean  mid geomrunge and mid wope hi getacnia&t; heora modes lufe  be &t;am &t;e hy gemyndige beo&t; mid &t;&ae;s reccendun spr&ae;ce. 
 Hy si&t;&t;an heora lichoman mid micelre swigan gereorda&t;  swa micel &t;icggende  swa hy heora h&ae;le mid gehealden megen; on eallum &ae;tum mid forh&ae;fednesse gifernesse gewilnunge wilda&t;  &t;&ae;t heora heortan fur&t;um mid wacum mettum and unweor&t;um ne syn ofersymede. 
 Witodlic fram fl&ae;sce and wine hy forhabba&t;  to&t;y &t;&ae;t hy &ae;lcne galscype on hy gewylden  na &t;&ae;t an  ac fram eallum &t;am &t;igenum  &t;e hracan o&t;&t;e inno&t; to miclum luste getyh&t;. 
 Eallunga swa hw&ae;t swa hy gesparia&t; on heora forh&ae;fedness&ae; and swa hw&ae;t swa toforan &t;am neadbehefum belifen by&t; on heora m&ae;genes tilunge  mid swi&t;e micelre embhydignesse &t;earfum ged&ae;la&t; and swa micel to lafe l&ae;ta&t;  swa hy nede habban scylan. 
 To &t;es halgan &t;eowdomes ingehyde cuma&t; na &t;a ane  &t;e freo synt  ac gyt ma &t;a  &t;e &ae;htborene synt and o&t;era manna freosceattas and for &t;am anum foroft gefreode. 
 Of cyrliscum life and of folclicum gedeorfe gehwelces creftes men to &t;am ylcum Godes &t;eowdomes gecampe gecyrra&t;  &t;a butan tweon swa micele ea&d;elicor and sel drohtnia&t;  swa hy sti&t;lice afedde w&ae;ron. 
 Gif man swilce underfon nelle  hit by&t; swi&d;e hefigtyme gylt. 
 Heora form&ae;nig forem&ae;re wear&t; and earfo&t;e to geefenl&ae;cenne  for&t;y God &t;a unstrangan &t;yses middangeardes geceas  &t;&ae;t &t;a strangmodan  &t;e on agenum mihtum truwia&t;  gescende wurdon. 
 &T;a he eac geceas  &t;e dyselice and nahtlice ge&t;uhte synt  &t;&ae;t &t;a  &t;e mid ofermettum hy sylfe for aht telia&t;  aidele  &t;&ae;t nan &t;ing fl&ae;sclices beforan Gode mid getote ne bogie. 
 Witodlice  &t;am ylcum gemete wunia&t; and gyt a&t;olede synt munecena mynstru  &t;e man hurum dryhtne ymbhydelice and clenlice on &t;eowia&t;; &t;ara wunung is ascyred and asyndrod feor fram munecum; hi beo&t; him &t;eah ge&t;eodde mid so&t;re lufe and mid &ae;f&ae;snesse heora halignysse and mid efenl&ae;cinge haligra m&ae;gena. 
 Nis nanra geongra manna neosung to &t;&ae;m  ne for&t;on &t;eah hy fulripode syn and wel afandode  buton &t;ara anra  &t;e for neode him &t;enunge &ae;t &t;&ae;s mynstres ingange r&ae;can scylon. 
 Heora wealda&t; anlipige sta&t;alfeste and wel afandode and on heora ealdorscype heora under&t;eodra &t;eawas gesta&t;elia&t; and gefr&ae;tewia&t;  ne &t;&ae;t an  ac gyt swi&t;or heora mod getimbria&t;  gel&ae;ra&t; and gegearwia&t;. 
 Mid willenum reafe heora lichoman gegearwia&t; and gescyrda&t;  hy munecum reaf sylla&t; and munecas him bileofan. 
 Ane &t;eawas  an lif and ane gesceadwisne hi healda&t; muneca mynstra on eallum &t;ingum. 
 Munucas &t;urh heora ea&t;modnesse hy beo&t; gecorene and Gode andfencge. 
 Heora form&ae;nig mid idelum lofe by&t; besmittad and mit &t;am leasum gylpe beswicen  manige hy anhebba&t; for heora miclan forh&ae;fednesse  menige eac for heora lare. 
 God hy wyrca&t;  ac &t;&ae;t for idelum lofe and na for ecum life  ac &t;urh &t;&ae;t gewilnia&t; ideles lofes wulder  &t;&ae;t hy mid to&t;undennysse cumen m&ae;gen to ge&t;inc&d;e &t;&ae;s gewilnedan wyr&t;myntes. 
 Betweoh swylcum unge&t;w&ae;rnessa aspringa&t;; gif hwylc bro&t;or on Gode &t;yh&t;  on &t;&ae;t hy andia&t;  him sih&t; on &t;yssa hwilwendra &t;inga lufu  hi swi&t;e gr&ae;dilice eor&t;cundum lustum filiga&t; and oft swi&t;e sceamleaslice on manna gesyh&t;e. 
 &T;a &t;e &t;yllice beo&t;  nateshwon hy na synt to nemnenne munecas  for&t;y &t;e hy ne sint Gode ge&t;eodde mid godum d&ae;dum  ac mid worda andetnesse. 
 BE MYNSTRES SACERDUM AND HYRA &T;ENUM. 
 Gif hwylc abbod ge&t;afa&t;  &t;&ae;t m&ae;ssepreost o&t;&t;e diacon in to mynstre gange  to&t;y &t;&ae;t hy messan singan and &t;a andrysnan &t;enunge mid arwyr&t;nesse gefyllen  syn hy swa gecorene  &t;&ae;t hy unt&ae;le syn and sacerdhades &t;urh ealle gode cysta wel wyr&t;e. 
 Syn hy cl&ae;ngeorne  ge&t;yldige and ea&t;mode  bewarnian hy georne wi&t; upah&ae;fednesse and modignesse; ne hy nan &t;ing ne gedyrstl&ae;cen  ne n&ae;nne ontige on &t;am mynstre butan &t;&ae;re m&ae;ssan anre. 
 Ne hy nane spr&ae;ce wi&t; &t;a gebro&t;ra agen; ne hy &t;&ae;rinne leng ne wunien  &t;onne seo m&ae;sse gesungen sy  ac sona swa seo m&ae;sse gesungen sy  hi ea&t;modlice utgan. 
 Warnian &t;a gebro&t;ra georne  &t;&ae;t hy nane syndrigan spr&ae;ce wi&t; &t;a m&ae;ssepreostas n&ae;bben  ne wi&t; heora &t;eningmen. 
 Gif &t;ara gebro&t;ra hwylc his diglan synna &t;&ae;m m&ae;ssepreoste andettan wille  sy &t;&ae;t gedon be &t;&ae;s abbodes leafe innan cirican  na on digelre stowe  ac swa  &t;&ae;t &t;a gebro&t;ra him onlocian m&ae;gen  ealswa hit &t;urh &t;a halgan f&ae;deras beboden is. 
 Gif hyra hwylc geuntrumad bi&t;  &t;&ae;t he ciricean gegan ne m&ae;ge  do his andetnesse on &t;an huse  &t;e he onwuna&t;  on gesyh&t;e his gebro&t;ra. 
 Gif heora hwylc butan &t;&ae;re neode and butan &t;&ae;s abbodes h&ae;se gedyrstl&ae;c&t;  &t;&ae;t he &ae;nige spr&ae;ce wi&t; hy age  eawunga o&t;&t;e dearnunga  he sy ge&t;read mid &t;&ae;re sti&t;estan &t;reale. 
 H&ae;bbe se abbod swa gecorenne m&ae;ssepreost  &ae;g&t;&ae;r ge deacon ge subdiacon  and Gode swa gecweme &t;urh halige drohtnunge  &t;&ae;t him nan unhlisa ne fylge &t;urh &ae;nigne fracodscype  ac heora goda hlisa gearweor&t;ige &t;a  &t;e hy to &t;&ae;re m&ae;ssan &t;enunge gecuren.

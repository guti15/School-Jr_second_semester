 Biscpas scoldan symle Godes riht bodian and unriht forbeodan  and witodlice  sona swa biscpas rihtes adumbia&d;  and sona swa hy eargia&t; and hy rihtes forscamia&d; and clumia&d; mid ceaflum  &t;onne hy scoldan clypian  sona heora wyr&d;mynt bi&d; waniende swi&d;e. 
 &D;onne is hit yfel so&d;  &t;eh &t;&ae;t ic secge: sume we synt gewunode  &t;&ae;t we syn to li&d;ie and to lofgeorne; and we willwyrda&d; mannum &ae;fter freondscipe and &t;urh &t;&ae;t ol&ae;ca&d; oftost on unnyt and so&t;es geswygia&d; ealles to swy&t;e. 
 And hit is egeslic gewuna  &t;&ae;t we eac habba&d;: sylfe we bysnia&d; oft and gelome  &t;&ae;t we geornost scoldan &ae;ghw&ae;r forbeodan  &t;&ae;t is woroldwlence and idele rence; and we oferdrucen lufia&d; to georne and mid &t;am huru &t;enca&d;  &t;&ae;t we us sylfe weor&d;ian wide  &t;e we o&d;re men drecan to swy&t;e. 
 Ac us eallum gebyrede  gyf we riht woldan  &t;&ae;t we mid wisdome &ae;g&t;er dr&ae;nctan ge ure agen andgyt ge &ae;lces &t;era  &t;e us to come  and na mid o&d;rum drince to oferlice swy&d;e  ac be mannes m&ae;&d;e mid fullan gescade. 
 La  yfele gebyrede biscopum &ae;fre  &t;&ae;t hy woroldmannum for idelum gylpe cwemdon on unriht ahwar to swy&d;e; eac hit is &t;e wyrse  &t;e hy unmannum ol&ae;ca&d; georne &ae;fter hereworde. 
 Wi&d; gligmen we ficia&d; and &t;am ure feoh gifa&d;  and wi&d; scandwyrde we ol&ae;ca&d; georne  willa&d; habban hereword and scyldan wi&d; unword; eal &t;&ae;t is idel and Gode ungecweme. 
 Ne gebyra&d; us &ae;fre  &t;&ae;t we on unriht awiht gestrynan  ne hit eac r&ae;d ne bi&d;  &t;&ae;t we rihtebegytan myrran on unnyt; ac us symle gebyre&d; &t;&ae;t swy&d;e rihte  &t;&ae;t we Godes &t;earfan geornlice gladian mid feo and mid fodan  &t;&ae;s &t;e we don magan. 
 Eac us bi&d; gerisenlic  &t;&ae;t we urum freondum luffeorme d&ae;lan  swa swa hit gerise. 
 And huru ne gerise&d; biscopum &ae;fre on &ae;nigum ealasele  &t;&ae;t hy to lange clyfian on b&ae;nce ne &ae;t ham ne on si&d;e  ac &ae;g&d;er gemedmigan. 
 Ac we syn gewunode  swa we na ne scoldan  &t;&ae;t we talia&d; to rihte  loc hw&ae;t us gelicigie  and fylga&d; urum luste ealles to swy&d;e. 
 And we hogia&d; eac swy&t;ost a ymbe &t;a &t;ing  &t;e we l&ae;st scoldan; smeaga&d; ymbe woroldcara and idele bisga and &t;ringa&d; &ae;fter &t;rymme and &ae;fter woroldwlence. 
 And we unriht gestreon eac lufia&d; to swy&t;e  sylla&d; wi&d; weor&d;e o&d;re hwile  &t;&ae;t we orcepe scoldan mid rihte. 
 Ac so&d; is  &t;&ae;t ic secge  gecnawe  se &d;e wille: a sy&d;&d;an biscpas awendan heora wisan of regollicre wisan eall to woroldwisan  sy&d;&d;an heora weor&d;scipe w&ae;s waniende swy&t;e; &d;onne is &t;&ae;re bote timlice micel &t;earf. 
 La  utan &t;&ae;t ge&t;encan oft and gelome and georne ure wisan gelogian mid ge&t;inc&t;an and understandan  &t;&ae;t so&d; is  &t;&ae;t n&ae;fre ne gerise&d; ge&t;ungenre ylde to geonclic wise ealles to swy&d;e; ne ealdan esne ne bi&d; buton tale  &t;&ae;t he hine sylfne wyrce to wencle on dollican d&ae;dan o&t;&t;on on geb&ae;ran.

 Ad Sacerdotes. 
 La leof  understanda&d; eow sylfe and lufia&d; and weor&d;ia&d; God &ae;lmihtigne ofer ealle o&d;re &t;inc and healda&d; his beboda georne. 
 And lufige eower &ae;lc o&t;erne rihtlice  and beo &ae;lc o&d;rum eage and eare and &ae;lc o&d;rum on helpe for Gode and for worulde. 
 Libba&d; heononfor&d; regollican life; seca&d; eowre cyrican and gefylla&d; eowre tida aa on gesetne timan. 
 L&ae;ra&d; cristen folc georne and lacnia&d; hit georne and ge&t;enca&d;  &t;&ae;t se l&ae;ce  &t;e sceall yfele wunda h&ae;lan  he mot habban gode sealfe to. 
 Ne syndon nane swa yfele wunda  swa syndon synnwunda. for&d;am &t;urh &t;a forwur&d; se mann ecan dea&t;e  butan he &t;urh andetnesse and &d;urh geswicennesse and &d;urh d&ae;dbote geh&ae;led weor&d;e. 
 &T;onne mot se l&ae;ce beon wis and w&ae;r  &t;e &t;a wunda geh&ae;lan sceall. 
 &D;urh gode lare man sceall &ae;rest hi lacnian and mid &t;am gedon  &t;&ae;t man aspiwe &t;&ae;t attor  &t;&ae;t him innan on by&d;  &t;&ae;t is  &t;&ae;t he gecl&ae;nsige hine sylfne &ae;rest mid andetnesse. 
 Eall man sceall aspiwan synna &t;urh gode lare  eallswa man unlibban de&d; &t;urh godne drenc; ne m&ae;g &ae;nig l&ae;ce well lacnian  &ae;r &t;&ae;t attor ute sy; ne &ae;nig man ne m&ae;g eac d&ae;dbote wel t&ae;can  &t;am &t;e andettan nele. 
 Ne &ae;nig man ne m&ae;g synna butan andetnesse wel gebetan  &t;e ma &t;e se m&ae;g wel hal weor&d;an  &t;e unlibban gedruncen h&ae;f&d;  butan he &t;&ae;t attor aspiwe. 
 &AE;fter andetnysse man m&ae;g mid d&ae;dbote Godes mildheortnesse hra&d;e geearnian  gyf he mid inweardre heortan heofa&d; and behreowsa&d;  &t;&ae;t he &t;urh deofles scyfe &ae;r gefremode to unrihte. 
 On wisum scrifte bi&d; swi&d;e for&d;gelang wislic d&ae;dbot  eallswa on godum l&ae;ce bi&d; &t;earflic broces bot. 
 Be mannes gewrihtum hit man mot secan &ae;fter canondome and eac medemian be mihtum and be m&ae;&t;e and be &t;am  &t;e man ongit his heortan hreowsunge and his sylfes geornfulnesse.

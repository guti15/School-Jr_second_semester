 Horalogium  Horarum breue inchoat en hic. On VIII kalend Ianuarii &t;&ae;t by&d; on cristes m&ae;sse d&ae;g by&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none seofon &amp; twentigo&t;an healfes fotes &amp; to midd&ae;ge feower &amp; twentig. 
 On VIII idus Ianuarii &t;&ae;t ys on &t;one twelftan d&ae;g by&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none XXV fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge XXII. 
 On XII kalend Februarii bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none an &amp; twentig fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge ehtatyne &amp; lytle mare. 
 On II non Februarii by&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none ehta teo&t;an healfes fotes &amp; to midd&ae;ge fiftyne. 
 On XII kalend Martii bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none fiftyne fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge twelfe. 
 On II non Martii bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none &t;reottyne fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge teo&t;an hielfes. 
 On XII kalend Aprilis &t;&ae;t is emnihte by&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none endlufon fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge nigo&t;an healfes. 
 On non Aprilis bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none teo&d;an healfes fotes lang &amp; to midd&ae;ge forneah seofun. 
 On XII kalend Maii by&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none ehta fota &amp; lytel eaca &amp; to midd&ae;ge forneah syx. 
 On II non Maii by&t; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none forneah ehta fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge fiftan healfes. 
 On XII kalend Iunii bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none seofon fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge feower. 
 On kalend Iunii bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none lytle lengre &t;onne seofon fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge feower. 
 On idus Iunii bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none ehto&d;an healfes fotes lang &amp; to midd&ae;ge feower. 
 On VIII kalend IVLI &t;&ae;t ys on Iohannes m&ae;sse d&ae;g bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none wel neh ehta fota &amp; to midd&ae;g IIII. 
 On II non IVLI bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none eahta fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge lytle mare &t;onne feower. 
 On XII kalend Agvsti bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none ehta fota &amp; lytle mare &amp; to midd&ae;ge fiftan healfes. 
 On VIII ID Agvsti bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none nigo&t;an healfes fotes lang &amp; to midd&ae;ge lytle mare &t;onne V. 
 On duodecima kalend Septembris bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none nigun fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge syx. 
 On non Septembris bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none endleftan healfes fotes lang &amp; to midd&ae;ge seofon. 
 On XII kalend Octobris &t;&ae;t ys emnihte bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none twelf fota lang &amp; to midd&ae;ge nigun. 
 On II non Octobris bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none feowertyne fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge endlufon. 
 On XII kalend Novembris bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none syxtyne fota lang &amp; lytle mare &amp; to midd&ae;ge XIII. 
 On non Novembris bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none nigontyne fota &amp; lytle mare &amp; to midd&ae;ge seofontyne. 
 On XII kalend Decembris bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none forneah feower &amp; XX fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge an &amp; twentig. 
 On IIII none Decembris bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to non syx &amp; XX fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge &t;reo &amp; twentig. 
 On XIX kalend Ianuarii bi&d; seo sceadu to underne &amp; to none seofon &amp; twenti fota &amp; to midd&ae;ge fif &amp; twentig forneah.
